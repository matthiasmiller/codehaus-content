# **Essential Visual Aids for "The Demilitarized Business"**

1. **Peak Performance Pyramid** \- A four-level pyramid showing:  
   * Want To (motivation): Base level  
   * Will Do (conation): Second level  
   * Can Do (cognition): Third level  
   * Inspired To (revelation/flow): Top level  
2. **Organizational Alignment Matrix** \- A simple grid showing how misalignment occurs between:  
   * Vertical axis: Executive → Management → Frontline  
   * Horizontal axis: Department vs Department (especially Sales vs Marketing)  
3. **The 80/20 Opportunity Framework** \- 2×2 matrix with:  
   * X-axis: Effort Required (Low to High)  
   * Y-axis: Impact Potential (Low to High)  
   * Clear highlighting of the High Impact/Low Effort quadrant  
4. **Head Trash to Business Strength** \- Simple two-column transformation showing:  
   * Left: Limiting patterns (need for uniqueness, people-pleasing, etc.)  
   * Right: Corresponding business strengths (innovation, customer focus, etc.)  
5. **Herrmann Whole Brain Model** \- Four-quadrant diagram showing:  
   * A Quadrant (upper left): Analytical, logical, fact-based, quantitative  
   * B Quadrant (lower left): Organized, sequential, planned, detailed  
   * C Quadrant (lower right): Interpersonal, feeling-based, kinesthetic, emotional  
   * D Quadrant (upper right): Holistic, intuitive, integrating, synthesizing  
   * With note about CEOs being 20X more likely to be multi-dominant across quadrants

These five core visuals cover your most important frameworks while keeping preparation manageable. Each represents an essential concept that benefits significantly from visual representation.
