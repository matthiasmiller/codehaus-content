# **Executive Focus Finder**

1. The purpose of this exercise is to identify your best area of focus, along with the area of greatest opportunity.

2. Make a list of all categories within your business. This will include departments, product lines, etc. If you have more categories than fit on one page, continue it on a second page.

3. Rank each of these items from 1-10 based on where your business is currently at.

   1. If you have trouble determining how to place these on a scale, find the WEAKEST item and mark it as a 1\. Find the STRONGEST item and mark it as a 10\. Then, rank the remaining items relative to the highest and lowest items.

   2. **Business Profitability** is the amount of net profit that is currently being generated by this category.

   3. **Business Scalability** is the ability of this part of the business to grow and scale.

   4. **Personal Interest** is how much joy, pleasure, and fulfillment you get from fulfilling your responsibilities in this part of the business.

   5. **Personal Ability** is how skilled you are in fulfilling the required responsibilities in this part of the business.

   6. **Score** is the MULTIPLIED value of each of the columns. This should be a number from 1 to 10,000.

4. This process can be repeated based on the business projections from 6 months, 1 year, 5 years, etc.
