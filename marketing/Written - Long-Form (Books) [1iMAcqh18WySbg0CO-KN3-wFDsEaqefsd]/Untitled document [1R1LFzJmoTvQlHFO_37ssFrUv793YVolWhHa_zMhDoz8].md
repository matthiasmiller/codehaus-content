Kip sat along the edge of his bed, face in his hands, weeping.

“What is the point of business if I don’t garner a profit?” Jack insisted. “I can’t imagine letting that go.”

“It’s not about ignoring your profitability. Though it may require holding less of a grasp on it,” the old man clarified. “What I’m talking about is doing the things *only you can do.*”
