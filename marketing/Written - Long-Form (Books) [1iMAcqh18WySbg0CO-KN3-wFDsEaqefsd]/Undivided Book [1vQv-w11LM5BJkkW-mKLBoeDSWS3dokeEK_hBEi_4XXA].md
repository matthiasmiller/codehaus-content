Part 2 \- The Business War Plan

* 360-Degree Discovery  
  * Activities: Comprehensive interviews with leaders & managers throughout the company, using the described framework   
  * Outcome: 360-degree report of the current situation

* 80/20 Opportunity  
  * Activities: Facilitated discussion with stakeholders, based on greatest opportunity with least effort  
  * Outcome: Singular focus with highest return on effort/resources 

* Strategic Roadmap  
  * Activities: Collaborate with stakeholders to development a roadmap, ensuring that all resources will be available  
  * Outcome: Clear path towards outcome, with buy-in from all necessary stakeholders, and clear communication with all required parties 

* Distraction-Free Execution  
  * Activities:  
    * Develop sprints for all respective parties, ensuring incremental wins and sustained momentum, opportunities for shared lessons & error correction  
    * Create and optimize any systems or processes needed to create sustainable change.  
    * Project manage to identify obstacles, shift priorities, and remain on course  
    * Communicate progress to stakeholders  
  * Outcome:  
    * 

* Autopilot & Autocorrect  
  * Activities:  
    * build cadences & processes for sustainable long-term success  
    * provide executive / leadership coaching to make sure that responsible parties are able to sustain the moment  
    * Do activities to encourage a culture of continuous improvement

* Review & Restart (or, Elevate and Iterate)  
  * Activities:  
    * Review what was achieved, the impact to the organization and all stakeholders  
    * Restart with the next iteration of improvement
