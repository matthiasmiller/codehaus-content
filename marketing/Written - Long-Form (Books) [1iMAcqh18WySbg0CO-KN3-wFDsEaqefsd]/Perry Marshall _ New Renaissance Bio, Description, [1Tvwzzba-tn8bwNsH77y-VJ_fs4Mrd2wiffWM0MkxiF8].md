# **The Demilitarized Business: How Ending Internal Wars Creates Unstoppable Momentum**

## **Bio**

Matthias Miller is the founder of CodeHaus, a company that helps business owners find and eliminate constraints within their business systems. He helps his clients improve their processes, then automate them through existing tools, AI, or fully custom software solutions. He has helped build these tools for clients ranging from startups and mid-size enterprises, as well as top banks and insurance companies in the US. He has co-architected full end-to-end ERP systems, helped run highly successful software projects, and has consulted with 9-figure businesses on their ERPs. His experience allows his clients to experience the ongoing benefits of profitable, predictable business growth.

## **Summary**

Matthias Miller, founder of CodeHaus, reveals how conflicts—both within organizations and within ourselves—cause most businesses to plateau. Drawing from his experience building systems for startups, mid-size enterprises, and top financial institutions, he shares how finding and eliminating constraints creates unstoppable momentum.

Most businesses operate at only 50-70% capacity due to fragmentation at multiple levels: departments battling each other, misaligned goals, and leaders struggling with their own internal tensions. Attendees will learn how to identify alignment opportunities at organizational, interpersonal, and personal levels.
