# The Lead Like a CEO Network

## Going from Owner/Operator to CEO Leadership

Dear business owner,

What if you could *do for yourself* what you so *easily* do for your team members and peers?

I’d bet that… **For those around you,** you have a remarkable ability to cut through the noise, advise, and encourage.

So why can’t you do that for yourself?

The answer is simple, and you already know it: Because we humans are wired this way. We are interdependent. We can often see for others but not for ourselves.

And so overthink. We overwork. We get stuck working on the wrong problems.

My intention for the Lead Like a CEO Network is to create a community of business owners who learn together, grow together, and encourage each other.
