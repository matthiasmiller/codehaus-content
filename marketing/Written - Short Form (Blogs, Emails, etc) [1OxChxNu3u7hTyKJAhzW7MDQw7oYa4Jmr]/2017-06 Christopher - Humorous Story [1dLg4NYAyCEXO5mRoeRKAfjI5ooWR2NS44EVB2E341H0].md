Once there was a brave hunter who had took many daring adventures. Naturally, he had many stories to tell of his unusually exciting (and sometimes hilarious) exploits. At first, he told his stories to only his siblings and parents, but then he got married and told his stories to his wife and children. Again, and again, and again. He spun his tales of risk late into the night so many times, his family grew weary of his yarns and groaned whenever he’d launch into one of his favorites.

Weeping and crying for fear his history might be forgotten, he suddenly made a discovery. He found, in his personal message delivery box, a peculiar orange leaflet. He flicked the pages open—reading the accounts of other great adventurers, their risk-taking strategies, and even a few stories that brought tears of joy to his face.

But only when he saw the letters in bold was his joy complete: ”**SHARE YOUR STORIES\!”** it read in pure white dye. Suddenly, he knew exactly what he had to do...

(Sorry. We got a little carried away with our imagination there. What we’re trying to say is… We know many of our readers have had some pretty wonderful experiences throughout their lives. Some of them challenging, some inspiring, some just downright funny. If that’s you, we would love to hear your stories and, with your permission, share some of them with our readers in an upcoming issue. Even if you don’t want it published, we’d love to hear them anyways\! You can send your stories, thoughts, inspirations, or anything at all to [stories@multiplyyourstrengths.com](mailto:stories@multiplyyourstrengths.com).)
