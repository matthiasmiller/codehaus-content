   2. Ecosystem  
      1. Each one feeds into the next one  
      2. Understand that each one is a spectrum  
      3. The stronger anyone is in any specific category, the more support that they’ll need. Very simple to understand:  
         1. Strong visionary needs more planning an execution  
         2. Strong manager needs bigger vision and more doers  
         3. Strong doer needs bigger/more projects and more checking  
         4. Strong checker needs more things to to validate/check  
      4. Blindspots often come two steps over  
      5. As your organization grows, your ecosystem needs to grow  
      6. This ecosystem is fractal as well, and you may find this pattern repeated within teams and organizations  
   3. Visionary  
      1. Output (Wants To) \- Ideas & Opportunities  
      2. Waste \- Chaos, Disorder, Unformed Plans (thus, wasted opportunities)  
      3. Blindspots  
         1. Manager \- Full scope of planning required  
         2. Doer \- Logistical issues  
         3. Checker \- Legal/compliance/technical problems  
      4. Roles  
         1. Director  
         2. Chair  
         3. Specific board members  
   4. Manager  
      1. Output \- Priority, Structure, Sequence, Plans  
      2. Waste \- Incomplete Projects  
      3. Blindspots  
         1. Visionary \- Major pivots (will see many opportunities)  
         2. Doer \- Specific steps/issues in the process  
         3. Checker \- Legal/compliance/technical problems  
      4. Roles  
         1. Director  
         2. Board member  
         3. Case manager  
         4. Program manager  
         5. Etc  
   5. Doer  
      1. Output \- Completed Projects, Consistency, Action  
      2. Waste \- Mistakes, Errors, Misdirected Actions  
      3. Roles  
         1. Volunteers  
         2. Program Staff  
         3. Case workers  
      4. Blindspots  
         1. Visionary \- opportunities, different approaches  
         2. Manager \- ways to optimize/streamline, seeing how different parts work together  
         3. Checker \- limited number of mistakes; details about legal/compliance/etc  
   6. Checker  
      1. Output \- Stability, Conformity. Accuracy  
      2. Waste \- Unsolved Problems, Bottlenecks, Obstacles  
      3. Roles:  
         1. Compliance  
         2. Legal  
         3. Policy  
         4. HR  
      4. Blindspots  
         1. Visionary \- alternatives to problems found  
         2. Manager \- priority of issues found  
         3. Doer \- Overanalysis; overthinking; etc
