We are FOR heart-centered leadership.  
We are AGAINST using numbers and systems to rule over people.

We are FOR results-oriented leadership and accountability.  
We are AGAINST using and abusing people to achieve those results.

We are FOR finding the most effective way to achieve those results.  
We are AGAINST achieving results merely by working longer, working harder, or working faster.

We are FOR finding the deepest bedrock of truth.  
We are AGAINST easy answers, simplistic solutions, and shallow tactics

We are FOR putting people into their places of giftedness, strength, and genius.  
We are AGAINST forcing people to fill roles they’re not made to fill.

We are FOR helping people tap into their greatest intrinsic motivations.  
We are AGAINST relying on extrinsic rewards, incentives, and punishments to move people into action.

We are FOR people who are aligned with their purpose at the deepest level.  
We are AGAINST the emotional pain, the physical suffering, and the mental anguish of those who are forced to show up as someone they’re not.

We are FOR transparency and honesty.  
We are AGAINST half-truths, deception, and illusion.

We are FOR vulnerability and acceptance.  
We are AGAINST shame.

We are FOR intentionally pursuing the most important things.  
We are AGAINST doing things just because they’re urgent, or because we’ve been asked or assigned.

We are FOR innovation and risk-taking.  
We are AGAINST blind leaps of faith.

We are FOR adapting to the world through healthy, positive change.  
We are AGAINST the stubborn resistance to do things the way they’ve always been done.

We are FOR taking rest and having fun, even amidst life’s demands.  
We are AGAINST the guilt that plagues us when we step away from our work.

We are FOR providing clear, honest feedback.  
We are AGAINST pretending everything’s okay when it’s not.

We are FOR embracing conflict, even when it’s challenging.  
We are AGAINST cutting people down when we disagree.

We are FOR taking action, even when we don’t see the whole picture.  
We are AGAINST over-analysis, over-planning, and over-thinking.

We are FOR community and connection.  
We are AGAINST isolation and loneliness.

We are FOR family and belonging.  
We are AGAINST orphan thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.

We are FOR lifting others to help them succeed.  
We are AGAINST leveraging people to gain an upper hand.

We are FOR openly sharing the stories of our successes.  
We are AGAINST embellishing reality to make us appear more effective than we are.

We are FOR leaving a legacy by giving a voice to our children and building a platform for them to succeed.  
We are AGAINST standing by complacent to the silent cries of the generation who will one day lead our nation and our world.
