Requirements of a Movement Maker

1. This is not something that you get talked into. This is something that you give your YES to.  
2. If you want to prosper and thrive leading a movement, this is for you.  
3. Are you Willing and Able to Go From Just Having a Message…**To Become the Messenger**  
4. You want to be a movement maker with POWER and AUTHORITY.  
   1. Power is the ability to get results. You must help people get the RESULT of that message.  
   2. Authority comes from personal victories. You want to be a messenger that has done battle. What personal battles have you won? What giants have I slain?  
   3. We may be at the high point of hypocrisy in our world.  
5. Leaders of movements usually have been great students of other leaders and mentors.  
   1. They’ve been learning, drawing, receiving revelation, knowledge, etc from multiple places.  
   2. “Success has many fathers.”  
   3. Where have you been a student?  
6. Are you willing to prepare in private? Private preparation leads to public celebration.  
7. Truth and Righteousness must be the only option for you.  
   1. When you take a bold and courageous stand  
   2. You want to have an unshakeable level of confidence and certainty.  
   3. You will need 0% doubt.  
   4. The way you get that, you need to commit and decide to stand in absolute TRUTH and RIGHTEOUSNESS….right standing with GOOD, with the highest and best.  
   5. I must pursue the highest and best.  
   6. You must sacrifice personal agenda.  
   7. You cannot be for sale.  
8. The most powerful person in the room is the person with nothing to hide.  
   1. This will probably require you to step outside of the car industry.  
   2. Movement makers are set apart. They are not going with the flow. They are saying, “No, that is wrong. There’s something wrong with that.”  
   3. Movement makers are exposing darkness and lie.  
   4. How can you expose a lie and not have a deep commitment to the truth?  
   5. When you start exposing lies and the flashlight turns on you, will you be ready for this?  
   6. Get yourself to a place of truth and what is right. What is the highest and best.  
9. Transparency  
   1. Most leaders are not willing to be this. This is why there are constantly scandals and expositions.  
   2. 40-minute careers end in a 4-minute video clip.  
   3. When you do this, your power is being thwarted.  
10. Radical Humility  
    1. Humility is a weapon.  
    2. Yield humility like a sword.  
    3. The most successful people are asking Pedro, how did you do it? In this, you’re the expert. Tell us what to do.  
    4. Humility will protect  you.  
11. Unoffendable and Quick to Forgive  
    1. If you want to be a movement maker, you must be unoffendable.  
    2. You must keep a short records of those who have wronged you.  
    3. Offense is a trap.  
    4. Do not entertain mockers.  
    5. Heart wounds will create blind spots in your mind.  
    6. The punches that you don’t see will knock you out. They’re hiding in your blind spots. Those blinds spots are from your heart wounds.  
12. Self-Love \-- Embrace/Exaggerate the Difference  
    1. “This guy is crushing it. I want to be like that guy.” Etc etc.  
    2. You have full permission to be yourself.  
    3. The leader serves the movement.  
13. Real Leadership over Positional Leadership  
    1. Just because you call yourself something, doesn’t mean that’s it.  
14. Other  
    1. You must be the best example for your tribe to have.  
    2. Live what you preach. Be the message.  
    3. You will probably have to be ok living with a measure of scrutiny.  
    4. You have to okay knowing that more people are looking at you.  
    5. You have a steadfast commitment to what you believe, and you must have non-negotiables.  
    6. You must be willing to make hard and tough decisions at times.  
    7. You must know you will never please everyone, and you are not perfect.  
       1. There will always be some unhappy people. That’s just life, and there’s nothing you can do about it.  
       2. You should try to live your life and your movement to the best possible way.  
       3. There are always “the miserables”. They want to stay miserable, and there’s nothing you can do.  
       4. You are here to be a leader, not a rescuer or savior.  
       5. Have grace for the miserable, leave them in the group, hope the coconut falls from the tree, hits them on the head, and they have a wakeup call.  
    8. Own your mistakes and repent quickly.  
       1. The probably means you’re doing something wrong, and you’re probably doing something right.  
       2. At the same time, you can learn from your haters.  
       3. Saying sorry is apologizing. Repentance is acknowledging that a mistake was made and doing something instead. Fix it and make it right.  
    9. Offer grace when someone else needs it and grace will be offered back when you need it.
