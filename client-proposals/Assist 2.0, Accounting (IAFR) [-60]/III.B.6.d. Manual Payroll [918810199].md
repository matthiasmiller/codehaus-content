3.2.6.4. Manual Payroll

  


Requirements

No special accommodations will be required for processing payroll.

  


Payroll expenses will be entered into the accounting system, with one entry per individual, per month. Each entry will be tied out to three expense categories:

  * Wages
  * State Taxes
  * Federal Taxes



  


In the short-term, payroll information will be handed off to Whitney, processed in QuickBooks, then entered back into the Accounting system.
