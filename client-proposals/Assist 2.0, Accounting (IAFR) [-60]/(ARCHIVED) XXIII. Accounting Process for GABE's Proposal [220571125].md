Building Built

  * Credit (increase) Production Labor Liabilities by the building's production labor costs
  * Credit (increase) Finished Goods Inventory - Production Labor Assets by the building's production labor costs
  * Credit (increase) Production Overhead Liabilities by the building's production overhead costs
  * Credit (increase) Finished Goods Inventory - Production Overhead Assets by the building's production overhead costs
  * Debit (decrease) Raw Materials by the building's costs
  * Credit (increase) FGI Materials by the building's costs
