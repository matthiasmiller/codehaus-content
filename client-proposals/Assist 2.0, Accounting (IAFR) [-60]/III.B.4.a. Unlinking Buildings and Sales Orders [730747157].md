3.2.4.1. Unlinking Buildings and Sales Orders

  


Requirements

Add a warning when unlinking buildings from sales orders: "Unlinking a building from a sales order may have an unexpected impact on accounting."

  


Add a warning when canceling a sales order with one or more payments, "Canceling a sales order with one or more payments may have an unexpected impact on accounting."
