3.2.1. Permissions

  


Requirements

Add three nested permission:

  * View FGI Costs (controls visibility on building record)
    * Edit FGI Costs (edit FGI Cost Estimates and FGI Actual Costs)
      * Configure FGI Costs (edit FGI Financial Allocation records that defaults to "Deny All")



  


All three permissions will be disabled by default.
