11\. Mobile Support

  


Requirements

The website will primarily be accessed via the equipment tag. Scanning it will show the information for the customer, with a login button at the bottom for employees to log in for additional information.

  


  * For the customer, show:
    * Lead text
    * Extended Description
    * Photos
    * Selling Price



  


  * For the person in the shop:
    * Pictures of all related paperwork (wash bay time sheet, inspection sheet, and repair sheet)
    * Ability to start / stop tracking time
    * Ability to enter stock numbers not yet in the system (i.e. consignment items)



  


  * For the salesperson, show:
    * A way to upload pictures of equipment
    * All equipment information, including costs.



  


NOTE: For shop staff who do not have their own smart phone, consider having smart phones available in the shop for them to can the QR code and enter, or having a kiosk that allows them to select their name, enter the stock number, and start/stop tracking time.
