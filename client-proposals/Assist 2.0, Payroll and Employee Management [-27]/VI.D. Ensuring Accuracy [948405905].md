6.4. Ensuring Accuracy

  


Requirements

Assist will include checks to ensure that all data is entered correctly:

  


  * Assist will warn when trying to enter information for a date after the building was built.



  


  * Assist will also warn when entering a time card for an employee without a base wage set.



  


Development Specification

NOTE: About 5000 buildings were completed this past month. At 20 days/month, this means that about 25 buildings need to be entered every day.
