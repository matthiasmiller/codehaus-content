4.1.1.5. Payroll Report Types and Users

  


Requirements

The company will specify users for the payroll reports:

Report Type| Action| User| Role  
---|---|---|---  
Required; Choice from Below| Required; Choice from Below| Required| User-configurable list  
  
  


The Report Type will be a choice of:

  * Owner Payroll Report
  * Employee Payroll Report



  


The Action will be a choice of:

  * Verify
  * Notify Only



  


The Role is an optional, informational field only.

  


NOTE: The Owner Pool % in Configure Company Types & Pools must be specified if an Owner Payroll Report is specified.
