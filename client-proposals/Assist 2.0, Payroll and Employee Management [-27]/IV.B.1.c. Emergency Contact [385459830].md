4.2.1.3. Emergency Contact

  


Requirements

The Employee record will include the following fields for the emergency contact:

  * Name
  * Phone Number
  * Relationship



  


Assist will not provide any automated way for companies to backfill or retrieve this information via fax. Burkholder Management will be responsible for doing this.

  


Development Specification

None are required

  


  * ContactEmergencyContactName-- simple text field
  * ContactEmergencyContactPhone -- phone field
  * ContactEmergencyContactRelationship -- simple text field
