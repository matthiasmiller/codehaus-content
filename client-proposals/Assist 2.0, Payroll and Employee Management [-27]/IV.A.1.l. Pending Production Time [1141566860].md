4.1.1.12. Pending Production Time

  


Requirements

For technical reasons, production time is stored on the Contact record and is automatically transferred to the Building record.

  


Most importantly, it also eliminates the possibility of edit collisions while entering payroll. Other users can change the building record without affecting production values.
