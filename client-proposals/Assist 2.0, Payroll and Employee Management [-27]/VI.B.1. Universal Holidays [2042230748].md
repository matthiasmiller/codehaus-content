6.2.1. Universal Holidays

The Universal Holidays report prompts for:

  * Start Date (defaulting to Jan 1 of the current year)
  * End Date (defaulting to Dec 31 of the current year)



  


The report shows the following columns:

  * Date (date when time off for holiday starts)
  * Through (date when time off for holiday ends)
  * Reason
  * Paid Time Off
