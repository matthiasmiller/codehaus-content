7.2.5. Individual Hourly Employee Summary

  


Requirements

The Production & Payroll Verification Report will include a page for each individual hourly employee.

  


It would include:

  * Employee Name
  * Week Number
  * Date Range
  * Vacation Period Start/End Date (if using vacation)
