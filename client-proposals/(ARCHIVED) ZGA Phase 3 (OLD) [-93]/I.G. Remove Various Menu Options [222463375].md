1.7. Remove Various Menu Options

  


Requirements

The following existing menu options and their corresponding reports are to be removed: 

  


  * Open Work Orders (opens a report of all Open Work Order incidents) 
    * Note: Replaced by the My Schedule menu option / report. 
  * Employee Shop Schedule (opens a report of all employees'   Weekly Shop Schedules from their contact records) 
    * Note: Not needed. 
  * Weekly Schedule Overview (opens the main Schedule report, filtered down to jobs projected to be finished in the next 7 days)
    * Note: Not needed.
