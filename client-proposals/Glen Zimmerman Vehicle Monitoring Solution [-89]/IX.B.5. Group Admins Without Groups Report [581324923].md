9.2.5. Group Admins Without Groups Report

  


Requirements

*Done. 

  


Overview: This is a standard report of active "Group Admin" Contacts that are not included on the "Group Admins" embedded spreadsheet for any Account Group records. It is used for the "Group Admins Without Groups" email notification to Master Administrators.

Administrators | Group Management | Group Admins Without Groups (opens the report directly, bypassing the filter screen)
