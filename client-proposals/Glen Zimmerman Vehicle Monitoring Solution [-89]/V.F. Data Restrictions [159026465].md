Data Restrictions:

  * For users with the "Full Access" Permission, allow full access to the entire database.
  * For Account Reseller, show all contacts (including drivers), accounts, and vehicles linked to the Reseller.
  * For Account Group Admins, show all contacts (including drivers), accounts, and vehicles linked to the Account Group.
  * For Account Managers, show all contacts (including drivers), accounts, and vehicles linked to the Account.
