16.3.3. Send "Group Admin Changes" Email

*Done. 

  


  * Name: "Send "Group Admin Changes" Email"
    * Description: Sends the "Group Admin Changes" email and clears the "Group Admin Changes Email Recipients" embedded spreadsheet. 
    * Prompts: 
      * Recipients (see spec of the "Select Recipients & Send Email" button on the Account Group record 
    * Initiated:
      * When the "Select Recipients & Send Email" button is clicked on the Account Group record.
    * Action(s): 
      * Takes the selection(s) in the "Recipients" prompt, and the "Group Admin Changes" email to the "Login Email" email address for the corresponding Contacts.
      * Deletes all rows from the "Group Admin Changes Email Recipients" embedded spreadsheet on the corresponding Account Group record.
