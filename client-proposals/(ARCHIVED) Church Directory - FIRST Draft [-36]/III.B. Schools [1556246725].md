3.2. Schools

  


Requirements

The school will include a School Name, Church #, and Active checkbox. Both fields are required.

  


Churches can have multiple schools. Two schools may have the same name, but be in different churches.

  


The New School link will prompt to save the current school, then open a new, blank school.

  


These will not be linked to households in any way.
