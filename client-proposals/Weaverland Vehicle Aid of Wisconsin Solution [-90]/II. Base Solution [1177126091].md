2\. Base Solution

This Silverloom Software Solution includes the following: 

  * Contacts module (member management) 
  * Vehicle module (vehicle & coverage management) 
  * Automation for annual renewals and invoicing 
  * Invoices module (invoicing fees & credits) 
  * Deposit / income documentation 
  * Claims module (incident / claim management) 
  * Many printouts related to members, vehicles, coverages, claims, and more
  * Opportunity for additional customization in the future, including the possibility of adding the Silverloom Accounting module
