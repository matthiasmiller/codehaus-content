16.10. QuickBooks Invoice Record

  


Requirements

Overview: This record is used to sync estimates and invoices with QuickBooks.

  


The record includes the following sections and fields:

 Type (Estimate, Invoice) 

  * Bill To 
  * Ship To 
  * Line Items 
    * Item Code
    * Qty
    * Description 
    * Price 
    * Tax
