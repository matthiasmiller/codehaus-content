17.5.1. Find Litter

  


Requirements

Clicking this menu option opens a prompt page with various filter options. Clicking "Continue" opens the the Litters report with the selected criteria. 

  


Prompts include: 

  * Search By (drop list of Mother's Serial, Litter DOB) 
  * Mother's Serial (drop list of serials for all female dogs; filters down as you type) 
  * Litter DOB
