17.2.3. Animals Sold Reports

Pigs Sold: Shows all pig sales for the selected criteria, with a total. 

  * Filters: 
    * From Date
    * To Date 
    * Location (drop list) 
    * Customer (multi-select drop list) 



  


Dogs Sold: Shows dog sales for the selected filter criteria, with a total. This probably could be combined with the Pigs Sold Report (maybe have two views - one simple/summary view and one detail view). 

  * Filters: 
    * From Date
    * To Date
