16.9. Incident Record

Overview: This likely will be the standard AppHosting Incidents module, with customizations. This is used to track Health Incidents and Service Incidents (negative things that happen after the customer has received the order), and would also be used for Sales Opportunities if/when that feature is added to the Solution. 

 

The record includes the following sections and fields:

  


Use the following standard fields:

  * Incident Type; choice of:
    * Animal Incident
  * Workflow; choice of:
    * Health Incident
    * Service Incident
  * Customer
  * Stage (formerly status)
  * Incident Notes



  


Use the following custom fields:

  * Incident Date
  * Issue
  * Resolution Notes
  * Animals (visible for Health Incidents; embedded spreadsheet:)
    * Serial ID
    * Type (automatic; Pig vs Dog)
