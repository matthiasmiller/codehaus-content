5.1.1. Contact Type Record

Overview: This is the standard AppHosting Contact Types feature, and is used to configure the various contact types that are use for contacts in the Solution. 

  


The Solution will use the following Contact Types to begin with (listed in alphabetical order): 

  * Customer
  * Driver
  * Employee
  * Principle Investigator (linked to Customer?)
  * Vendor (corresponds to the existing "Sources" list) 



  


Sections and fields: 

  * Contact Type 
  * Driver
    * Show in Dispatch (checkbox)
