3\. Settings

The Solution includes the following settings.

  


  


General Settings:

  * Last Rabies Tag ID # (number; editable; the tag IDs continue sequentially from this number)
  * Invoice Options: Various settings for defaulting fields on invoices (such as line item codes/descriptions/prices; contact information; default ship method; etc.)
  * The system should be able to convert between pounds and kilograms (both directions) for animal weights. Oak Hill works exclusively in pounds, but the majority of their customers work with kilograms.
