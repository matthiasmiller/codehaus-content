17.11. Incident Report

  


Requirements

The Solution will include standard AppHosting incident reports with customizations as needed, including menu options to view Health Incidents and Service Incidents, which would be reports of health and service incidents created within the selected date range, with a total number of incidents and subtotals for each category. 

  


Filters: 

  * From Date 
  * To Date
  * Incident Type
