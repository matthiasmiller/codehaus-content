15.1. Order Status

  


Requirements

The Solution will include the following list of order statuses: 

  * Canceled
  * Delayed
  * Delivered
  * Emailed to Allan (needed?)
  * Emailed to Char... (needed?)
  * HP Created
  * On Hold
  * Ordered
  * Pending
  * Scheduled
  * Shipped
  * TG Assigned



  


Other Notes: 

  * Note that an order may have multiple statuses at the same time.
