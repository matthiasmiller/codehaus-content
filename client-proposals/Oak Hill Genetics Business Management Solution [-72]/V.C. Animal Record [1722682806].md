5.3. Animal Record

Overview: This record is used to track animals. Some records will be specific to dogs and some to pigs. 

  


Accessed Via:

  * New Animal option on the main menu



  


The record includes the following sections and fields:

  


  * General Information section:
    * Serial (required; unique ID; manually assigned)
    * Type (required; drop list of "Canine" and "Porcine")
