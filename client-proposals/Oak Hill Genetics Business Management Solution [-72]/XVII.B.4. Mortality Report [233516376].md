17.2.4. Mortality Report

Report (probably of Litter records) that shows details about the litter, with totals. This also shows details about the father and/or mother. 

  * Columns: 
    * Date 
    * # Weaned 
    * # Live Born 
    * Various reasons of death 
    * Mortality %
  * Grouped by parent 
  * Filters: 
    * From Date
    * To Date
