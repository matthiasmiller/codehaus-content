4\. SPE Member ACH Portal

  


Requirements

This SPE Member Application Portal enables:

  * Viewing and editing contact information.
  * Requesting changes to authorized users. (addition or removal)
  * Viewing all applications for the business, including the total contribution amount and school designations.
  * Editing the total contribution amount for unpaid applications except for year 2 of 2 applications.
  * Editing school designations for unpaid applications.
  * Submitting payment for member applications (using a secure handoff link)



  


Note: Navigating away from a page without saving the changes will alert the user.
