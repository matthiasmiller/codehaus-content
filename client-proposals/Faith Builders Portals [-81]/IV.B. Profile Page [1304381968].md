4.2. Profile Page

Contact Information:

  * Business Name (read-only; visible for Business logins)
  * First Name (read-only; visible for Individual logins)
  * Last Name (read-only; visible for Individual logins)
  * EIN (only visible for Business logins; read-only, last four shown)
  * SSN (only visible for Individual logins; read-only, last four shown)
  * Address Line 1 (required)
  * Address Line 2
  * City (required)
  * State (required)
  * Zip (required)
  * County (required)
  * Primary Phone (read-only)
    * Displays the primary-type phone number from the associated Business or Individual Contact record.
  * Primary Email (read-only)
    * Displays the primary-type email address from the associated Business or Individual Contact record.
  * Anonymous Donor



  


Login Access:

  * Users with login access:
    * Displays all email addresses currently able to log in on behalf of the business or individual.
  * Request changes to authorized users (mailto link; opens a draft email to the SPE Setting Email from Silverloom Settings)
