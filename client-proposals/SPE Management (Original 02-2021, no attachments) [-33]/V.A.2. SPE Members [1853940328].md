5.1.2. SPE Members

  


Requirements

This report will show SPE members.

  


It will prompt for:

  * SPE (required; list of SPEs)
  * SPE Application Status (blank = all; list of Approved, Pending, Denied)
  * SPE Member Status (blank = all; multiple choice of Admitted, Waiting for Payment, Pending)
  * Tax Year (required; number field)
  * Group by Approval (checkbox; default to checked)



  


If the "Group by Approval" checkbox is checked, the report will group by SPE Application. Otherwise it will be ungrouped.

  


Its columns will include:

  * Member Name (link to the Contact detail)
  * Address
  * County
  * City
  * State
  * Zip
  * Total Contribution
