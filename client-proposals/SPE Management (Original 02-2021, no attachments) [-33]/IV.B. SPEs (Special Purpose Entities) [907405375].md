4.2. SPEs (Special Purpose Entities)

  


Requirements

Each SPE will be tracked separately in AppHosting. It will track:

  * Name (i.e. "SPE Original", "SPE II", etc)
  * Legal Name
  * Legal Address
  * Legal City
  * Legal State
  * Legal Zip
  * Legal County
  * EIN



  


It will include the following report links:

  * View SPE Applications
  * View SPE Members
