7.5.1. Receipt of Member Application

  


Requirements

Type: Email

Email subject: Receipt of SPE Application

Send to: Members

Trigger: Application

Reminder: None

Description:

Note:

  * The "for Business Entity" should be replaced with the business name if the applicant is a business entity.
  * The 2021 should be the application tax year + 1.
  * The 2020 should be the application tax year.



Custom Values: 

  * Total application amount
  * Insert “for [business name]” if this is a business application rather than individual
  * School(s) and amount(s) [100% amount, total of all schools equal total application amount]
  * Anonymous [insert not if Anonymous is No]
  * Check for active (unfilled) state approval of SPE Application and adjust wording
  * References to Tax Year and Tax Year + 1
  * Check for Check Status of Held and add associated paragraph



  


  


Template:

  


Applicant,

  


This email is to communicate receipt of your $________ application [for Business Entity] to become a member of a Faith Builders Special Purpose Entity (FB SPE).  Thank you for your interest in supporting Christian schools by applying for membership!  

  


We have your requested school designation(s) as follows:

  * School 1, $Amount
  * School 2, $Amount



The school will receive 95% of the amount.  We have recorded your preference [not] to remain anonymous to the school(s).  Please let us know promptly of any errors you see.

  


When no active state approvals are being filled:

We currently have no slots for admittance.  We will hold your application and be in touch when we receive our next approval allowing admittance.  This application can remain pending for a 2021 tax credit if no 2020 tax credit becomes available.

  


When there is an active state approval being filled:

We will be in touch once we make final decisions on admittance to a FB SPE for the current state approval.

  


If check is Included, use this next paragraph:

We will not deposit your check unless you are admitted for a 2020 tax year credit. We will communicate about shredding the check by the middle of January if you are not admitted for a 2020 tax year credit by then. Upon admittance for a 2021 tax credit, we will contact you for a new check.
