7.5.11. Receipt of Payment

  


Requirements

Type: Email

Email subject: Receipt of SPE Payment

Send to: All member applications based on a check received date

Trigger: Payment

Reminder: Starting day after if the email wasn't sent

Custom Values:

  * Check Amount
  * Check for Accepted by SPE status and insert appropriate language
  * Scheduled to Deposit date
  * School(s), 100% amount(s)
  * Anonymous Donor (insert “not” if No)



  


Template:

  


Member,

  


We acknowledge receipt of your check in the amount of $__________

Being Deposited:

and will be depositing it to the SPE bank account on __________.

  


Being Held Until Admitted:

, and we will hold your check until decisions concerning admittance to a Faith Builders SPE have been made.  

  


We have your requested school designation(s) for this payment as follows:

  * School 1, $Amount
  * School 2, $Amount



The school will receive 95% of the amount.  We have recorded your preference [not] to remain anonymous to the school(s).  Please let us know promptly of any errors you see.
