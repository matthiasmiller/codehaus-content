6.1. RTO Contract Word Merge

  


Requirements

Refer to the existing contract, with the following changes.

  


[ ] Switch to the Arial font.

  * Logo (consider using thumbnail to force max size)

Footer:

  * RTO Company Name (instead of header)
  * Page X of Y

Content:

  * Evaluate the selected RTO Contract Template (from sales order) and embed.
