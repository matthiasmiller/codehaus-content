1\. Overview

  


Requirements

Bartlett Co-op is a multi-location agricultural cooperative located in southeast Kansas requiring software to manage maintenance of their equipment. The software will support tracking equipment, viewing maintenance records on equipment, and scheduling one-time or recurring maintenance and deadlines. The purpose of the software is to streamline operations, ensure adequate equipment maintenance, and to facilitate compliance with regulations. 

  


Goals:

  * Easy for employees to use.
  * On-line searchable access to maintenance records.



  


CodeCrafters International Ltd is an American-owned software company serving small and medium organizations throughout the U.S. as well as internationally. CodeCrafters provides custom management solutions for businesses and other organizations through the AppHosting.zone platform.

 

Ellis Miller, Managing Director, has over 20 years of experience in software development and over 15 years of experience in project management and team leadership. He brings his skills in software design together with extensive experience in business and project management. Joining him are Joel Iwashige, a Chartered Financial Analyst with over 15 years of software development experience, and a team of a dozen software developers and engineers.

  


CodeCrafters provides customized web-based business applications using the AppHosting.zone application framework. These are offered as software as a service (SaaS) on the AppHosting.zone platform. CodeCrafters will build and deploy a web database application for Bartlett Co-op to manage their equipment maintenance. The systems will be accessible from any internet-enabled computer or tablet. Users will log in using a Google account to verify their identity.
