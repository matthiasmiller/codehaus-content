9.1. Cut List Schedules

The Cut List reports include links to the following schedules:

  * Sawing Schedule
  * Brewer Schedule
  * Keystone Schedule
  * Scalpy Hollow Schedule



  


These include items for:

  * Pcs
  * Size
  * Misc. Prep
  * Item
  * Customer
  * Date



  


The Sawing Schedule is grouped by:

  * Saw
  * Raw Material Info (Sawing)



  


The other schedules are grouped by:

  * Raw Material Info (Gangsaw & Scrag)
