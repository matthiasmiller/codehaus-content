8.6. Cut List History

Overview: This is an editable report of orders and cut list materials.

  


Accessed from: Home | Cut Lists | Cut List History

  


Title: Cut List

  


Columns: 

  * Sales Order Number
  * Include in Cut List (checkbox; editable)
  * Cut List Date (editable)
  * Item Code
  * Qty
  * Due Date
  * Total Pieces (editable)
  * Single Item Pieces
  * Dimensions
  * Raw Material Info (Gangsaw & Scrag) (editable)
  * Raw Material Info (Sawing) (editable)
  * Misc. Preparation (editable)
  * Brewer (editable)
  * Keystone (editable)
  * Scalpy Hollow (editable)
  * Sawing 1 (editable)
  * Sawing 2 (editable)
  * Sawing 3 (editable)
  * Sawing 4 (editable)
  * Sawing 5 (editable)



  


Grouped by:

  * Scheduled for [DATE]
  * Unscheduled



  


Sorted by: 

  * Due Date + Sales Order Number (reverse)



  


Filters: 

  * Cut List Date



  


Buttons: 

  * Include Selected in Cut List
  * Sawing Schedule
  * Brewer Schedule
  * Keystone Schedule
  * Scalpy Hollow Schedule



  


Menu Visibility: 

  * Always



  


Other Notes: N/A
