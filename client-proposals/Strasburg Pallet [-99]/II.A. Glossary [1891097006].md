2.1. Glossary

Overview: The purpose of this glossary is to provide a description of key terms, roles, and other things referred to in this proposal. These are listed in alphabetical order below:Â 

  


  * Brewer, Keystone, Scalpy Hollow: Sawing locations



  


  * Cans: Pieces of lumber that sawmills cannot use for high-grade purposes like furniture, veneer, or railroad ties. These are typically byproduct lumber used for pallet making.



  


  * Cut List: A detailed list of lumber pieces needed to create pallets, including dimensions and quantities.



  


  * Gang Saws: Large industrial saws that can cut multiple pieces of lumber simultaneously.



  


  * Sage: An accounting and business management software used for tracking orders, inventory, and financial information.
