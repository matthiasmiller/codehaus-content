6.5. Order Records

Overview: This is used to track orders.

  


Accessed via: Home | Orders | Orders

  


Sections and Fields: 

  


  * General section:
    * Silverloom ID (autonumber; hidden & read-only)
    * Sales Order Number (number; 0 decimals)
    * Order Date (date)
    * Due Date (date)
    * Customer Name (drop list of Contacts)
    * Items; embedded spreadsheet of:
      * Item (drop list of Items)
      * Qty Ordered (number; 0 decimals)
      * Unit Price (number; 2 decimals; read-only)
      * Qty Sold Last 12 Mo (number; 0 decimals; read-only)
      * Times Sold Last 12 Mo (number; 0 decimals; read-only)
      * SageRowID (number; 0 decimals; hidden & read-only)
  * Cut List section:
    * Populate Cut List (checkbox)
    * Cut List; embedded spreadsheet of:
      * ItemCode (drop list of Items; hidden & read-only)
      * ItemLumberRGID (number; 0 decimals; hidden & read-only)
      * Total Pieces (number; 0 decimals)
      * Single Item Qty (number; 0 decimals)
      * Dimensions (text; read-only)
      * Raw Material INFO (Gangsaw & Scrag) (text)
      * Raw Material INFO (Sawing) (text)
      * Misc. Preparation (text)
      * Brewer (checkbox)
      * Keystone (checkbox)
      * Scalpy Hollow (checkbox)
      * Sawing 1 (drop list of SawingEquipment)
      * Sawing 2 (drop list of SawingEquipment)
      * Sawing 3 (drop list of SawingEquipment)
      * Sawing 4 (drop list of SawingEquipment)
      * Sawing 5 (drop list of SawingEquipment)
      * Item Qty (number; 0 decimals; hidden & read-only)
    * Cut List Date (date)



  


Modification History: This contains the following standard features for modification tracking: 

  * Created: (name, date, and time stamp) 
  * Last Modified: (name, date, and time stamp)
  * Modification History (link to open the report for the record)
