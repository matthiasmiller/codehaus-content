6.2.6. High-Risk Drivers

Overview: This is a custom report of high-risk drivers.

  


Accessed from: Admin | Exception Reports | High-Risk Drivers

  


Title: High-Risk Drivers

  


Columns: 

  * Driver Name (displays the driver name)
  * Client Name (displays the client name, link to record)
  * Owner Name (displays the owner name)
  * # Paid HRD Fees (displays the number of high-risk driver fees for that driver with a status of paid)
  * # Unpaid HRD Fees (displays the number of high-risk driver fees for that driver with any status other than paid)



  


Grouped by: Current High-Risk Drivers/Past High-Risk Drivers

  


Sorted by: # Unpaid HRD Fees + # Paid HRD Fees + earliest effective fee date

  


Filters: 

  * Client or Vehicle Owner Name (drop-list of clients and vehicle owners, defaults to blank)
  * Driver Name (drop-list of drivers for the client or vehicle owner selected above, defaults to blank)
  * Include Past High-Risk Drivers (checkbox, defaults to checked)
  * Include Canceled Fees (checkbox, defaults to checked)



  


Buttons: N/A

  


Menu Visibility: Admin
