1.2. CodeCrafters Overview

*Done. 

  


CodeCrafters delivers tailored software solutions for growing businesses and non-profits. Since its founding in 2007, the company has expanded to a team of more than 30 people, with offices in the US and Bangladesh. 

  


The US-based team works closely with clients to provide system design and ongoing support, ensuring a strong customer focus. Software development is a collaborative effort between both the US and Bangladesh teams. 

  


Leveraging their extensive experience in enterprise software, CodeCrafters works with clients as a committed business partner, offering design, development, and long-term support to meet the specific needs of each client.
