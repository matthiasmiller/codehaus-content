6.5.2. Invoices for Contact Report

*Done. 

  


Overview: This is a standard report of Invoice records for a single Contact.

  


Accessed from: the "View All Invoices" link on Contact records

  


Title: Invoices for <ContactName>

  


Preface: N/A

  


Columns: 

  * Invoice # (link to open the Invoice record) 
  * Date (displays the "Invoice Date") 
  * Status
  * Total Amount (total rows show sum)



  


Grouped by: 

  * First by: Contact 
  * Second by: Status ("Draft" / "Invoiced" / "Paid" / "Canceled")



  


Sorted by: Date (newest at the top)

  


Filters: 

  * Contact (drop list of Contacts; defaults to the Contact where the report was accessed; blank = none) 
  * Show canceled invoices (checkbox; defaults to checked) 



  


Buttons: 

  * New Invoice (opens a new Draft invoice record, with "Invoice Date" set to the current date and Contact set to the selection on the Contact filter from the report)



  


Data Access: All

  


Other Notes: 

  * N/A
