9.11. Claim General Release Printout

  


Requirements

*Done. 

  


Purpose/Overview: The General Release Agreement custom printout is used at the conclusion of a Claim to confirm the completion of the Claim. The body for the General Release is generated from the user-configured "Claim General Release Printout" text field in Silverloom Settings. 

  


Printed From: 

  * Claim record: "General Release PDF" link
  * Claim record: "General Release Word Document" link



  


File Format/Name: 

  * PDF: "General Release - <Claim ID> (exported on <current date in the yyyy-mm-dd format>)"
  * Word Document: "General Release - <Claim ID> (exported on <current date in the yyyy-mm-dd format>)"



  


Fields to be Filled (PDF & Word Document are the same): 

  


  * Header: 
    * Logo (displays the Plan-specific "Plan Logo" from Silverloom Settings; dimensions: 500 x 108 points (max of 1.5" high, up to 6" wide)) 
    * "<displays the "General Release: Optional text below logo" System Switch> 
    * "<Plan Address Line 1 from Silverloom Settings> <Plan City from Silverloom Settings>, <Plan state abbreviation" System Switch> <Plan Zip from Silverloom Settings>" 
    * "<Plan Phone from Silverloom Settings>" 



  


  * The printout body is drawn from the "Claim General Release Printout" text field in Silverloom Settings.



  


Handling Page Breaks: N/A (this printout always fits on a single page)

  


Other Notes:

  * This printout does not include the standard "Powered by Silverloom Business Suite" footer.
