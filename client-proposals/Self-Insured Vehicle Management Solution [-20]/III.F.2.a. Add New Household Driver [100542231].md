3.6.2.1. Add New Household Driver

*Done. 

  


  * Name: "Add New Household Driver"
    * Description: Runs from a Vehicle record to add a new Household Driver to the list on the Owner's Contact record. 
    * Prompts: 
      * First Name (required; plain text) 
      * Middle Name (plain text) 
      * Last Name (required; plain text) 
      * Date of Birth (date) 
      * Notes (multi-line plain text) 
    * Initiated:
      * When the "Run Import" button is clicked on the "New Household Driver" child screen on the Vehicle.
    * Action(s): 
      * Takes the information entered into the prompts on the "New Household Driver" child screen, and adds a new row to the "Household Drivers" embedded spreadsheet on the Contact record of the Vehicle's "Owner".
