6.2.7. Print Annual Invoices & Insurance Cards

*Done. 

  


Overview: This is a special custom report that runs on Contact records and is used to batch-print Invoices and Insurance Cards for all clients of each Agent, by generating PDF documents of the Invoices and Insurance Cards.

  


Accessed from: Admin | Renewals and Printing | Print Annual Invoices & Insurance Cards (opens a prompt screen with the following message: "Print for Period Start Date: July 01, <current calendar year>" and a "Continue" button) 

  


Title: "Print Annual Invoices & Insurance Cards"

  


Preface: N/A

  


Columns: 

  * Agent
  * Link to Print Invoices (link; displays as "Link for Client Invoices"; generates and opens a PDF document of the "Statement (Unpaid Invoices)" printouts for all of the Agent's clients, with separate pages for each client) 
  * Link to Print Cards (link; displays as "Link for Client Insurance Cards"; generates and opens a PDF document of the front of the "Insurance Card (Front & Back)" printout for all of the Agent's clients, with separate pages for each client)



  


Row-Specific Buttons: N/A

  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: N/A

  * N/A



  


Menu Visibility: Admin (by virtue of being on the Admin menu)
