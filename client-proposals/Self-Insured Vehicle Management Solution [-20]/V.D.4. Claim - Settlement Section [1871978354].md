5.4.4. Claim - Settlement Section

*Done. 

  


  * Settlement section:
    * Settlement Amounts (label)
    * Liability 1 (optional; number; 2 decimals; must be greater than zero)
    * Include in Release (checkbox; applies to "Liability 1"; if checked, that amount is included in the "Total Release" amount below)
    * Liability 2 (optional; number; 2 decimals; must be greater than zero)
    * Include in Release (checkbox; applies to "Liability 2"; if checked, that amount is included in the "Total Release" amount below)
    * Towing / Storage / Other (optional; number; 2 decimals; must be greater than zero)
    * Include in Release (checkbox; applies to "Towing / Storage / Other"; if checked, that amount is included in the "Total Release" amount below)
    * Pain & Suffering (optional; number; 2 decimals; must be greater than zero)
    * Include in Release (checkbox; applies to "Pain & Suffering"; if checked, that amount is included in the "Total Release" amount below)
    * Medical (optional; number; 2 decimals; must be greater than zero)
    * <Alternate text for Collision - Long label> (number; 2 decimals; must be greater than zero)
    * Total Release (read-only macro; number; 2 decimals; displays the sum of the amount fields above for which "Include in Release" is checked) 
    * Total Settlement (read-only macro; number; 2 decimals; displays the sum of all six amount fields above) 
    * Settlement Details (optional; memo)
