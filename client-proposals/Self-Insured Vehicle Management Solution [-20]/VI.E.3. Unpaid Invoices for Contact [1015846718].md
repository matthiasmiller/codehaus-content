6.5.3. Unpaid Invoices for Contact

*Done. 

  


Overview: This is a custom report of unpaid Invoice records for a specific Contact.

  


Accessed from: "View Unpaid Invoices" link on Contact records

  


Title: "Unpaid Invoices for <Contact Name>"

  


Preface: N/A

  


Columns: 

  * Invoice Date 
  * Invoice Number (link to open the Invoice record) 
  * Amount



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: Invoice Date (oldest at the top)

  


Filters: 

  * Contact (drop list of Contacts; defaults to the Contact where the report was accessed) 



  


Buttons: N/A

  


Data Access: All

  


Other Notes: N/A
