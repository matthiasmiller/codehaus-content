2.2. Menus

  


Requirements

*Done. 

  


Reports and features are accessed via the Silverloom Business Suite menu.

  


The Solution includes the following menu tabs, with the listed menu options in addition to other options specced in their corresponding record / report specs (any menu options noted here are ones that are not specced out in other sections of this spec): 

  


  * Home Menu (customized; visible to all users; some menu options can be disabled or hidden based on permissions)
    * Up to 10 customized menu options, configured in System Switches and pulling from Wiki pages (see corresponding details in the proposal) 
    * Print Vehicle Guidelines Sheet (opens the Vehicle Guidelines Wiki page as a PDF, per the System Switch) 



  


  * Admin Menu (custom; visible to Admin users)



  


  * Advanced Menu (standard; visible to all users; some menu options can be disabled or hidden based on permissions)
