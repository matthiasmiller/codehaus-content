9.6. Insurance Card (Back Only) Printout

  


Requirements

Purpose/Overview: This is an additional custom printout template for times when a user wants to print out only the back portion of the insurance card. This is a custom printout that is the same as the "Back of card" portion of the "Insurance Card (Front & Back)" printout, with all 3 sections filled in.

  


Printed From (opens the PDF in the browser, with the option to download or to print): 

  * Home | Resource | Insurance Card (Back) menu option (prints only the back for one card, for users who with to avoid duplex printing) 



  


File Format/Name: 

  * PDF: "Insurance Card (Back Only)"



  


Fields to be Filled: Same as the "Back of card" portion of the "Insurance Card (Front & Back)" printout (see corresponding spec) 

  


Handling Page Breaks: N/A (always only a single page) 

  


Other Notes:

  * This printout does not include the standard"Powered by Silverloom Business Suite" footer.
