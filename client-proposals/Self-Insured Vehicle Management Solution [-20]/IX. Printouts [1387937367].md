9\. Printouts

*Done. 

  


The Solution can generate various printouts based on templates. Unless otherwise noted, all printouts contain the standard "Powered by Silverloom Business Suite" footer. 

  


These templates/printouts can fall into two main categories: 

  


Basic printouts can be generated from the Advanced menu of a report. Note that the PDF versions of such printouts are branded with the Plan logo and contact information but do not have any additional special customizations. Basic Excel files do not include the logo/branding.

  


Customized printouts are provided for various reports and/or records. CodeCrafters will take some liberty with the design of these printouts to simplify development and to compensate for technical limitations. Customized PDFs include an Plan letterhead, and customized Excel printout/files can include the logo.
