11.1. Customers Report

  


Requirements

The Customers report is a report of all customers and a summary of their contact information. 

  


There will be a "New Customer" button at the top of the report to create a new customer contact. 

  


Columns: 

  * Customer (link to Customer/Ads Page)
  * Primary Contact
  * Address Line 1 (defaults to the Customer's Billing address type; if there is no Billing address, display their next available address - same as invoicing)
  * City
  * State
  * ZIP
  * Email
  * Phone 1
  * Phone 2
  * Fax
  * Linked Contact Names
  * Linked Contact Email
  * Linked Contact Phone 1
  * Linked Contact Phone 2
  * Linked Contact Fax
  * Publication (Full Admin only)



  


Filters:

  * Customer (filters down as you type)
  * Primary Contact (text field; search by partial)
  * Email (text field)
  * Phone/Fax (text field; searches all phone types)
  * Active Customers Only (checkbox; defaults to checked)
  * Current Customers Only (checkbox; defaults to unchecked; visible if "Active" is checked; Current = ones with ads scheduled for the current publication date)
  * Publication (only for Full Admins; list of publications; default to blank = all)



  


Grouping/Sorting: 

  * Active/Inactive (if Inactive is selected)
  * Sort by Customer



  


To export a copy of this report, simply select the desired filters, then export it via the choices in the Advanced option at the top of the page.

  


Development Specification

This is a custom report, not the standard Contacts Report.
