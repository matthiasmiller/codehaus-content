3.5. Sales Report / Graph

  


Requirements

Include a daily CRM report that shows:

  * Number of opportunities for the period
  * Number and $ value of open opportunities
  * Number of Won, Lost, Abandoned opportunities for the period
  * Pie graph of:
    * Count, $, and $% value of leads by status (Not Yet Quoted, Quoted, Won, Abandoned)
  * Tasks (list of follow-ups, contact name, due date, and assignee)



  


This will be interfaced on the menu for sales managers.
