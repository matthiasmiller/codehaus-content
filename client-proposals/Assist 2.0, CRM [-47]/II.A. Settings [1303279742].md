2.1. Settings

  


Requirements

The CRM will have the following global settings for the entire CRM:

  


  * Round Robin Turnaround for Assigned Salesperson (i.e. 4 hours)
  * Round Robin Turnaround for Alternate Salesperson (i.e. 15 minutes)
  * Round Robin Turnaround for Alternate Sales Lot (i.e. 15 minutes)
  * Days Until Abandoned (default to 90)



  


Development Specification

System switches are fine. You can wait to add them until you need them. They can be defined in minutes (as numbers)
