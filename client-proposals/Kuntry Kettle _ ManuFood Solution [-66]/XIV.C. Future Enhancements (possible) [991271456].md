14.3. Future Enhancements (possible)

The following items are listed as possible future features, which could be considered and designed separately:  

  


  * AppHosting Accounting module and Invoicing module (to replace QB), and complete integration with other system elements (OR, integration with QuickBooks if that makes more sense)



  


  * Phone/email interface to access reports



  


  * Scanner / portable device / terminal (inventory tracking and work order entry)



  


  * Small Private Labels (i.e. packaging a batch of apple butter, but using multiple labels for it)



  


  * Truck Inspections (currently hand-stamped on the packing list, but could be digitized). The truck inspections currently use a stamp that includes the following: 



  


SHIPPING & RECEIVING INSPECTION

  


Clean and fit to load/unload?  Y / N

No holes in floor, roof or sides?  Y / N

No pets or strong offensive odors?  Y / N

Dry, with no dripping & condensation?  Y / N

  


Date: __________ Seal No: ____________

Loaded/Unloaded by: _________________

Material conforms to specifications?  Y / N  Initial _____

  


  * Alternative access to ManuFood: 
    * Data entry person
    * Self-hosted
    * Tablet
    * Business workstation
    * QR Code that opens a table to enter just a little bit of information
    * Phone call w/ speech recognition
