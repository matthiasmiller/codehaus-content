17.4. Stock Record

  


Requirements

Purpose/Overview: 

  


File Format: 

  


Information to be included: 

The stock record will show:

  * Item Number
  * Unit Size
  * Vendor(s) -  \- do we need to put Vendors on the SKU?
  * Item No(s) -  \- where does this come from?



  


It will include a section for reorders (i.e. purchase orders): 

  * Date
  * Vendor
  * Qty
  * Price -  \- do they use this?



  


It will include a section for stock:

  * Date
  * Customer/Vendor
  * Received
  * Used/Sold
  * Balance



  


The stock entries will be grouped first by actual stock adjustments (orders received or manufactured), and secondarily by pending sales orders.
