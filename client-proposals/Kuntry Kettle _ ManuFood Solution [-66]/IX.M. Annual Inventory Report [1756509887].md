9.13. Annual Inventory Report

The Annual Inventory Report will be a 2-pane report. It will prompt for:

  * Year (defaulting to current year)



  


The left pane will include columns for:

  * SKU
  * Description
  * Billing Price (editable field)



  


The right pane will show every time the selected SKU was purchased during the prompted year. It will show the average price (weighted by quantity) as well as the average price (unweighted). It will include columns for:

  * Supplier
  * Quantity
  * Price
