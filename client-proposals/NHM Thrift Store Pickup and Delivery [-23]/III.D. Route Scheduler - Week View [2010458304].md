3.4. Route Scheduler - Week View

This will add two menu options on Route Scheduler:

  * By Weekday (prior behavior)
  * By Week (new behavior)



  


The By Week will show additional options for:

  * This Week (always visible)
  * Next Week (always visible)
  * Week of Sep 6 (visible if one or more stops have been scheduled)
  * Week of Sep 13 (visible if one or more stops have been scheduled)
  * Etc



  


The By Week view will have a link at the top of each day for "View Weekday". It will toggle the view to that weekday. The By Weekday view will have a link at the top of each day for "View Week". It will toggle the view to that week.

  


When selecting a specific week, it will show all available weekdays for that week (excluding holidays and other days not in the schedule).
