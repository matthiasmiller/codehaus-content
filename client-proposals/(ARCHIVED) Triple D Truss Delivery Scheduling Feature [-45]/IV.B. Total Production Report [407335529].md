4.2. Total Production Report

This will be accessed from a menu option that will open as a report in the AppHosting software.

  


Prompts:

  * Status, multiple choice of:
    * In Progress 
    * Made
    * Delivered
  * Start Date (blank = all)
  * End Date (blank = all)



  


Columns:

  * Customer 
  * Job Name
  * Delivery Date
  * Made (checkbox)
  * Made Date 
  * Item
  * Board Feet
  * Will Call (checkbox) 



  


Group by:

  * Status 



  


Sort by:

  * Delivery Date then Customer (Will Call should be at the bottom since it has no delivery date)
