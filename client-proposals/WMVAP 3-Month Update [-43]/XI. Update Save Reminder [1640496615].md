11\. Update Save Reminder

  


Requirements

The "Save" button on a record begins blinking after the page has been open for a while. The default length of time is 5 minutes, but this could be shortened if desired.

  


Option 1: Change the time amount to 1 minute

  


T&M Estimate: Approximately $50

  


  


Option 2: Change the time amount to an amount less than a minute (could be set to as little as 1 second).

  


T&M Estimate: $300-500
