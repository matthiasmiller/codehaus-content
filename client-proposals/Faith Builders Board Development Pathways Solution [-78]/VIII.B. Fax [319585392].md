8.2. Fax

There are multiple options for fax integration. Details to be determined in the IDD. 

  


  * Direct integration with an api: Development & maintenance costs (at least $25/month) + $0.007/page. 
  * Fax.plus with email bridge (solution sends email to Fax.plus, then Fax.plus passes it on as a fax):  $7/month for 200 pages.
