6.5.7. Participant Wrap-up

  


Requirements

This is the communication that gets sent to all the Participants on a board when the board completes a Pathway.

  


It contains things such as:

  * Congratulations for completing the Pathway
  * Thanks for participating
  * Additional Pathway options if they want to continue with another one
