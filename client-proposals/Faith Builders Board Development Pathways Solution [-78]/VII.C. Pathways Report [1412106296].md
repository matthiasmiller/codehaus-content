7.3. Pathways Report

This is a report of all Pathways that FB offers, with various filters. 

  


This is accessed from (TBD in IDD). 

  


Title: Pathways

  


Columns: 

  * Pathway Title (link to open record) 
  * Pathway Description 
  * Tags
  * Number of Lessons
  * Price per Person
  * Times Purchased
  * Times Completed
  * # of Participants



  


Grouped by: Active / Inactive

  


Sorted by: Pathway Title (alphabetical)

  


Filters: 

  * Pathway Title (drop list of all Pathways; filters down as you type)
  * Tags (drop list of all Tags; filters down as you type)
  * Number of Lessons (number with no decimals)
  * Times Purchased (number with no decimals)
  * Times Completed (number with no decimals)



  


Buttons: 

  * New Pathway (opens blank new Pathway record)
