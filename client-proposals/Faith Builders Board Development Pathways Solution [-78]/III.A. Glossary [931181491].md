3.1. Glossary

Overview: The purpose of this glossary is to provide a description of key terms, roles, and other things referred to in this proposal. These are listed in alphabetical order below:Â 

  


  * Board Representative - A person associated with the Organization who is in charge of managing the subscription for the Organization.
  * Lesson - The piece of the Pathway that gets sent out on a monthly basis.
  * Organization - Usually the board of a nonprofit organization. Will most likely be expanding to school boards, etc.
  * Participant - Any board member participating in a Pathway.
