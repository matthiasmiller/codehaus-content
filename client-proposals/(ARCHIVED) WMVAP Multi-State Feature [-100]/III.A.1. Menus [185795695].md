The Solution includes the following menu tabs: 

  


  * Home (visible to all users; some menu options can be disabled or hidden based on permissions)
    * Contacts section:
      * Contact Search 
      * My Clients
      * All Clients
      * All Contacts
      * New Contact
    * Vehicles section:
      * Vehicle Search
      * All Vehicles
      * No-Charge Vehicles
      * Coverage Deactivation Dates
      * Missing License Plates
    * Claims section:
      * My Client Claims
      * New Claim
    * Invoices section:
      * View Invoices
      * New Invoice
      * Aging Reports
      * Receive Payments / Prepare Deposit
      * Deposit Breakdown
    * Alerts section:
      * View User Alerts
    * Resources section:
      * View Handbook
      * Print Insurance Card (Back)
      * Allowed Vehicles
      * Truck Weight Chart
      * Vehicle Guideline Sheet
      * Wiki
      * Up to 10 customized menu options, configured in System Switches and pulling from Wiki pages (see corresponding details in the proposal)

      * New User Alert
    * Exception Reports section:

      * Missing License Plates

      * Dismissed Credits
