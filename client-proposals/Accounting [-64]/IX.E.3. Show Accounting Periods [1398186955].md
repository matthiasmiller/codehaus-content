9.5.3. Show Accounting Periods

Overview:

[X] All closing accounting periods and locking transaction dates are specified on the "Accounting Periods" record.

[X] "Show Accounting Periods" opens the record with the help of an autopush report.

[X] User needs "View accounting data" permission to see the record.

  


Menu link:
