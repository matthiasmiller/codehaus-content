6.7.2. Agreements Needing Renewal Report Alert

  


Requirements

Overview/Purpose: This alert occurs on the 1st of the month to remind Sales Reps of Managed Service Agreements that are due for renewal in the next two months. Note that this only applies to Agreements that have one or more printers (Agreements with only non-printer Devices do not need to go through this process).

  


Details: 

  * Title (can include an expression): Agreements Needing Renewal
  * Displays within 20 minutes of: The Agreements Needing Renewal background process
  * Action: Opens the Agreements Needing Renewal Report, filtered to the corresponding Agreement Sales Rep
  * User(s) to notify: The user of the Agreement Sales Rep linked to the Agreement needing renewal 
  * Dismiss type: Auto-dismiss when all items are resolved
