10.3. Buyer Invoice Email

  


Requirements

Overview: This email merge is used to send the Buyer Invoice after the Delivery Ticket has been entered into the Solution. This is sent from the Delivery Ticket record, when a user clicks the "Send Buyer Invoice Email" link.

  


From: The email address specified in SMTP settings

  


To:

  * Any primary email address(es) for the Buyer for the Delivery Ticket



  


CC: 

  * Any primary email address(es) for the Salesperson for the Delivery Ticket



  


BCC:

  * N/A



  


Reply To: Same as the "From" email address

  


Subject: "Invoice <Invoice #> from GemWood"

  


Attachments: 

  * Buyer Invoice PDF generated by the Solution for the Delivery Ticket record 
  * Delivery Ticket printout PDF generated by the Solution for the Delivery Ticket record 
  * Note that the email body (see the corresponding spec) also includes links for the following:
    * Original PO from the Buyer (i.e. any/all files from the "Purchase Order PDF" on the linked Purchase Order record)



  


Body: 

<Text from the "Default Text for Buyer Invoice Email" field in Silverloom Settings>

<Text from the "Add'l Notes for Buyer Invoice Email" memo on the Delivery Ticket record, if any>

  


Other Notes: 

  * The GemWood logo and signature line are hard-coded at the bottom of the email merge.
