1.3. Design & Development Phases

Multiple phases are anticipated for the LoadHawk Solution:

  * Phase 1: 
    * Base System 
    * Order & Delivery Management 
    * Sales Commission 
    * Internal Fee Payouts 
    * Data Migration & Solution Setup 
    * Related Printouts
  * Phase 2: 
    * Phase 2a: In-Field Tablet for Delivery Tickets (with tablet access)
    * Phase 2b: Sawmill Log Inventory (database & tablet) 
  * Potential future phases / features: See the "Additional Features to Consider" section near the bottom of this proposal.
