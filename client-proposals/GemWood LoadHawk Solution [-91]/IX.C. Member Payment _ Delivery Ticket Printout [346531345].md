9.3. Member Payment / Delivery Ticket Printout

  


Requirements

Purpose/Overview: This is a combined printout with multiple functions. It contains a "Member Payment" portion and a "Delivery Ticket" portion. Both portions are printed together for the Member Payment Printout, and the "Delivery Ticket" portion is printed by itself for the Delivery Ticket Printout. 

  


Printed From: 

  * Combined printout:
    * Included in the "Member Payment Issued" email
    * "Print Member Payment Printout" link on the Member Payment detail screen
  * Member Payment printout only:
    * N/A
  * Delivery Ticket printout only:
    * "Print Delivery Ticket Printout" link on the Delivery Ticket record
    * Included in the "Salesperson Invoice Review" email
    * Included in the "Buyer Invoice" email
    * Included in the "Delivery Ticket Closed" email



  


File Format/Name:

  * Combined Member Payment printout:
    * "Member Payment <Payment ID> (generated on <current date in yyyy-mm-dd format>.pdf"
  * Member Payment printout only:
    * N/A (not generated by itself in Phase 1) 
  * Delivery Ticket printout only:
    * "Delivery Ticket <Delivery Ticket #> (generated on <current date in yyyy-mm-dd format>.pdf"
