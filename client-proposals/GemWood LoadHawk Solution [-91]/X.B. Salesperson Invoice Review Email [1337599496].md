10.2. Salesperson Invoice Review Email

  


Requirements

Overview: This email merge is used to send the Buyer Invoice to the Salesperson after the Delivery Ticket has been entered into the Solution and before it is sent to the Buyer. This is sent via the "Send Salesperson Invoice Review Email" link on the Delivery Ticket record. 

  


From: The email address specified in SMTP settings 

  


To:

  * Any primary email address(es) for the Salesperson for the Delivery Ticket



  


CC: N/A

  


BCC: N/A

  


Reply To: Same as the "From" email address

  


Subject: "Review & Approve Invoice <Invoice #> for <Buyer Name>"

  


Attachments: 

  * Buyer Invoice PDF generated by the Solution for the Delivery Ticket record 
  * Delivery Ticket printout PDF generated by the Solution for the Delivery Ticket record 
  * Note that the email body (see the corresponding spec) also includes links for the following:
    * Original Delivery Tickets from the Member (i.e. any/all files in the "Delivery Ticket Files" on the Delivery Ticket)
    * Original PO from the Buyer (i.e. any/all files from the "Purchase Order PDF" on the linked Purchase Order record)



  


Body: 

<Text from the "Default Text for Salesperson Invoice Review Email" field in Silverloom Settings>

<Text from the "Add'l Notes for Review Email" memo on the Delivery Ticket record, if any>

  


Other Notes: 

  * The GemWood logo and signature line are hard-coded at the bottom of the email merge.
