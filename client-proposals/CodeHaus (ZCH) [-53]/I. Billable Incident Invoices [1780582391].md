1\. Billable Incident Invoices

  * Go to Accounting | Billing Reports | Billable Incident Invoices.
  * Enter the correct info (Example: Billing Contact:Ellis Miller, Billing Organization:Codecrafters, Intl., Sales Item:Sales), then Continue.
  * After the report runs, click on "New Invoice" beside one of the line items.Â 
  * An invoice will generate with all the line items. Double check to make sure the total is correct (check against Billable Incidents report).
  * Return to the Billable Incidents Invoices report and click "Record Invoice" in top right corner. Select the date that you will send the invoice. Continue.
  * Check to make sure all the incidents were recorded as invoiced (make sure the invoiced total is now in the "Total Unpaid" column, and that the "Total Uninvoiced" column is now zero.)
  * "Save" the invoice and download it as a PDF.
  * After the invoice is paid, go back to Accounting|Billing Reports|Billable Incident Invoices, fill in pertinent info, and click "Record Payment" in upper right corner. Enter invoice date and paid date.
