6.1.2. Packs

Overview: This is a custom totals-only report of packs.

  


Accessed from: Main Menu | Mobile App | Packs

  


Title: Available Packs

  


Columns: 

  * Type
  * Packs (count of packs in grouping)
  * BF Qty (sum of BF Qty for all packs in grouping)



  


Grouped by:

  * Open/Closed (if Include Closed is checked)
  * Member
  * Thickness + Species + Grade



  


Sorted by: N/A

  


Filters: 

  * Include Closed (checkbox)



  


Buttons: 

  * New Pack
