13.1. Printer setup instructions

Configuring the printer:

  * Turn the printer on. Wait until it shows Ready.
  * Press “OK”. Click right arrow until it says “Setup”.
  * Press right arrow until it says “Wi-fi Direct”. Press OK.
  * It will probably say “Off”. Press right arrow so that it says “On”. Press OK.
  * It will say “Turning Wi-fi Direct on…”
  * One Wi-fi Direct is on, press the right arrow until it says "Wifi Direct Name". Press OK and make note of the name.
  * Then press the right arrow until it says "Wifi Direct Password". Press OK and make note of the password.



  


To print:

  * Open your device's Wifi settings and select the printer.
  * Enter the Wifi Direct password.
  * Load the Delivery Ticket.
  * Press menu, then “Print”.
  * A dialog will come up. Select the printer. It may say that it is not available right now. Go to the printer. You should see a prompt saying to press “OK” to connect. Do so. The phone will now say that it is ready to print.
