3.1.2. Grouping 2: Ready to Load (DTs)

Grouping 2: Ready to Load

This grouping contains all open member delivery tickets that have not been marked as loaded.

  


The member delivery ticket screen displays buyer PO details, the Gemwood PO #, general shipping information, and itemization.

  


Delivery Ticket screen:

  * Header:
    * Buyer PO Description
    * Buyer PO #
    * Gemwood Load #
  * Ship to information
  * Terms, Conditions, and Specifications of Sale
  * Itemization list (one card for each species + thickness + grade)
    * Species
    * Thickness
    * Grade
    * Remaining Required BFT
    * List of Packs
      * Pack #
      * Thickness
      * Grade
      * BFT
      * Button to remove pack
    * Add new (button that adds new packs)
      * Prompts for:
        * Species (uneditable)
        * Thickness (editable drop-list)
        * Grade (editable drop-list)
        * Pack # (uneditable)
        * BFT (number; uneditable)
      * Cancel (button)
      * Add Pack (button)
    * Total BFT for each card
  * Total BFT (sum of BFT for all entered packs)
  * Total Packs
  * Ticket # (number of linked delivery ticket)
  * Ship to information
