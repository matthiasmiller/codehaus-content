1.6.2.4. My Schedule Report

  


Requirements

This is the Master Schedule Report, filtered to include all incidents assigned to the current logged-in user. 

  


This is accessed from: Good's Ag Repair | Scheduling | My Schedule 

  


The following filters are to be set: 

  * Location Category: blank = all  
  * Assignee: Current logged-in user
