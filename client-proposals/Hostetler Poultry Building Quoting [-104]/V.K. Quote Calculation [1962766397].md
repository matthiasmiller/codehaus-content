5.11. Quote Calculation

  


Requirements

Quote Calculations are used to easily calculate certain values, such as truss counts.

  


Fields:

  * Name (string)
  * Expression (quote-level expression; numeric)



  


Examples:

  * Number of Building Trusses - calculated based on Length and Centers
  * Number of Manure Storage Trusses - calculated based on Length and Centers
  * etc
