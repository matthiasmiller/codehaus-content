7.1. New: GemWood Purchase Order Email

Overview: This email merge is used to send the GemWood Purchase Order to a Member. This is sent from the "Send Email" link on the GemWood Purchase Orders embedded spreadsheet and the "Send Email" column on the GemWood Purchase Orders report.

  


From: The email address specified in SMTP settings

  


To:

  * Any primary email address(es) for the Member for the GemWood Purchase Order



  


CC: 

  * The primary email address for the Salesperson selected on the Purchase Order record



  


BCC:

  * N/A



  


Reply To: Same as the "From" email address

  


Subject: GemWood Purchase Order #<GemWood PO #>

  


Attachments: 

  * GemWood Purchase Order printout PDF generated by the Solution for the GemWood-type Purchase Order record



  


Body: 

<Text from the "Configure GemWood Purchase Order Email" field in Silverloom Settings>

  


Other Notes: 

  * The GemWood logo and signature line are hard-coded at the bottom of the email merge.
