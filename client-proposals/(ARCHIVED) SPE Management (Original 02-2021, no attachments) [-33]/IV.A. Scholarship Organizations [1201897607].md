4.1. Scholarship Organizations

  


Requirements

Most of the contributions will go through FBSS, the Faith Builders Scholarship Organization. However, some of the members want to make contributions to an individual school that has its own scholarship organization.

  


These will be Contact records with a specific "Scholarship Organization" contact type. This will allow easy tracking of the contact information for the organization. These records will require a Primary Contact to be specified.

  


Development Specification

Append to the detail screen in outer catalog and add a Detail Record Validation:

Scholarship Organization records must have a Primary Contact specified.
