7.5.10. Payments Due

  


Requirements

Type: Email

Email subject: SPE Payment Due Soon

Send to: Members (All members accepted by SPE, with completed application, but w/o full payment)

Trigger:

  * Manually, or
  * Via Reminder



Reminder:

  * 5 business days before payment due date



Confirmation: Read-only Payment Reminder Date on Member Application

Custom Values:

  * Application Amount
  * Payment Due Date
  * Remaining Payment Amount (if partial payment has been made)
