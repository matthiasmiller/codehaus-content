3.4. Employee Hours Summary Report

This is a report of a summary of all Employee Hours.

  


This is accessed from Good's Ag | Time Tracking | Employee Hours Summary. 

  


Title: Employee Hours Summary

  


Columns: 

  * Employee
  * Regular Hours
  * Overtime Hours
  * PTO Hours
  * Billable Hours



  


Grouped by: Work Week or Employee (determined by the filter option)

  


Sorted by: Employee (alphabetical)

  


Filters: 

  * Starting Week (drop list of all weeks when time was tracked)
  * Ending Week (drop list of all weeks when time was tracked)
  * Group By (drop list of Work Week and Employee; defaults to Work Week)



  


Buttons: 

  * N/A



  


Menu Visibility: 

  * All Users



  


Other Notes:

  * N/A
