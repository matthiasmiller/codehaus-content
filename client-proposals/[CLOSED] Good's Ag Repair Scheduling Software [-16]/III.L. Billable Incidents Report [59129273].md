3.12. Billable Incidents Report

This is a two pane report of Billable Incidents.

  


This is accessed via the View Hours link in the Billing Details child screen. 

  


Title: Billable Time for Incident

  


Right Pane:

Columns: 

  * Employee



  


Left Pane:

Columns:

  * Incident Title (indented to indicate job hierarchy)
  * This Job Hours 
  * This Job Amount
  * Total Estimated Hours
  * Combined Hours (this job plus child jobs)
  * Combined Amount (this job plus child jobs)
  * Combined Estimate (this job plus child jobs)



  


Grouped by: Employee on the Left Pane

  


Sorted by: Incident Title on the Right Pane (alphabetical)

  


Filters: 

  * Incident ID (drop list of all Incidents)
