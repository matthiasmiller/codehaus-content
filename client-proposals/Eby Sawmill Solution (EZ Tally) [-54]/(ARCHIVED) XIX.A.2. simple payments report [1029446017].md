This is a report of all Payment records in the Solution, with various filters.

  


This would be accessed from an All Payments menu option in the Payments section of the Financial menu. 

  


Title: Payments

  


Columns: 

  * Payment ID (link to record)
  * Payment Type
  * Tract
  * Payee
  * Flat Payment Type
  * Flat Payment Due Date
  * Payment Total
  * Check #
  * Check Amount
  * Notes
  * Paid (Yes/No)
  * Payment Date
