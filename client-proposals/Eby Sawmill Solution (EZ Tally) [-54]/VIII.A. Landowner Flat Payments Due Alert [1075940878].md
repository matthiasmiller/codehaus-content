8.1. Landowner Flat Payments Due Alert

  


Requirements

Overview/Purpose: This is an internal reminder in the Solution for Flat Rate Date-based Payments for Landowners that are coming due (not needed for Harvest-based flat payments). This should be triggered a week before the payment Due Date. Note that this is the same as the Landowner Flat Payments Due Email (redundancy seems helpful for this reminder).

  


Title: Landowner Flat Payments Due 

  


Sent via: A report alert that runs one week before the Payment Due Date

  


Action: Link opens the Landowner Flat Payments Due Report

  


User(s) to notify: All Administrators

  


Other Notes:

  * The alert should persist as long as there are due unpaid Payments.
