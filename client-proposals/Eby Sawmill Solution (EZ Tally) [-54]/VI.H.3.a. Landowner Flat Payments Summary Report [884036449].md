6.8.3.1. Landowner Flat Payments Summary Report

  


Requirements

Overview: This is the Master Payments report, with the following filters set:

  * Payment Status = "All Paid"
  * Payment Type = "Landowner Flat"



  


Accessed from: Financial | Summaries | Landowner Flat Payments Summaries 

  


Title: Landowner Flat Payments Summary

  


Other Notes:

  * Note that the right pane is blank for Landowner Flat payments, as there is no itemization for Landowner Flat payments.
  * Note that there also are no buttons for this report, as summaries are not printed/sent for flat payment-based tracts.
