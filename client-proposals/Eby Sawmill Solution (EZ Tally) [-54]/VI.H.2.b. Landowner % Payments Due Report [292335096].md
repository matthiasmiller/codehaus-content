6.8.2.2. Landowner % Payments Due Report

  


Requirements

Overview: This is the Master Payments report, with the following filters set:

  * Payment Status = "Unpaid + Paid Today"
  * Payment Type = "Landowner %"



  


Accessed from: Financial | Settlements | Landowner % Payments Due

  


Title: Landowner % Payments Due

  


Other Notes:

  * Accounts normally are settled monthly for %-based Tracts/Landowners (but sometimes more frequently).
