6.8.2.1. Landowner Flat Payments Due Report

  


Requirements

Overview: This is the Master Payments report, with the following filters set:

  * Payment Status = "Unpaid + Paid Today"
  * Payment Type = "Landowner Flat"



  


Accessed from: Financial | Settlements | Landowner Flat Payments Due

  


Title: Landowner Flat Payments Due

  


Other Notes:

  * Accounts are settled for flat payment-based Tracts/Landowners according to the Due Date or the Forester.
  * Note that the right pane is blank for Landowner Flat payments, as there is no itemization for Landowner Flat payments.
