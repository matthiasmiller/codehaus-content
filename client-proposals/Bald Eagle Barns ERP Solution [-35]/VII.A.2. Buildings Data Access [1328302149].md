7.1.2. Buildings Data Access

Data Access: (applies to all Building records and all buildings reports; note that all editing permissions are limited with some status-related restrictions on editing, etc.)

  * Admins, Drivers, Office Workers, Member Salespeople can view and edit all
  * Shop Workers can view and edit all buildings with active Work Orders of "Manufacturing" and "Modification" type.
  * Non-member Salesperson can only view and edit inventory for buildings on their dealer's lot(s) and buildings that have been ordered/sold by their current dealer. This means that if a Salesperson changes dealers, they will not be able see buildings that they sold while with their previous dealer.
