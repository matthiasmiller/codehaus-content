19.8. Member Labor Report

This is a report of payment due to member contacts for labor, sales commission, driver deliveries, task completion, and salesperson bonuses.

  


This would be accessed from a "Member Labor Report" option on the Financial Menu. 

  


Title: Member Labor Report

  


Columns: 

  * Member Name 
  * Member Initials 
  * Work Week (link to Time Sheet record) 
  * Worked hours (with totals) 
  * PTO Hours (with totals) 
  * Overtime Hours (with totals) 
  * Worked Days (with totals) 
  * PTO Days (with totals) 
  * Commission $ (with totals) 
  * Delivery $ (with totals) 
  * Tasks $ (with totals) 
  * Salesperson Bonus $ (with totals) (others would have a manual bonus entered in QB) 
  * Total Amount (with totals) 
  * 


  


Grouped by: Pay Period, then by Member Name 

  


Sorted by: Member Name 

  


Filters: 

  * 


  


Buttons: 

  * Sync to QB
