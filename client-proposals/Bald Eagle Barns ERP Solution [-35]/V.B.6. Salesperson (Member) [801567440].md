5.2.6. Salesperson (Member)

  


Requirements

The detail screen for a Salesperson (Member) contact will have the following custom section and fields: 

Salesperson (Member) Details section: 

  * Dealer (required; defaulted to the BEB Dealer contact & read-only; field is cleared when Contact Type is changed)
  * Sales Report (link to the Sales by Salesperson Report, filtered to the current Salesperson)



  


Other Notes:

  * Member Salespeople can approve Sales Orders for Non-member Salespeople, and their own Sales Orders are automatically approved.
  * Member Salespeople can also view and edit all Sales Orders, Work Orders, etc.
  * The Salesperson contact would always be an Individual.
