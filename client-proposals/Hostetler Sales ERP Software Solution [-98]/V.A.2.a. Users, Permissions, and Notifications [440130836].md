5.1.2.1. Users, Permissions, and Notifications

  


Requirements

Permissions

  * Can modify ordered sales orders
  * Can modify picked sales orders
  * Can modify shipped sales orders



  


Notifications

  * Notify Superintendent and Salesperson on sales order changes



  


User Group / User

  * Used for real-time notifications
    * Notify for Changes on Ordered Sales Orders
    * Notify for Changes on Picked Sales Orders
    * Notify for Changes on Shipped Sales Orders
    * Notify for Backorders on Sales Orders
