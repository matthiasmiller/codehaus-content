5.1.4.1. Special Items

The Excel files can specify a special item SKU that Purchasing has to fix once it turns into a sales order. This could be a predefined SKU such as "unknown.purchasingreview".

  


Reports & Notifications:

  * The sales manager could be assigned to review estimates with this SKU.
  * Inside sales will review the special item estimates (fixing lines with this SKU and reviewing lead times, etc)
