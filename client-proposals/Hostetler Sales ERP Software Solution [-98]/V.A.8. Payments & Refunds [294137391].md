5.1.8. Payments & Refunds

  


Requirements

Payment Methods:

  * Name
  * Active (checkbox)
  * Available for Payments (checkbox)
  * Available for Refunds (checkbox)



  


Writeoff Reasons:

  * Name
  * Active (checkbox)
  * Available for Underpayment
  * Available for Overpayment



  


Sales Transactions:

  * Customer
  * Date
  * Type (Payment / Refund / Underpayment Writeoff / Overpayment Writeoff)
  * Amount (auto-calculated)
  * Method (for Payments/Refunds)
  * Writeoff Reason (for Writeoffs)
  * Sales Orders (for customer deposits or underpayments)
    * Sales Order #
    * Amount
  * Invoices (for invoices or overpayments)
    * Invoice #
    * Amount
  * Memo/Notes



  


Reports:

  * Sales Transactions



  


Development Specification

  * "Sales Form Transaction"
  * Needs to be an optional feature for Sales Forms & update payment status appropriately
