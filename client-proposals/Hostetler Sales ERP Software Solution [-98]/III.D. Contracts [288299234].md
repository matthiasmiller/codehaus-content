3.4. Contracts

  


Requirements

The estimate is turned into a contract, which is reviewed and signed. This is typically signed at 30 weeks. At this point, the superintendent reviews the project.

  


Non-poultry builders who have their sales force will do this on their own. The contract will be sent to inside sales for review. This also pushes the equipment sales order.

  


The contract will include and exclude sections based on the estimate.
