5\. Upgrades

You will be required to upgrade your software every 18 months. Around the time of the upgrade, you will start receiving notices that your software is about to expire.

  


We are constantly upgrading our software. By upgrading regularly, we can ensure that we can continue making enhancements to your software without a lot of hassle.

  


At the time of the upgrade, we will upload a copy of your database for our archival purposes.
