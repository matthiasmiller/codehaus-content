10.1.1. Job Event Screen

  


Requirements

This is the digital job packet - everything the crew needs, including job details, address, a link to Google Maps, photos, documents, and PA 1 confirmation - accessible on a tablet instead of a printed clipboard packet.

  


  * Job Name 
  * Dates
  * Location (Job Address)
  * Link to Google Maps
  * Attachments
  * View/Edit Job in ProEstimator
