4.1.6. (New) Shop Time Record

  


Requirements

This provides categories for tracking time that isn't tied to a customer job but still needs to hit payroll, like shop work or other non-billable activities.

  


Sections and fields: 

  * Name (required; plain text; validate against duplicates) 
  * Active (checkbox)
