This defines your Prep and Paving work crews (including their calendar color) so jobs can be assigned to the right team with minimal setup.

  


Sections and fields: 

  * Name (automatic & read-only; i.e. "Paving Crew #1")
  * Crew Type (drop list of "Crew Types" list items; required)
  * Active (checkbox)
  * Crew # (required; number; 0 decimals; must be unique by Crew Type")
  * Calendar Color (drop list of colors; this sets the color used on the scheduling calendar)



  


  * Example Crews:
    * Prep Crew 1
    * Prep Crew 2
    * Paving Crew 1
    * Paving Crew 2
    * Paving Crew 3
