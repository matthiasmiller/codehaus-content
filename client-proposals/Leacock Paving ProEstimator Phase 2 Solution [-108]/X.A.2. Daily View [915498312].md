10.1.2. Daily View

  


Requirements

This is the morning view displayed in the shop, showing every crew, their assigned jobs, crew members, trucks, trailers, and which plant they're getting material from. This replaces the Excel dispatch sheet you're managing now.

  


It includes a section for each crew and details for the following:

  * Job(s)
  * Asphalt plant (if applicable)
  * Crew members
  * Resources (trucks, trailers, equipment)
  * Hired haulers



  


It also includes a section for employees who are off.
