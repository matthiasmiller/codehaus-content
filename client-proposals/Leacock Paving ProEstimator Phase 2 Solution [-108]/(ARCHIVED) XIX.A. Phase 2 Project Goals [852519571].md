19.1. Phase 2 Project Goals

  


Requirements

The main goals of Phase 2 are as follows: 

  


  * Job scheduling 
    * Linking with Proposals 
    * Job packets 
    * Reminders to confirm with customers
    * Daily / weekly / monthly schedule views 
  * PA 1 Call planning and tracking 
  * Deposits received tracking 
  * Time tracking 
    * Linking with Proposals/jobs 
    * Tracking prevailing wage time 
    * Mobile time clock app 
  * Prevailing wage tracking on Proposals 
  * Job-specific expense tracking 
  * Syncing with QuickBooks for job expenses 



  


NOTE: After discussion, we're deferring QuickBooks integration.

  


Phase 2 features end at the point of invoicing.
