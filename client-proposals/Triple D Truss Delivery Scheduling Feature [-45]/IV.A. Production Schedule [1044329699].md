4.1. Production Schedule

This is the equivalent to existing grid view.

  


This will be accessed from a menu option that will open as a report in the AppHosting software but can also be printed. See the printout notes for details. 

  


  * Columns: 
    * Customer  
    * Quantity 
    * Item
    * Board Feet
    * Job Name
    * Cut (would be blank or hidden on the report, as they are not entering this information in the database) 
    * Made (checkbox) 
  * Total Board Feet at the bottom
    * If there are multiple weeks showing on the report, show a subtotal for each week
  * Group by Week then by Day (Mon-Fri; using the Delivery Date) 
  * Sort by Schedule Sequence 
  * Prompts
    * Recent Weeks (field to enter number of weeks; default to the system switch value)
