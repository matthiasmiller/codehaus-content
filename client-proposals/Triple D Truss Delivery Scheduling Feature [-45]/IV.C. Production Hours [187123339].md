4.3. Production Hours

Accessed via: New "Production Hours" menu option in the Deliveries section of the Triple D Truss menu

  


Title: Production Hours

  


Columns: 

  * Customer
  * Job
  * Schedule Date
  * Item (link to Delivery record) 
  * Production Hours



  


Grouped by: Status

  


Sorted by: Schedule Date

  


Filters: 

  * Status (multi-select drop list; defaults to blank = all) 
  * Start Date (looks at Schedule Date; defaults to blank = all) 
  * End Date (looks at Schedule Date; defaults to blank = all) 



  


Buttons: 

  * N/A 



  


Menu Visibility: 

  * N/A (visible for all users) 



  


Other Notes:

  * N/A
