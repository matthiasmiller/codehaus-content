1\. Overview

  


Requirements

Timberline Buildings is a postframe building contractor in Pennsylvania. They specialize in pole buildings, including hobby shops, garages, horse barns, and commercial projects. 

  


Timberline has 30+ employees, with 6-8 in admin/drafting/accounting. 

  


Currently Timberline is operating with a complex network of Excel spreadsheets for pricing and project management, as well as QuickBooks for accounting. They also use AutoCAD and SketchUp for design/drafting. 

  


Timberline has established a clear vision to be the most reliable and most effiecient pole building company in Pennsylvania. As part of pursuing that vision, they are looking to improve internal processes by adding software to consolidate information & processes and to cut out redundancy.

  


The name of this project is "Timberline Operating System", or "TOS".

  


The #1 goal of the project management tools is to reduce redundancy and enter things only one time.
