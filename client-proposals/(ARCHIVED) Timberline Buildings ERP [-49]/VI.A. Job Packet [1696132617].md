6.1. Job Packet

The Job Packet is a binder that is put together for each project. The exact layout is subject to change, but currently it contains pages/sections for the following:

  * Job Worksheet
  * Picking Lists
  * Material List
  * Trusses
  * Sketches and Misc.
  * Invoices and receipts
  * Vendor options
  * Site pictures



  


Further details on how TOS would help with this can be explored in the In-Depth Design.
