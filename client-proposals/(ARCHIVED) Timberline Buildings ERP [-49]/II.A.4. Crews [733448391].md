2.1.4. Crews

  


Requirements

TOS should track the various crews working on projects, to be able to assign jobs to crews and to report on things such as crew efficiency, etc. 

  


The following should be tracked for Crews: 

  * Crew Name (text) 
  * Employees (embedded spreadsheet of employees)
