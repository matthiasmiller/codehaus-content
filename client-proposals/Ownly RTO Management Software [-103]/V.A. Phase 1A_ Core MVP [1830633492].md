5.1. Phase 1A: Core MVP

Goal: Replace RTO Pro and support the contracts under Burkholder Management’s services. Establish a strong foundation that allows basic contract management and customer payments with clear visibility into overdue accounts.

  


Key Features:

  


  * External Contract Submission for contract submission (priced within Silverloom)
  * Customer Portal for account setup and payments
  * Merchant Processor Integration (one per Rental Company; priced for [Authorize.net](http://authorize.net "http://authorize.net") and Stripe)
  * Sales Tax Support via TaxJar or Avalara
    * Automatic tax code lookup based on location
    * Tax validation during contract submission
    * Ensure correct tax rates are applied to all contracts
  * Overdue Payments Dashboard with sorting
  * Role-Based Admin Portal with permissions
  * Secure Document Handling for SSNs and/or driver’s licenses
  * Depreciation Reporting:
    * Book and tax models
    * Straight-line/accelerated
    * Repeating Group (RG) on product record - specifically for tracking depreciation dates and amounts
    * Editable historical depreciation events with permission-controlled visibility
  * Logistics:
    * Tracking Retrievals
