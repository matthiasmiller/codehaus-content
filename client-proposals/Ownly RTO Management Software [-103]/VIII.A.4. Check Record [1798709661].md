8.1.4. Check Record

CHECK record

  * Accounting Check (section)
    * Accounting Company
    * Account
    * Number
    * Amount
    * Date
    * Payee (list of all contacts for the rental company)
    * Name & Address
    * Memo



  


  * Miscellaneous Items (section)
    * Misc Items (RG)
      * Account
      * Ref #
      * Amount
      * Memo



  


  * Payments (section with an RG)
    * Level
    * Record ID
    * View
    * Payment Amount
    * Is Refund (read only and auto calculated)
