4.5. Terms

  * CRA (Cash Reserve Account) is money up front that is used to reduce the monthly payment. 
  * Security Deposit is money paid up front that does NOT affect the monthly payment. If they repo the building, that money is used to fix the building, or it's used to pay past-due rent. Or it can be used to cover a month of rent. Otherwise, it is applied at the point of payoff as directly reducing the early cash payoff.
