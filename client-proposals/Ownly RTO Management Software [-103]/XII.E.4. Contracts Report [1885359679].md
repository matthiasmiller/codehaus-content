12.5.4. Contracts Report

Overview: This is a custom report of Contracts.

  


Accessed from:

  * Contracts | Contracts | Search Contracts



  


Title: Contracts

  


Columns: 

  * Contract (link to open the record)
  * Rental Company
  * Manufacturer
  * Contract Type
  * Contract Total
  * Total Due



  


Grouped by: Contract Status

  


Sorted by: "Contract " \+ String( ContractIDAutoNumber)

  


Filters: 

  * Status
  * Rental Company
  * Manufacturer
  * Contract Type



  


Buttons: 

  * New Contract (opens a blank new Contract record)



  


Menu Visibility: 

  * All Users



  


Live Updating: No
