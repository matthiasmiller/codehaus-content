4.1. Phased Development Plan

This document outlines a high-level design for a Silverloom system to help Rental Companies manage contracts, oversee collections, and engage customers.

  


The ultimate goal of the platform is to improve collections performance while giving Rental Companies flexible tools, clear insights, and better customer-facing features.

  


The rollout will follow a phased approach, starting with a streamlined MVP and building up toward deeper intelligence, integrations, and engagement. The phases (1-7) are summarized below. Details for the design of Phase 1A-C are included. Other future phases are not designed out.
