5.9. Phase 7: Advanced Analytics & System Expansion

Goal: Leverage behavioral data and offer data-driven decision-making tools.

  


Key Features:

  


  * Enhanced Behavioral Tracking:
    * Pixel tracking on manufacturer sites and marketing pages
    * Facebook Pixel integration for marketing attribution
    * Track website visits, page views, and interaction depth
    * Monitor manufacturer website visits by customers
  * Customer Activity Dashboard:
    * Comprehensive view for collections teams
    * Tracks total website hits by customers
    * Monitors app usage statistics
    * Shows past-due customers' activity patterns
    * Optimal contact time recommendations
  * Customer Activity Logs with time-of-day data
  * News Feed for collections teams highlighting noteworthy events
  * Comprehensive Financial Reports:
    * Revenue line items
    * Merchant fees breakdown
    * Detailed cash flow analysis by timeframe and product category
    * Asset management
  * PCI Vault Integration for tokenized payments
