13.5.5. Payments Report

Overview: This is a custom report of Payments.

  


Accessed from:

  * Accounting | Payments | Search Payments



  


Title: Payments

  


Columns: 

  * Payment (link to open the record)
  * Customer
  * Date
  * Payment Amount
  * Payment Method
  * Confirmation Date
  * NSF Date



  


Grouped by: NA

  


Sorted by: PaymentIDAutoNumber

  


Filters: 

  * Rental Company (list; required; defaults if there is only one active rental company)
  * Customer (list of all customers)
  * Payment Method (list of payment methods)
  * Start Date (date)
  * End Date (date)



  


Buttons: 

  


Menu Visibility: All users
