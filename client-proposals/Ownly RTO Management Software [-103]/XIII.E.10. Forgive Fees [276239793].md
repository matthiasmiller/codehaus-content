13.5.10. Forgive Fees

Overview: This is a custom report of fees for an individual contract which can be forgiven. 

  


Accessed from: Contract record (opens the report directly, bypassing the filter screen)

  


Title: Fee and Payment History

  


Preface: NA

  


Columns: 

  * Period
  * Type
  * Due Date
  * Subtotal
  * Subtotal Balance



  


Row-Specific Buttons: NA

  


Grouped by: 

  * Contract ID
  * Due Date



  


Sorted by: Due Date

  


Filters: 

  * Contract ID



  


Buttons: 

  * Forgive Partial Amount 
    * Prompts for amount to forgive and forgiveness reason
  * Forgive Full Amount
    * Prompts for forgiveness reason



  


Other Notes: 

  * Includes all fees that can be forgiven where the subtotal balance is greater than zero
  * The forgiveness amount cannot be more than the subtotal balance for the fee
