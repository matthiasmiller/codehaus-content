13.5.8. Fee and Payment History

Overview: This is a custom report of history for fees and payments, which displays information for an individual contract.

  


Accessed from: Contract record (opens the report directly, bypassing the filter screen)

  


Title: Fee and Payment History

  


Preface: NA

  


Columns: 

  * Due Date
  * Payment Date
  * Next Due Date
  * Adjustment Type
  * Rental Fee
  * Security Deposit
  * Late Fee
  * Tax
  * Total



  


Row-Specific Buttons: NA

  


Grouped by: 

  * Contract ID
  * Due Date



  


Sorted by: Due Date

  


Filters: 

  * Contract ID



  


Buttons: 

  * View Contract (Opens the Contract record whose information is being displayed in the report)



  


Other Notes: NA
