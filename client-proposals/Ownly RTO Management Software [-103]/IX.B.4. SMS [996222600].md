9.2.4. SMS

Basic Approach

  * Report to view conversations based on contact phone number(s)
  * x30 to send SMS (no images/attachments)
  * Report to view new messages (not dealt with)



  


One-off Sends

  * x30 button on the contact record to send SMS, prompts for text in ask prompt



  


Bulk Sends:

  * Report to filter down contacts by all kinds of factors
  * ABility to highlight and click Send Bulk SMS and fill in an ask prompt on the contents
