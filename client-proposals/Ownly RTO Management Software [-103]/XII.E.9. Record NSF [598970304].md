12.5.9. Record NSF

Overview: This is a custom report to record NSF for payments. It includes all payment rows for the customer.

  


Accessed from: the menu, Contract record, and Payment record

  


Title: Record NSF

  


Preface:

Select the ellipses on a row for the payment you wish to record an NSF for, then select "Record NSF".

The NSF will be recorded for all rows for the payment. The NSF Fee will be added to the contract for the selected row.

  


Columns: 

  * Payment Date
  * Due Date
  * Type
  * Total



  


Row-Specific Buttons: NA

  


Grouped by: 

  * Payment
  * Contract



  


Sorted by:

  


Filters: 

  * Customer
  * NSF Date



  


Buttons: 

  * Record NSF



  


Other Notes: 

  * Recording an NSF can automatically add Late Fees
