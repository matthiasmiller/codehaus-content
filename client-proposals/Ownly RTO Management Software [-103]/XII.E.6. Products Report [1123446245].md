12.5.6. Products Report

Overview: This is a custom report of Products.

  


Accessed from:

  * Accounting | Buildings | Search Products



  


Title: Products

  


Columns: 

  * Description
  * Rental Company
  * Manufacturer
  * Primary ID
  * Prod Secondary ID
  * Model
  * Style
  * Build Date
  * Depreciation



  


Grouped by: NA

  


Sorted by: "Product " \+ String( ProdIDAutoNumber)

  


Filters: 

  * Rental Company
  * Manufacturer
  * Model
  * Depreciation Method



  


Buttons: 

  * New Product (opens a blank new Product record)



  


Menu Visibility: 

  * All users
