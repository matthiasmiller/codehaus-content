5.5. Phase 3: Risk Scoring & Onboarding Experience

Goal: Enable intelligent collections prioritization and structured onboarding.

  


Key Features:

  


  * Basic Risk Scoring Model based on internal data
  * Customer Contract Variables:
    * Cost Reduction Agreement (CRA) - flagged as higher value than standard down payment
    * Deposit-to-building-cost ratio analysis
  * Welcome Call Script Tool with checkbox validation
    * Guided script with validation checkboxes
    * Records outcomes that feed into risk model
    * Verifies key customer details
  * Call Recording Integration for risk analysis and quality assurance
  * Customer Record Variables like:
    * Marital status
    * Housing (rent vs. own)
    * Time at current address
    * References
  * Optimal Collections Timing:
    * Analysis of when each customer is most likely to respond
    * Time-of-day recommendations for collections calls
    * Priority scheduling based on customer online activity patterns
  * Optional SMS-Based Points System
  * Prioritization View combining dollar amount, risk, and timing
  * KPIs for Collections Team performance tracking
  * Optional "Apply Before Sale" Process
    * Allow customers to review terms and sign digitally before finalizing sale
    * Pre-sale application and qualification
