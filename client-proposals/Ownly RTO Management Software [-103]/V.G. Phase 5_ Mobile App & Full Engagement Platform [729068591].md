5.7. Phase 5: Mobile App & Full Engagement Platform

Goal: Deliver a full customer experience through app-based tools and incentives.

  


Key Features:

  


  * Mobile App for payments, notifications, and engagement
  * Points System rewarding behavior (e.g., on-time payments, surveys)
  * Redemption at Payoff to incentivize consistent behavior
  * Location Awareness (optional) for collections strategy
  * Content Engagement Tools, contests, and surveys
  * Survey to catch the onboarding questions
  * Digital Incentive Pricing (e.g., 3% premium for standard customers vs. 4% premium for customers with app installed and survey completed)
