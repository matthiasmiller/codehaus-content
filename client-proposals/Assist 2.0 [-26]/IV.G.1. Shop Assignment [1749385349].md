4.7.1. Shop Assignment

Factors:

  * Proximity - How close is the shop to the location?
  * Capability - Can the shop build this type/features? (For example, ABC 1 and ABC 2 build different types of buildings.)
  * Inventory - Does the shop have the building materials on hand for this building? What's the lead time for their materials? (This might not be for first cut.)
  * Capacity - What is the lead time for this shop?
  * Logistics - What are the scores/lead time for the haulers for this shop?



  


Perhaps 2 expressions:

  * Can they do it?
  * How badly do they want it?
