Requirements

The Contact record will also include the following custom sections & fields:

    * Dealer Pricing Ratio (required; number field to 2 decimals; defaults to 1.00, used to determine the pricing for the Dealer, see Calculations section; Example: 1.10 will price this dealer 10% higher than the default prices; Validation Error if out of range: "The Pricing Ratio must be between 0.75 and 2.00.")
