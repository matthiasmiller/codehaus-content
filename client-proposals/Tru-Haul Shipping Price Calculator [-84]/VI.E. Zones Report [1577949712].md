6.5. Zones Report

  


Requirements

This is a report of Zones.

  


This is accessed from Tru-Haul | Zones | Zones. 

  


Title: Zones

  


Columns: 

  * Zone Number (link to record)
  * Zone Name 
  * Route 
  * (blank spacer column) 
  * Bottom Bucket Dropoff $ 
  * Bottom Bucket White Glove $ 
  * Top Bucket Dropoff $ 
  * Top Bucket White Glove $ 
  * (blank spacer column) 
  * Bottom Bucket Dropoff Ratio 
  * Bottom Bucket White Glove Ratio 
  * Top Bucket Dropoff Ratio 
  * Top Bucket White Glove Ratio



  


Grouped by: Active / Inactive 

  


Sorted by: Zone Number (smallest number at the top)

  


Filters: 

  * Route (drop list of Route; filters down as you type; defaults to blank = all)
  * Zone (drop list of Zone; filters down as you type; defaults to blank = all)



  


Buttons: 

  * New Zone (opens blank new Zone record)



  


Menu Visibility: 

  * Employees & Support



  


Other Notes:

  * N/A
