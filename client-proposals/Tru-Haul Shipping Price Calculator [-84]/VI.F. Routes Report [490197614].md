6.6. Routes Report

  


Requirements

This is a multi-pane report of Routes.

  


This is accessed from Tru-Haul | Zones | Routes. 

  


Title: Routes

  


Left Pane: 

Columns: 

  * Route Name (link to record)
  * # Zones (number of zones in this route)



  


Grouped by: Active / Inactive

  


Sorted by: Route Name (alphabetical)

  


Right Pane: This is the Zones report, filtered to the most recently-selected Route in the left pane.

  


Filters (applies to left pane): 

  * Route (drop list of all Route Names; filters down as you type; defaults to blank = all)



  


Buttons: 

  * New Route (opens blank new Route record) 
  * New Zone (opens blank new Zone record, defaults to Route selected in left pane)



  


Menu Visibility: 

  * Employees & Support



  


Other Notes: 

  * A sub-pane shows the Zones within the route
