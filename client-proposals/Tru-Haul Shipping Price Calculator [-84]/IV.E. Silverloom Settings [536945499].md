4.5. Silverloom Settings

  


Requirements

The Silverloom Settings on the Advanced menu will include the following sections for various configuration settings:

  


  * Tru-Haul Settings section (custom section): 
    * Address (plain text field; used to display Tru-Haul's address on the Quote printout)
    * City (plain text field; used to display Tru-Haul's city on the Quote printout)
    * State (drop list of States; used to display Tru-Haul's state on the Quote printout)
    * Zip (plain text field; used to display Tru-Haul's zip code on the Quote printout)
    * Tru-Haul Email: (email field; used to determine what email address is used when the "Email Tru-Haul" link on the Calculator record is clicked; also used to display the email address on the Quote printout)
    * Phone (phone number field; used to display Tru-Haul's phone number on the Quote printout)
    * Business Hours (plain text field; used to display Tru-Haul's business hours on the Quote printout; i.e., "M-F 8am - 5pm")
