3.2. Menus

  


Requirements

The Solution will have the following menu tabs. These tabs are not visible to Dealers since they only see the Calculator: 

  * Tru-Haul menu
    * Customized menu options:
      * Calculator section:
        * View Calculator (opens the Calculator record)
      * Zones section:
        * Zip Codes (opens the Zip Codes report)
        * Zones (opens the Zones report)
        * Routes (opens the Routes report)
      * Pricing section:
        * Master Pricing Table (opens the Master Pricing Table record)
        * Furniture Types (opens the Furniture Types report)
      * Contacts section:
        * Contacts (opens the Contacts report)
        * New Contact (opens blank new Contact record)
      * Wiki section:
        * Wiki (opens the Wiki report)
  * Advanced menu (contains menu options for various settings, configurations, etc.)
