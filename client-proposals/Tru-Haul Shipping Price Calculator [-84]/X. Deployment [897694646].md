10\. Deployment

CodeCrafters will assist the client in deploying the system initially:

  


  * Importing required data
    * Master Pricing table
    * Furniture Types
    * Zip Codes
    * Zones
    * Routes
    * Dealers / Retailers
  * Setting up initial Contact Types (see the corresponding section of the proposal) 
  * Setting up initial User Groups (see the corresponding section of the proposal) 
  * Documenting how to set up dealers to give them access to their calculator 
  * Configuring SMTP servers to point to Tru-Haul's email servers
