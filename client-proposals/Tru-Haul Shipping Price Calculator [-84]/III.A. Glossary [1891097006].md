3.1. Glossary

Overview: The purpose of this glossary is to provide a description of key terms, roles, and other things referred to in this proposal. These are listed in alphabetical order below:

  


  * Dealer: a furniture retailer that uses Tru-Haul's services to deliver furniture
  * Zone: a region that has a specific pricing, composed of one or more zip codes in the region
  * Route: a specific named delivery route. A route will include one or more zones.
  * Bucket: a single point where the x-axis and the y-axis of the Master Pricing Table meet
