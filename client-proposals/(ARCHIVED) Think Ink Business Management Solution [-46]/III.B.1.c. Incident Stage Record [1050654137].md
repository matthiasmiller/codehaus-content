3.2.1.3. Incident Stage Record

The Solution will use Incident Stages.

  


Sections and Fields:

  * Stage (plain text field)
  * Workflow (droplist)
  * Sort Order (plain text field)
  * Assignee (droplist)
  * Status (droplist)
  * Priority (droplist)



  


Data Access:

  * Editable by Admins
  * Viewable by all other users
