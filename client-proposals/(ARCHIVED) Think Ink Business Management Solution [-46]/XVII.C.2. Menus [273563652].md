17.3.2. Menus

Menus: The Solution will have the following menus:

  * Home (originally titled "ThinkInk LLC"; used for the most common/general features; accessible for all users)
  * Financial (used for finance-related features, etc.; used by and accessible for ___) 
  * Admin (accessible only for Full Admin users; used for Full Admin-level reporting and features)
  * Advanced (accessible to all users, with limited capabilities depending on permissions; used for settings, configuration, and other various features) 



  


Other Notes: 

  * Within a given menu, some menu options may be hidden for certain user groups or disabled (but still visible) for certain user groups.
