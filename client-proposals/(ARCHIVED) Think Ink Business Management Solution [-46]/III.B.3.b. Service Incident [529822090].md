3.2.3.2. Service Incident

  


Requirements

The Service-type incident would include the standard fields for the AppHosting incidents and standard Think Ink-specific sections/fields, plus the following custom section and field(s):

  


Service Incident Details section: 

  * Request Source (optional; drop list of Email, Fax, Phone)



  


Other Notes: 

  * The Contact field should be required for Service-type incidents.
  * There will be a "New Service Incident" menu option in the Incidents section of the Home Menu. Clicking this opens a new blank Service-type incident; Assignee should be defaulted to the contact's sales rep.
