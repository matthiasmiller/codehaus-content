3.3.2.2. Contacts + Incidents Report

  


Requirements

This is a multi-pane report of contacts with incidents (left pane) and their corresponding incidents (right pane).

  


This would be accessed from a "Contacts + Incidents" option in the Contacts section of the Home Menu. 

  


Title: Contacts + Incidents

  


Left Pane: Contacts with Incidents (shows all contacts in the database who are the Contact for at least one incident)

Columns: 

  * Contact (link to record)
  * Street Address 
  * City
  * State
  * Phone
  * Email 



  


Grouped by: Active/Inactive (active at the top)

  


Sorted by: Contact

  


Filters: 

  * Contact Type (multi-select drop list)
  * Name (same behavior as the Name filter on the standard Contacts report) 
  * City
  * State 
  * Zip
  * Email 
  * Phone #



  


Right Pane: Incidents for Selected Contact

Columns: 

  * ID
  * Title (link to record)
  * Type
  * Workflow
  * Assignee
  * Origination Date



  


Grouped by: Open/Closed (open at the top)

  


Sorted by: Origination Date (newest at the top)

  


Filters: N/A

  


Buttons: 

  * New Incident for Selected Contact (opens a blank incident with Contact defaulted)



  


Menu Visibility: All users

  


Other Notes: N/A
