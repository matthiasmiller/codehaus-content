4.3.3. QuickBooks Invoice Record

  


Requirements

Overview: This record is a read-only copy of the data from the QuickBooks Invoice record.

  


This is a custom record. 

  


Read-Only QB Fields:

  * Silverloom Internal ID (automatic number)
  * ListID (QuickBooks ID)
  * Customer
  * Invoice Date
  * Invoice #
  * Total
  * Balance
