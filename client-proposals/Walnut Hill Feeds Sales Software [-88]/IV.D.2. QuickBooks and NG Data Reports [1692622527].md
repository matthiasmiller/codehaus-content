4.4.2. QuickBooks and NG Data Reports

  


Requirements

The solution will include various reports to view:

  * QuickBooks Contacts
  * QuickBooks Invoices
  * NG Customers
  * NG Locations
  * NG Items
  * NG Orders



  


Each of these reports will include various columns and filters for things like customer, date, amount, and more.
