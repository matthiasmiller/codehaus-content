4.3.9. Incidents Record

  


Requirements

Overview: The solution will include the standard Silverloom Incidents feature. This is specifically to be used for the Check-In Incidents, with the following modifications: 

  * Frequency should be Every [__N__] [__Months__]
  * Also allow “Weeks” as a frequency
  * Automatically ensure there's 1 incomplete check-in so that the office can flag something that needs to happen.
