6.2. Grain Banks

The grain bank is used for farmers who haul in grain (such as corn) and retain ownership of that grain to be used in their feed. The software tracks how much grain has been used in the customerâ€™s orders.

  


Fields:

  * Grain Bank Name
  * Customer
  * Item (list of items)
  * Effective Date
  * Quantity (original quality)
  * Quantity Remaining



  


NOTE:

  * Grain banks do not have a start and end date
  * Customers may have multiple loads
  * Grain banks do not have a price since the customer owns the product
