1\. Overview

  


Requirements

Affordable Barns, Inc along with several other parent companies, coordinate the production of individual builders. This involves tracking various pieces of information in different databases. To facilitate easy access to this information by fax, AppHosting.zone will develop a system that can be accessed by telephone or fax and provides access to production/sales information, financials, and the ability to store/retrieve documents.
