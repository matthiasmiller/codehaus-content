Cypress Hills Local Development Corporation is a Brooklyn-based nonprofit dedicated to local community development and improvement in the Cypress Hills area. It serves an ethnically-diverse area with a variety of programs to promote the economic situation and leadership skills of local youth through employment opportunities.

  


CHLDC operates a variety of programs, including Summer Youth Employment Program, to facilitate youth employment with local businesses and organizations. These programs may involve more than one thousand youth and 150 work sites. The purpose of this software project is to enable SYEP participants to easily choose their preferred work sites, as well as empowering CDLHC staff to efficiently manage the process of matching participants to work sites.

  


Josh Nisly, working with AppHosting.zone, is an experienced application designer and developer, with more than fifteen years of experience developing software for Fortune 500 financial companies.
