3.2.3.1. Sales Opportunity Incident

  


Requirements

The Sales Opportunity-type incident would include the standard fields for the AppHosting Sales Opportunity incidents and standard Think Ink-specific sections/fields, plus the following custom section and field(s):

  


Think Ink Sales Opportunity Details section:

  * Prospect First Name (optional; text field)
  * Prospect Last Name (optional; text field)
  * Prospect Business (optional; text field)
  * Prospect Phone (optional; phone number field)
  * Prospect Email (optional; email field)
  * Tags (optional; multi-select drop list of Contact Tags; same as the Tags field on the Contact record) 
  * Print Volume (optional; plain text field)
  * Lead Source (optional; drop list of the following:) 
    * Website
    * Phone Call
    * Email
    * Other 



  


Other Notes:

  * The Lead Source list should be pre-populated with the items listed above.
  * There will be a "New Sales Opportunity" menu option in the Incidents section of the Home Menu. Clicking this opens a new blank Sales Opportunity-type incident; Assignee should be defaulted to the contact's sales rep.
