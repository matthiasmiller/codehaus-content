7\. Phase 3a

  


Requirements

Phase 3a focuses on improvements to the check-in feature and includes the following:

  * Region record and report feature
  * Change Check-In calculations from date to "Week of"
  * Adjustments to various records and reports
  * Adding a setting to allow admins to manually edit Contact records that otherwise are read-only from CA
