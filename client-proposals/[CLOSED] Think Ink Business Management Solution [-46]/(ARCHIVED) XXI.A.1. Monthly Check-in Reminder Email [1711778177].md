21.0.1. Monthly Check-in Reminder Email

_TR

  


_CH: is this actually an Email Reminder? 

  


Overview/Purpose: The purpose of the Monthly Check-in Reminder is to alert users of customers needing check-ins in the upcoming month. 

  


Trigger: Sent on the first day of each month

  


Send to: any user with one or more customer check-ins that month

_CH

  


Subject: <Month> Customer Check-ins Reminder

  


Text: 

<FirstName>, you have one or more customer check-ins for <Month>.

  


You can view the here: <link to report>

  


Thanks!

  


Other Notes:

  * When the report is opened from the email, it should prompt for an as-of date, defaulting to today.
  * Once the system is live, this will be set up as a scheduled task.
