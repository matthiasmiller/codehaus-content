11.2.5. School Contribution & Application Summary

This is a an excel export of school balances and applications.

  


This is accessed from FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | School | School Contribution & Application Summary. 

  


Title: Std Schools by Affiliation

  


Columns: 

  * School Name
  * Current Balance
  * # of Applications for 2023



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: N/A

  


Filters: 

  * As of Date (defaults to the current date)
  * Affiliation (blank = all) (drop-list of affiliations)
  * Active schools only (checkbox)
