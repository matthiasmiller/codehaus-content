9.3. Contribution

Accessed via: FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | Business | All Contributions

  


Sections and Fields:Â 

Contribution:

  * Business (drop-list of active businesses)
  * Anonymous (checkbox; uneditable; looked up from the selected business)
  * Amount (number; two decimals)
  * Date Entered
  * Postmarked Date
  * Check Date
  * Check Number
  * School Year
  * Business Application (drop-list of business applications for the selected business)
  * Type (drop-list of "Pre-K", "K-12", "OSTC", and "Non-EITC")
  * Receipt to DCED Sent by FBSS (checkbox)



  


Schools, embedded spreadsheet of:

  * School Name (drop-list of active schools)
  * View (link to the selected school)
  * School Year
  * Gross Amount (number; two decimals)
  * School Portion (read-only; 95% of the Gross Amount)
  * Admin Fee (read-only; 5% of the Gross Amount)



  


Comments:

  * (unlabeled memo)



  


Reports:

  * Thank You Letter (link to PDF of thank you letter to business)
  * Donation Receipt (link to PDF of donation receipt)
  * Receipt Envelope (link to PDF for receipt envelope to be sent to the DCED)
  * Business Envelope (link to PDF for envelope to send to business)
