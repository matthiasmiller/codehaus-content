9.1. Business

Accessed via: FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | Business | Businesses

  


Sections and Fields:Â 

Business Information:

  * Business ID (auto-set and read-only; business name + unique ID)
  * Name
  * Entity Type (drop-list of Partnership, LLC, S Corporation, C Corporation, and LLP)
  * Incorporated in PA (checkbox)
  * Registered in PA (checkbox)
  * Pass-through Entity (checkbox)
  * NAICS (Bus. Code) (number)
  * FEIN (must be nine digits long; auto-formatted with a dash)
  * PA Revenue # (number)
  * Enterprise Type (embedded spreadsheet of:
    * Enterprise Type (user-editable drop-list of enterprise types)
  * Tax Year End Month (drop-list of months)
  * Tax Year End Day (number; an error is returned on save if not a valid day for the selected month)
  * Active (checkbox; defaults to checked)
  * Postcard (checkbox; defaults to checked)
  * Anonymous (checkbox)



  


Contact Information:

  * CEO First Name
  * CEO Last Name
  * CEO Title - (user-editable drop-list)
  * Contact First Name
  * Contact Last Name
  * Contact Title (drop-list; the same list as CEO Title)
  * Primary Phone
  * Extension
  * Additional Numbers (spreadsheet behind a button:
    * Phone Number
    * Extension
    * Type (drop-list of Fax, Home, Mobile)
    * Notes
  * Email
  * Send Email (link)
  * Address
  * City
  * State (unlabeled; drop-list of state abbreviations; defaults to PA)
  * Zip (unlabeled)
  * County (list of PA counties)
  * Municipality (text box)



  


Postcards (visible if the Postcard box is checked)

  * Use Different Name And Address (checkbox)
  * Name
  * Address
  * City
  * State (unlabeled; drop-list of state abbreviations)
  * Zip (unlabeled)
  * Logo (memo)
  * Community Message (memo)



  


Comments:

  * (unlabeled memo)



  


Reports:

  * Business Applications (link to Business Applications report, filtered for the current business)
  * Contributions (link to Contributions report, filtered for the current business)
  * Print Envelope (opens Envelope PDF for the current business)
  * Print Tax Compliance Form (opens the generic tax compliance form PDF)



  


Other Notes:

  * The name and address fields in the postcard section default to the name and address of the business. However, if "Use Different Name and Address" is checked, they become editable.
    * If the checkbox is checked and subsequently unchecked, the fields are reset to their default values.
  * Businesses have the option to be anonymous, in which case their mailing address on their post cards will be FB's address and their name on all school reports will be "Anonymous Donor #000".
