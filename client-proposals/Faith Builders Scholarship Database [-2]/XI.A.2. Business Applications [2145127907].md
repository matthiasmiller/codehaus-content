11.1.2. Business Applications

  


Requirements

This is a report of business applications.

  


This is accessed from FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | Business | Business Applications. 

  


Title: Business Applications

  


Columns: 

  * Business Name
  * Contact Name
  * Address
  * City
  * State
  * Zip
  * Email
  * Phone
  * Most Recent Contribution Date
  * Most Recent Contribution Amount
  * Most Recent Contribution Type
  * Application Date Filed
  * Application Type
  * Application Year In Cycle
  * Application Number from State
  * Tax Year End Month



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: Business Name

  


Filters: 

  * Active (blank = all) (drop-list of "Active" and "Inactive")
  * Tax Year End (blank = all) (label)
  * Starting Month (drop-list of months)
  * Ending Month (drop-list of months)
  * Filing Year (blank = all) (defaults to latest effective school year from AppHosting.zone settings)
  * Year in Cycle (blank = all) (drop-list of "1st Year" and "2nd Year")
  * Filing Schedule (blank = all) (drop-list of "May 15 to June 30" and "July 1")
  * Type (blank = all) (drop-list of "Pre-K", "K-12", "OSTC", and "Non-EITC")



  


Buttons: 

  * New Record
