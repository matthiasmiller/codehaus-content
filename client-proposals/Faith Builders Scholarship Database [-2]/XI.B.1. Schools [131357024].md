11.2.1. Schools

This is a report of schools.

  


This is accessed from FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | School | Schools. 

  


Title: Schools

  


Columns: 

  * School Name
  * Contact (First Name)
  * Email Address
  * Qualifies for OSTC in <Current school year>
  * County
  * School Type in <Current school year>



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: School Name

  


Filters: 

  * Active / Inactive (blank = all) (drop-list of "Active" and "Inactive")
  * Year (defaults to the current school year)
  * OSTC (blank = all) (drop-list of "OSTC" and "Non-OSTC"
  * School Type (blank = all) (drop-list of "September School" and "November School")
  * Application Status (blank = all) (drop-list of "Confirmed" and "Unconfirmed")



  


Buttons: 

  * New Record
