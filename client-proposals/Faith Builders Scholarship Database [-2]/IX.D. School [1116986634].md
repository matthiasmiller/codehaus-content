9.4. School

  


Requirements

Accessed via: FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | School | Schools

  


Sections and Fields:

School:Â 

  * Name
  * County (drop-list of PA counties)
  * Affiliation (user-editable drop-list
  * Active (checkbox; defaults to checked)
  * Export to SPE (checkbox; defaults to checked)



  


Contact:

  * First Name
  * Last Name
  * Phone (embedded spreadsheet of:
    * Phone Number
    * Extension
    * Type (drop-list of "School", "Contact", and "Fax")
    * Notes
  * Email
  * Send Email (link; visible if Email is not blank)
  * Address
  * City
  * State (unlabeled; drop-list of states; defaults to PA)



  


Comments:

  * Yearly Tuition (memo)
  * Comments (memo)



  


Reports:

  * Contributions (link to Contributions report filtered for the current school)
  * Distribution (link to Distributions report filtered for the current school)
  * Family Income (link to PDF)
  * Print Contribution Update Today (last sent on 01/01/2001) (checkbox; displays that last printed date or "(never sent)" in the label)
  * School Envelope (link to PDF for envelope to send to school)



  


Other Notes:

  * If the name of the school is not blank, the School Applications by School report is displayed at the bottom of the record, filtered for the school.
