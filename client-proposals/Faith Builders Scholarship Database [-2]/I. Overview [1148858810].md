Background

Pennsylvania provides tax credits for approved donations to scholarship programs. FBSS acts as a liaison between businesses who want to participate in this program and schools who want to receive funds through this program.

  


[EITC](https://dced.pa.gov/programs/educational-improvement-tax-credit-program-eitc/ "https://dced.pa.gov/programs/educational-improvement-tax-credit-program-eitc/") (Educational Improvement Tax Credit) - For any schools

  


[OSTC](https://dced.pa.gov/programs/opportunity-scholarship-tax-credit-program-ostc/ "https://dced.pa.gov/programs/opportunity-scholarship-tax-credit-program-ostc/") (Opportunity Scholarship Tax Credit) - Specifically for schools in under-performing public school systems
