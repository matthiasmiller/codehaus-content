9.7. Family Application

Overview: The family application will track, for each child:

  * Their grade level
  * Their OSTC qualification
  * Their Learning Disability status



These will determine each student's eligibility for funding.

  


Accessed via: FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | Family | Family Applications

  


Sections and Fields:Â 

Family:

  * ID (auto-set and read-only)
  * Family (drop-list of active families)
  * School Year (number; must be a valid year)
  * Adults (number)
  * Dependents (number)
  * Income (number; no decimals)



  


Students, embedded spreadsheet of:

  * Student (drop-list of students for the selected family)
  * Grade (drop-list of Pre-K, K, and 1-12)
  * School (drop-list of active schools)
  * OSTC (checkbox)
  * Learning Disability (checkbox)



  


Notes

  * Memo



  


Reports:

  * Print Application Confirmation Today (last sent on 01/01/2001) (checkbox)
  * Family Envelope (link to family envelope PDF)
  * Send Email (link to send email)
  * Create New Student (opens a new student record for the selected family)
