11.1.1. Businesses

  


Requirements

This is a comprehensive report that can be used to pull up and edit businesses, or to export information to Excel.

  


This is accessed from FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | Business | Businesses. 

  


Title: Businesses

  


Columns: 

  * Business Name
  * Contact Name
  * Address
  * City
  * State
  * Zip
  * Email
  * Phone
  * Most Recent Contribution Date
  * Most Recent Contribution Amount
  * Most Recent Contribution Type
  * Most Recent Application Date Filed
  * Most Recent Application Amount
  * Most Recent Application Type
  * Most Recent Application Year in Cycle
  * Most Recent Application # From State
  * Tax Year End Month



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: Business Name

  


Filters: 

  * Active (blank = all) (drop-list of "Active" and "Inactive")
  * Application (blank = all) (drop-list of "With an Application on File" and "Without an Application on File")
  * Year (blank = all) (visible if Application is not blank)
  * Tax Year End (blank = all) (label)
  * Starting Month (drop-list of Months)
  * Ending Month (drop-list of Months)



  


Buttons: 

  * New Record
