1\. Overview

  


Requirements

The Gemeinde Brief is a biweekly publication for information, news, and announcements for the Amish community in Pennsylvania.

  


Staff members input the entries into the database, and the system generates an export that can be used for the final publication layout.

  


They currently have a web app that they've been using. However, they are experiencing reliability issues and are wanting to expand its capabilities.
