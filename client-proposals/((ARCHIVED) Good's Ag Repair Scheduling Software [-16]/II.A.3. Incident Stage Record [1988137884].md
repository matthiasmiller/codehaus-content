2.1.3. Incident Stage Record

Overview: Stages will be configured on Stage records and viewed on the Manage Incident Stages report.

  


Accessed via: 

  * Advanced | Configuration | Manage Incident Stages



  


Sections and Fields: 

  * Stage (plain text field)
  * Workflow (required; drop list of all Workflows)
  * Sort Order (plain text field)
  * Assignee (drop list of Employees)
  * Status (drop list of To Do, Doing, Done)
  * Priority (drop list of 1. Very High, 2. High, 3. Medium, 4. Low, 5. Very Low)
