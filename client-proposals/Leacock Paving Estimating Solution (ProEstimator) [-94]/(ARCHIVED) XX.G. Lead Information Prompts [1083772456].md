20.7. Lead Information Prompts

_SM: Tim Reitz 07/07/2025: Let's write this up as a "future" CR.

Sean Miller 07/07/2025: [[[IN11682](https://zch.apphosting.zone/Detail/Edit/2?RecordType=Incidents&NumberID=-11684&)]] - ZLP - Lead Information Prompts 

  


To facilitate easier data entry for new leads, text prompts could be included beside the fields. 

  


For example: 

  * Beside "Preferred Contact Method": "What is the best way to contact you?" 
  * Beside "Lead Source": "How did you hear about us?" 
  * etc.
