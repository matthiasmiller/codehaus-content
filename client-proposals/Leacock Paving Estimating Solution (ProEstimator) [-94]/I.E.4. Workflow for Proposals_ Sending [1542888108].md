1.5.4. Workflow for Proposals: Sending

To send a Proposal to a customer, a user follows the following general steps: 

  * To send it via email: 
    * Clicks the "Print / Email Proposal" checkbox (this makes the PDF / Email links visible and marks the Proposal as "Sent to Customer"), and then clicks the "Email Proposal" link to open the email draft in Outlook. 
    * Clicks the "Proposal PDF" link to open the PDF in the browser. 
    * Downloads the PDF. 
    * Attaches the PDF to the email draft. 
    * Sends the email. 
  * To print / send a paper copy: 
    * Clicks the "Print / Email Proposal" checkbox (this makes the PDF / Email links visible and marks the Proposal as "Sent to Customer"), and then clicks the "Proposal PDF" link to open the PDF in the browser. 
    * Downloads or prints the PDF. 
    * Passes it along to the customer (mail, in person, etc.)
