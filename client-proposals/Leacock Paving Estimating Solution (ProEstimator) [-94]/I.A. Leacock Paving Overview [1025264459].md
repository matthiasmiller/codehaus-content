1.1. Leacock Paving Overview

Leacock Paving is a paving company that serves Lancaster County, Pennsylvania, and the surrounding counties. Leacock specializes in several different categories of paving: 

  * Residential
  * Agricultural 
  * General commercial 
  * Commercial sports 



  


Leacock has 3 paving crews, with each crew being assigned to a paving category:

  * Crew 1: Commercial crew that does parking lots, small scale street paving for contractors, and agricultural. 
  * Crew 2: Residential crew that does a lot of custom projects
  * Crew 3: Commercial crew that does commercial sports construction, such as running tracks and tennis courts.
