Pricing and Details for the Customized Solution

CodeCrafters provides customized software solutions using the Silverloom Business Suite's hosted software platform. 

  


There are three main categories of costs: 

  * An upfront cost for the customized software solution, which includes access to the Silverloom Business Suite and the solution-specific customizations. 
  * A monthly subscription, which includes hosting, a dedicated test system, ongoing backups, and annual system updates. 
  * An annual maintenance contract (billed on a monthly or annual basis) that includes personal US-based client support, bug fixes, up-to-date solution design description ("living spec"), and more. 



Additional details are included below. 

  


Because of the size of the project, specific packages and features have been priced separately.

Ongoing Maintenance & Support: 

CodeCrafters provides ongoing maintenance and support for systems. This includes the following: personal US-based phone and email support, ongoing platform enhancements, maintenance and fixes for the customized software solution, and up-to-date design documentation ("living spec"). 

  


Standard support includes answering general questions and supporting standard use of the product. If a request goes beyond standard support, requiring additional customization or premium support, CodeCrafters will offer that option. 

  


This cost of the Maintenance & Support agreement is based on the size of the customization and the support required for the solution. Note that the Maintenance & Support cost may increase due to inflation, and will increase as the Solution grows with additional customizations. The maintenance can be paid on a monthly or an annual basis. 

  


  


Terms and Conditions: 

  


Backups / Up-time 

Silverloom Business Suite uses Amazon EC2 to host the solution and will do everything reasonably possible to maintain solution up-time. 

  


Silverloom Business Suite provides monitored database backups to Amazon S3 storage, maintaining daily backups for one week and after that monthly backups for one year. The daily backups are also downloaded to off-line storage on a Silverloom server in New York and verified for database integrity there. 

  


Warranty and Liability

CodeCrafters will maintain the solution in good working order and ensure that, given notice to any error or defect, the system is returned to good working order reasonably promptly. Liability is limited to the price paid by the subscriber for the most recent three-month period during the terms of this agreement.

  


Usage Rights

The Client has full usage rights to the customized solution for the duration of their subscription.

  


All customization work remains the intellectual property of CodeCrafters International Ltd. 

  


Remarketing of the customized solution to third parties may be negotiated with CodeCrafters.

  


Data Rights

The Client retains full rights to their data. In the event of the termination of the contract, they can optionally take an export of the data for their own use. 

  


Confidentiality

CodeCrafters and Silverloom Business Suite will strictly maintain Client data confidentiality. All employees working on the project have signed confidentiality agreements.

  


Termination

The Client has the right to terminate this agreement with written notice. A one-month notice is required if terminating after deployment.

  


CodeCrafters has the right to suspend or terminate this Agreement upon written notice if the Client (a) fails to make payment within thirty (30) days after notice of non-payment, or (b) engages in illegal or unethical use of the solution. CodeCrafters will make reasonable efforts to notify the Client and allow time to correct any unintentional issues before termination.

  


Any unpaid costs are payable upon termination.
