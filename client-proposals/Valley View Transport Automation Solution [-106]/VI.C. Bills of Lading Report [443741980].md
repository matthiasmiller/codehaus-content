6.3. Bills of Lading Report

  


Requirements

Overview: This is a custom report of Bills of Lading.

  


Accessed from: Main Menu | Misc. Reports | Bills of Lading (to be confirmed after further design)

  


Title: Bills of Lading

  


Columns: 

  * DispatchTrack ID (link to record)
  * Start of Week



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: DispatchTrack ID (numerically)

  


Filters: 

  * Dispatch Track ID (drop list of all DispatchTrack IDs; filters down as you type)
  * Date Start (date field)
  * Date End (date field)



  


Buttons: 

  * New Bill of Lading (opens blank new record)

  * TBD



  


Other Notes:
