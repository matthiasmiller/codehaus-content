13.6.1. Review Contributions Report

  


Requirements

Overview: This is an editable, two-pane report of all Contribution records with the "Reviewed" checkbox unchecked.

  


Accessed from: FBSS | EITC Business | Review Contributions

  


Title: Review Contributions

  


Right Pane:

Columns: 

  * Date Entered (editable; date field)
  * Business (link to record)
  * Amount
  * Application Year
  * Reviewed (editable; checkbox)



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: Date Entered (oldest date at the top)

  


Left Pane:

Columns:

  * School (link to record)
  * School Year
  * Gross Amount



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


 Sorted by: School (alphabetically)

  


Filters: 

  * N/A



  


Buttons: 

  * Mark selected rows as reviewed (only visible to users with the "Review contributions" permission; marks all selected rows as "Reviewed")



  


Menu Visibility: 

  * All users



  


Save Type: Save Button

  


Other Notes:

  * N/A
