20.1.7.1. Receipt of Contribution

  


Requirements

Accessed from: Contribution record 

  


Name: Receipt of Contribution

  


Level: Contribution

  


Subject: Thank You for Contributing; Here's Your Receipt

  


Send to: Primary related contacts for the business that contributed

  


Attachments (note: the attachment path is defined in the import that creates the email records; it is not in the attachments table on the communications record): 

  * Contribution Donation Receipt



  


Body: This email body is configured by the client in the live system.
