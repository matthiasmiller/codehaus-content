13.3.4. Direct Businesses

  


Requirements

Overview: This is report of Business-type Contacts with the "EITC" checkbox checked, with various filters. This is a comprehensive report that can be used to view and open EITC Businesses, or to export information to Excel.

  


Accessed from: Faith Builders Scholarship Services | EITC Business | Direct Businesses. 

  


Title: EITC Businesses

Columns: 

  * Business Name (link to open record) 
  * Contact Name
  * Address
  * City
  * State
  * Zip
  * Email (displays all Primary email addresses for Primary Related Contacts, or all Primary email addresses for the Business if there is no Primary Related Contact; if multiple email addresses are displayed, they are separated with semicolons) 
  * Phone
  * Most Recent Contribution Date
  * Most Recent Contribution Amount
  * Most Recent Contribution Type
  * Most Recent Application Date Filed
  * Most Recent Application Amount
  * Most Recent Application Type
  * Most Recent Application Year in Cycle
  * Most Recent Application # From State
  * Tax Year End Month



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: Business Name

  


Filters: 

  * Active (blank = all) (drop-list of blank / "Active" / "Inactive"; defaults to "Active")
  * Application (blank = all) (drop-list of blank / "With an Application on File" and "Without an Application on File"; defaults to blank)
  * Year (blank = all) (visible if Application is not blank; defaults to blank)
  * Tax Year End (blank = all) (label)
  * Starting Month (drop-list of Months; defaults to blank = all)
  * Ending Month (drop-list of Months; defaults to blank = all)
