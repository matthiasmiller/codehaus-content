11.1.2. School District Record

  


Requirements

Overview: This is a custom record type used to track School Districts and their corresponding counties.

  


Accessed via: Configure School Districts Report 

  


Sections and Fields: 

  * School District section
    * Name (required; plain text; validate against duplicates; defaults to blank for new records) 
    * Active (checkbox; defaults to checked) 



  


Modification History: This contains the following standard features for modification tracking: 

  * Created: (name, date, and time stamp) 
  * Last Modified: (name, date, and time stamp)
  * Modification History (link to open the report for the record)



  


Data Access: All users can view and edit

  


Special Considerations: 

  * Copy Record: Allowed (all fields clear)
  * Delete Record: Allow deletion until the record has been referenced by another record, then disallow.
  * Merge Record: Allowed
