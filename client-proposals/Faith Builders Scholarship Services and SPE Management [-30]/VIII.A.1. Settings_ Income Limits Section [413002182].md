8.1.1. Settings: Income Limits Section

Income Limits:

  * Income Limits, embedded spreadsheet (RG):



Columns:

  * Effective Year
  * Base Salary (number; no decimals; required)
  * Per Dependent (number; no decimals; required)



Buttons:

  * "+" \- insert row
  * "-" \- delete selected row



Sorted by:

  * Effective Year



  


Validation:

  * Error: If Effective Year is not a valid year.
  * Error on change: if duplicate Effective Years are entered.
  * Error on save: if more than one blank Effective Year is entered.
