11.2.11. Contact - Business Contacts Section

  


Requirements

Business Contacts section (visible if Contact Type = Business and "Eligible for SPE" checkbox is checked):

  


  * Business Contacts (no label; embedded spreadsheet (RG) with the following:
    * Columns:
      * Contact Name (auto-set and read-only)
      * Link (opens the corresponding Contact record)
    * Automatically sorted by: Contact Name
    * Show 5 rows without scrolling



  


Other Notes: 

  * This RG is auto-populated with all Contacts who have the current Business-type Contact set as their 'Linked Organization'.
