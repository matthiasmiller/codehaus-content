16.0.2.5. Send Contribution Updates

Accessed from: FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | School | Send Contribution Updates

  


This runs the School Contribution Update email merge.

Ask prompts:

  * Message (used in the email body)
  * Only Include Schools With a Contribution Since Last Update (checkbox; defaults to checked)
  * School Type (blank = all) (drop-list of September School, November School)
