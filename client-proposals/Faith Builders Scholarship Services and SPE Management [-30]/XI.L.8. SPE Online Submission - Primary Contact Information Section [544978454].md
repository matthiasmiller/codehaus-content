11.12.8. SPE Online Submission - Primary Contact Information Section

  


Requirements

Primary Contact Information (visible for businesses):

  * Linked Primary Contact (drop-list of individual type contacts that match on name, phone number, or email address from the column to the right)
  * Contact First Name
  * Contact Middle Name
  * Contact Last Name
  * Contact Preferred Name
  * Contact Address
  * Contact City 
  * Contact County (drop-list of PA counties)
  * Contact State (drop-list of state abbreviations)
  * Contact Zip
  * Contact Phone Number
  * Contact Email



  


Additional notes:

  * All fields except for Linked Primary Contact display text in green if they match the column to the right.
