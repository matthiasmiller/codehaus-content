18.0.3.5. QuickBooks Disbursements

  


Requirements

This is a report of SPE disbursements.

  


Accessed from: Faith Builders SPE | Financial Reports | QuickBooks Disbursements

  


Columns:

  * Check Date
  * Check Number
  * Payee
  * State Application Number
  * Amount (total type = sum)
  * SPE Application (link to SPE application)



  


Sorted by: Check Date

  


Filters:

  * SPE (blank = all) (drop-list of SPEs)
  * Payee (blank = all) (drop-list of Scholarship Organizations)
  * Check Start Date (defaults to January 1 of the current year)
  * Check End Date
