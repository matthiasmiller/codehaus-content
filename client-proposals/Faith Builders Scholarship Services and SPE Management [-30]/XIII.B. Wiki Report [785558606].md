13.2. Wiki Report

  


Requirements

Overview: This is a two-pane report of Wiki records. 

  


Accessed from: 

  * Faith Builders Scholarship Services | Wiki | Wiki 
  * Faith Builders SPE | Wiki | Wiki 



  


Left pane: has a single Category column, including "(All Categories)", "(Recent Unread)", "Recent", and any categories added to the list for the Category field.

  


Right pane: is filtered by the category selection.

Columns:

  * [ ] (used to mark pages as read)
  * Category
  * Title
  * ID



Grouped by:

  * If the category selected in the left pane is "(Recent)" or "(Recent Unread)": last modified date. There are individual groupings for modifications in the last seven days, followed by groupings for Last Week, Two Weeks Ago, Three Weeks Ago, Last Month, and Older.
  * Category



Sorted by: 

  * If the category selected in the left pane is "(Recent)" or "(Recent Unread)": last modified date. 
  * All other categories: Title.
