25.1.2.1. by School

  


Requirements

The SO needs a list of all admitted members who've contributed to which school for a specific tax year.

  


The report will prompt for:

  * Tax Year (default to current tax year; required)
  * School (filtered list of schools; blank = all)



  


The columns should be:

  * Member
  * Address
  * City
  * State
  * Zip
  * County
  * Contribution Amount



  


The report should group by School.

  


Development Specification

4 Hours (if done w/ by Member)
