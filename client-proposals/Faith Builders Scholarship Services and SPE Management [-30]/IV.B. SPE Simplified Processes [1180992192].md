4.2. SPE Simplified Processes

  


Requirements

Here is the overview of the process:

  


  * Faith Builders establishes an SPE.
  * Faith Builders collects member applications from interested parties.
  * The SPE submits an application to the state for a "block" of contributions.
  * Once approved by the state, Faith Builders selects applicants for this approval ("block" of contributions).
  * The members make their contribution (by default) within 20 days.
  * The SPE submits a payment to the Scholarship Organization.
  * The SPE submits proof to the state.
  * The SPE provides tax documentation to each of the members.



  


Additional detail is included in the documentation provided by Faith Builders, as well as in the remainder of this proposal.
