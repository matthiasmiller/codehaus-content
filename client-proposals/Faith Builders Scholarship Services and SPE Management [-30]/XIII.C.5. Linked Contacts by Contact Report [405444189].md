13.3.5. Linked Contacts by Contact Report

  


Requirements

Overview: This is report that displays the Contact records on which a selected Individual-type Contact is set as a "Related Contact".

  


Accessed from: Displayed as a bottom report on Individual-type Contact records

  


Title: Linked Contacts by Contact

  


Columns: 

  * Linked Contact (link to open record; displays Contacts for which the selected Contact is set as a "Related Contact") 
  * Role (displays the "Role" of the selected Contact, from the corresponding Related Contacts RG on the Linked Contact's record) 



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: Linked Contact 

  


Filters: 

  * Individual Contact ID (required; number field; looks at the Contact ID; defaults to blank)



  


Buttons: 

  * N/A 



  


Menu Visibility: 

  * N/A (not accessible from the menu) 



  


Other Notes:

  * N/A
