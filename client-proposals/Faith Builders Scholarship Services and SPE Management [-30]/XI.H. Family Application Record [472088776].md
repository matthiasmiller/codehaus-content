11.8. Family Application Record

  


Requirements

Accessed via: Faith Builders Scholarship Services | Family | Family Applications

  


Overview: The family application tracks, for each child:

  * Grade level
  * OSTC qualification
  * Learning Disability status



  


These will determine each student's eligibility for funding.
