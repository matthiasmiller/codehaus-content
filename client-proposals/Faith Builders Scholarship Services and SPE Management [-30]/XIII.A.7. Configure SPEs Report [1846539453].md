13.1.7. Configure SPEs Report

  


Requirements

Overview: This is a report of SPE records.

  


Accessed from: Advanced | Configuration | Configure SPEs

  


Title: SPEs

  


Columns: 

  * SPE
  * Linked Business
  * Sort Order



  


Grouped by: Active/Inactive

  


Sorted by: Sort Number + SPE (name) 

  


Filters: 

  * Show Inactive



  


Buttons: 

  * New SPE
