16.0.5.7. OSTC Distributions

This is an excel export of OSTC distributions for the given year.

  


Accessed from: FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | EITC Accounting | OSTC Distributions

  


Columns:

  * County
  * Family
  * Household Size
  * Household Income
  * Student Name
  * OSTC Award
  * School



  


Filters:

  * Year (defaults to the current default school year from AppHosting.zone settings)
