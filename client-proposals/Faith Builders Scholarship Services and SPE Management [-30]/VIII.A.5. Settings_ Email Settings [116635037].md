8.1.5. Settings: Email Settings

  


Requirements

SPE Settings section:

  * Coordinator Details: (label)
  * Name (optional; plain text field) 
  * Title (optional; plain text field) 
  * Phone (optional; plain text field, to allow for entering an extension if desired)
  * Email (optional; plain text field; validates that the email address is in a valid format)
