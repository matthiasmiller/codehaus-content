11.2.19. Contact - Linked Contacts by Contact Bottom Report

  


Requirements

Linked Contacts by Contact Bottom Report (visible if Contact Type = Individual): 

  


This report across the bottom of the detail screen displays the Linked Contacts by Contact Report, filtered to this Contact, showing all Contacts on which this Contact is set as a "Related Contact". See the corresponding section of the proposal for further details.
