10.1. Online Submissions to Process Report Alert

  


Requirements

Overview/Purpose: This is a report alert that displays when there are open SPE Online Submissions. 

  


Details: 

  * Title: Online Submissions to Process
  * Displays within 20 minutes of: 
    * The creation of a new open Online Submission record 
    * An existing Online Submission record being changed from "Closed" to "Open"
  * Action: Open the "Online Submissions" report, filtered to show only Open records
  * User(s) to notify: All users 
  * Dismiss type: Auto-dismiss when all items are resolved
