Requirements

This is a report of all Member Applications with an admittance status of Waiting for Payment.

  


Accessed from: Accessed from: Faith Builders SPE | Financial Reports | Missing Payments

  


Title: Missing Payments

  


Columns:

  * SPE
  * SPE Application
  * Member
  * Payment Due Date
  * Amount Owed (total type = sum)



  


Filters:

  * SPE Application (blank = all) (drop-list of SPE Applications)



  


Sorted by:

  * Due Date (oldest first)
