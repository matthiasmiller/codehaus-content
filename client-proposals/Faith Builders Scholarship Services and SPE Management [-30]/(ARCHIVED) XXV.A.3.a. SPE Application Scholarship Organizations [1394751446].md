25.1.3.1. SPE Application Scholarship Organizations

  


Requirements

This is a totals only report of admitted SPE member applications.

  


Accessed from: bottom report for SPE Application record

  


Title: SPE Application Scholarship Organizations

  


Columns:

  * Scholarship Organization
  * Amount



  


Filters:

  * SPE Application (required; drop-list of SPE Applications)
