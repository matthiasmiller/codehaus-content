11.2.3. Contact - Phone Section

Phone section (standard):

  * (embedded spreadsheet with the following:) 
    * Columns: 
      * Phone (auto-formats with dashes if 10 digits long)
      * Type (drop-list)
        * List Items: Fax, Home, Mobile, Work
      * Primary (checkbox)
      * Notes
    * Automatically sorted by: "Primary" column, checked at the top
    * Buttons to insert/remove rows:  "+" / "-" 
    * Show 4 rows without scrolling
