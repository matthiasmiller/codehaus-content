16.0.6.2. Auto-Create Direct Business Applications

Accessed from: Faith Builders Scholarship Services | EITC Business Renewal Applications | Auto-Create Direct Business Applications

  


  


This is an import with a prompt for "Copy Applications from Year". 

  


The prompt screen also displays the following message: "NOTE: If any applications already exist for the following year, they will be updated to match the default values."

  


Selecting a year and clicking "Continue" creates Business Applications for the following year for all active Businesses who had an application for the selected year but not the following year.
