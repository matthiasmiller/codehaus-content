9.2. Person Titles List

  


Requirements

This is a user-editable simple list used to track titles of people within organizations. 

  


Example list items may include: 

  * President 
  * Secretary 
  * Vice President
  * Controller 
  * Director of Operations
  * CEO 
  * CFO 
  * etc.



  


This list is universal for all users, and all users can add to it via the "+" option on the drop list on the Contact record.
