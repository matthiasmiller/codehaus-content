16.0.3.3. School Contribution & Application Summary

  


Requirements

This is a an excel export of school balances and applications.

  


Accessed from: FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | EITC Reports | School Contribution & Application Summary 

  


Title: Std Schools by Affiliation

  


Columns: 

  * Name
  * Current Balance
  * # of Applications for <year from As of Date>



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: N/A

  


Filters: 

  * As of Date (defaults to the current date)
  * Affiliation (blank = all) (drop-list of affiliations)
  * Active schools only (checkbox)
