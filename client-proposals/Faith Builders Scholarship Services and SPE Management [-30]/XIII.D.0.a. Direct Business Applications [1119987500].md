13.4.0.1. Direct Business Applications

  


Requirements

Overview: This is an editable report of Business Application records, with various filters.

  


Accessed from: Faith Builders Scholarship Services | EITC Business | Direct Business Applications

  


Title: Direct Business Applications

  


Columns: 

  * Business Name (link to open record) 
  * Application Type
  * Tax Year End Month
  * Received App and Emailed Compliance (editable)
  * Application Number from State (editable)
  * Application Date Filed (editable)
  * Status
  * Compliance Filed (editable)
  * Notes
  * Email (displays all Primary email addresses for Primary Related Contacts, or all Primary email addresses for the Business if there is no Primary Related Contact; if multiple email addresses are displayed, they are separated with semicolons)
  * Contact Last Name
  * Contact First Name
  * Address
  * City 
  * State
  * Zip
  * Phone
  * Most Recent Contribution Date
  * Most Recent Contribution Amount



  


Grouped by: 

  * First by: Filing Schedule (in the following sequence:
    * July 1
    * May 15 to June 30)
  * Second by: Commitment (in the following sequence:
    * Year 1 of 1
    * Year 1 of 2
    * Year 2 of 2)



  


Sorted by: Business Name

  


Filters: 

  * Active (blank = all) (drop-list of blank / "Active" / "Inactive"; defaults to blank)
  * Tax Year End (blank = all) (label)
  * Starting Month (drop-list of months; defaults to blank = all)
  * Ending Month (drop-list of months; defaults to blank = all)
  * Filing Year (blank = all) (number; defaults to latest effective school year from AppHosting.zone Settings)
  * Year in Cycle (blank = all) (drop-list of "1st Year" and "2nd Year"; defaults to blank)
  * Filing Schedule (blank = all) (drop-list of blank / "May 15 to June 30" / "July 1"; defaults to blank)
  * Type (blank = all) (drop-list of blank / "Pre-K" / "K-12" / "OSTC" / "Non-EITC"; defaults to blank)



  


Buttons: 

  * New Record
  * Save Changes



  


Menu Visibility: All users

  


Other Notes:

  * N/A
