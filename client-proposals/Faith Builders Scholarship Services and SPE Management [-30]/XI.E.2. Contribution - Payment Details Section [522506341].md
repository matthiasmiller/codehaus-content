11.5.2. Contribution - Payment Details Section

Payment Details:

  * Postmarked Date (date; required)
  * Check Date (date; required)
  * Check Number (number; required)
  * Amount (number; required)
  * Check Status (required if a Check Date is specified; drop list of Check Status List options)
  * Deposit Date (date; required if Check Status = "Deposited" or "Scheduled for Deposit")
