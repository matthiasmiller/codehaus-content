13.16. Ad-Hoc Emails Report

Overview: This is a report of all Ad-Hoc Email recipients.

  


Accessed from: Advanced | Ad-Hoc Communication | Preview Email Recipients and Content

  


Title: Ad-Hoc Emails

  


Columns: 

  * Contact (link to record)
  * Emails
  * Subject (displays the subject from the Ad-hoc Email Template)
  * Body (displays the body from the Ad-hoc Email Template)



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: Contact (alphabetically)

  


Filters: 

  * N/A



  


Buttons: 

  * Edit Ad-hoc Email Template (opens the Ad-hoc Email Template)
  * Create Ad-hoc Emails (creates emails, viewable in the "View/Send <Department (from the Template> Emails> menu option



  


Menu Visibility: 

  * All users



  


Other Notes: N/A
