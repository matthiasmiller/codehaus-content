20.1.6.3. School Contributions Update

Accessed from: Scholarship Services | School | Send Contribution Updates

  


Name: School Contributions Update

  


Level: Contacts

  


Send To: Schools that do not have a future "Hold Contribution Notification Until" date and that match the filters:

  * Only Include Schools With a Contribution Since the Last Update (defaults to checked)
  * School Type (defaults to blank = all)



  


Subject: Contribution Update

  


Attachments (note: the attachment path is defined in the import that creates the email records; it is not in the attachments table on the communications record): 

  * School Contributions Update PDF



  


Body: This email body is configured by the client in the live system.
