11.2.7. Contact - School Details Section

  


Requirements

School Details section (visible if Contact Type = School):

  * Default Scholarship Organization (drop-list of Scholarship Organization type contacts)
  * Contact First Name (plain text)
  * Contact Last Name (plain text)
  * Financial Need (drop-list)



List items:

  * High
  * Medium
  * Low


  * Targeting Level (drop-list)



List items:

  * High
  * Medium
  * Low


  * Interested in Membership Communications (checkbox)
  * Hold Contribution Notification Until (date)
  * View Annual SPE Contribution Summary (report link; opens the Annual Contribution Summary by School report)
  * Print Contribution Update Today (checkbox)
    * "(last sent on dd/mm/yyyy)" (label; visible if Print Contribution Update Today was ever previously checked)
    * PDF (link; visible if Print Contribution Update Today is checked; opens a PDF of the contribution update)
  * Affiliation (drop-list)
  * School Envelope (link to PDF of envelope with school address) 
  * Do Not Send Postcards (checkbox; defaults to not checked; if checked, the School is excluded from postcard creation, the "Business Postcards" link is hidden on related Distribution records, and the reference to postcards is hidden on the "Scholarship Award Check Letter")
