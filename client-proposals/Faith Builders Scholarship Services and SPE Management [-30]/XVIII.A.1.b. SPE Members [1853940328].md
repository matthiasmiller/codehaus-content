18.0.1.2. SPE Members

  


Requirements

This is a report of SPE members.

  


Accessed from: Faith Builders SPE | SPE's | SPE Members

  


Title: Members of <SPE>

  


Columns:

  * Member (link to the Contact detail)
  * Address
  * County
  * City
  * State
  * Zip
  * Total Contribution (total type = sum)
  * View Applications (link, blank column heading)



  


Grouping:

  * If the "Group by Approval" checkbox is checked, the report will group by SPE Application. Otherwise it will not be grouped.



  


Filters:

  * SPE (required; list of SPEs)
  * SPE Member Status (blank = all) (multiple-select list of Admitted, Waiting for Payment)
  * Tax Year (required; number field)
  * Group by Approval (checkbox; default to checked)
