16.0.2.2. Schools

This is a report of school type contacts.

  


Accessed from: FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | School | Schools

  


Title: Schools <Year>

  


Columns: 

  * School Name
  * Paperwork
  * Family Application Deadline
  * Target Funds
  * School Size
  * Most Recent Contribution Update
  * Balance
  * Award Review Sent
  * School Comments
  * Current Year Distribution Notes
  * Contact (First Name)
  * Email



  


Grouped by: School Type

  


Sorted by: School Name

  


Filters: 

  * Active / Inactive (blank = all) (drop-list of "Active" and "Inactive")
  * Year (defaults to the current school year)
  * OSTC (blank = all) (drop-list of "OSTC" and "Non-OSTC")
  * School Type (blank = all) (drop-list of "September School" and "November School")
  * Application Status (blank = all) (drop-list of "Confirmed" and "Unconfirmed")



  


Buttons: 

  * New School
