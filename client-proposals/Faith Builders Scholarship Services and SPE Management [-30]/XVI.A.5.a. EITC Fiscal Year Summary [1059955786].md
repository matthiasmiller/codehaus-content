16.0.5.1. EITC Fiscal Year Summary

This is a report of totals (e.g. contributions, managed schools) for a given fiscal year.

  


Accessed from: FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | EITC Accounting | EITC Fiscal Year Summary 

  


Title: <School Year> Fiscal Year Summary

  


Columns: 

  * # of Business Contributions
  * Contribution Amount
  * # of Schools Total
  * # of Families Total
  * # of Students Total
  * # of Schools PASS Managed
  * # of Families PASS Managed
  * # of Students PASS Managed
  * # of Schools School Managed
  * # of Families School Managed
  * # of Students School Managed



  


Filters: 

  * School Year (defaults to the current school year)
