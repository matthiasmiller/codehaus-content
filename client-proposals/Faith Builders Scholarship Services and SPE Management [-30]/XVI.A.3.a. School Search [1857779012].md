16.0.3.1. School Search

This is a report of school contacts.

  


Accessed from: FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | EITC Reports | School Search

  


Title: Schools

  


Columns: 

  * School
  * Primary Contact Name
  * Primary Contact Email
  * County
  * Default Scholarship Organization
  * Financial Need
  * Targeting Level



  


Grouped by: Active/Inactive

  


Sorted by: School

  


Filters: 

  * County (blank = all) (drop-list of PA Counties)
  * Scholarship Organization (blank = all) (drop-list of Scholarship Organization type contacts)
  * Financial Need (blank = all) (drop-list of High, Medium, Low)
  * Targeting Level (blank = all) (drop-list of High, Medium, Low)
  * Active School Only (checkbox; defaults to checked)



  


Buttons: 

  * New School
