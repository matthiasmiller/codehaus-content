18.0.2.2. Online Submissions

  


Requirements

This is a report of all online SPE submissions. Note that this is used for the "Online Submissions to Process" report alert. 

  


Accessed from: Faith Builders SPE | Member Applications | Online Submissions

  


Title: Online Submissions

  


Columns:

  * Application Date
  * Member
  * Phone Number
  * Email
  * Amount



  


Filters:

  * Member (optional; list of contacts; excludes unlinked submissions)
  * Include closed submissions (checkbox; defaults to checked)



  


Grouped by:

  * Open/Closed



  


Sorted by:

  * Application date.
