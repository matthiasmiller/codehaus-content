18.0.2.1. Member Applications

  


Requirements

This is a report of member applications.

  


Accessed from: Faith Builders SPE | Member Applications | Member Applications

  


Title: Member Applications

  


Columns:

  * Member
  * Anonymous
  * Application Type
  * Application Date
  * Tax Year
  * Year #
  * Total Amount (total type = sum)
  * Amount Received (total type = sum)
  * Accepted by SPE
  * Admittance Status
  * Payment Due Date (red if within warning period)
  * # of Prior Admitted Applications



  


Filters:

  * Member (blank = all) (drop-list of individual of business contacts marked Eligible for SPE)
  * SPE (blank = all) (drop-list of SPEs)
  * Application Type (blank = all) (drop-list of Prefilled by SPE, Completed by Member)
  * Tax Year (blank = all) (defaults to the current tax year)
  * Application Start Date (blank = all)
  * Application End Date (blank = all)
  * Admittance Status (blank = all) (drop-list)
  * Accepted by SPE (blank = all) (drop-list of Yes or No)
  * Check Status (blank = all) (multi-select drop-list of Deposited, Scheduled for Deposit, Held, and Shredded)
  * Year (blank = all) (drop-list of Year 1 of 2 or Year 2 of 2)



  


Grouped by:

  * Admittance status, then by SPE application (which includes SPE name).



  


Sorted by:

  * Member



  


Additional notes:

  * The Amount Received is the check amount if it is Held or Deposited.
  * The # of Prior Admitted Applications shows the total number of admitted applications across all SPEs. It counts the number of applications with an Admittance Date before this application's admittance date (if specified) or application date (if not admitted).
