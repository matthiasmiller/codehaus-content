16.0.5.2. Direct Contribution by Business

This is a report of contributions, filtered by business.

  


Accessed from: FaithBuilders Scholarship Services | EITC Accounting | Direct Contribution by Business 

  


Title: Contribution by Business

  


Columns: 

  * Postmarked Date
  * Business Name
  * School Name
  * School Year
  * Gross Amount Before 5% (total type = sum)
  * Contribution Type



  


Grouped by: Business Name

  


Sorted by: Postmarked Date (most recent at the top)

  


Filters: 

  * Start Date (blank  = all) (defaults to the beginning of the current fiscal year)
  * End Date (blank = all)
