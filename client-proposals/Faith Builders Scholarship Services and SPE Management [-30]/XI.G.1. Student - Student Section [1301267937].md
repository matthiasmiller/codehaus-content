11.7.1. Student - Student Section

Student section:

  * Student ID (auto-set and read-only; <Last Name>, <First Name> #<unique ID>)
  * Family (drop-list of active families; required; only editable before the record has been saved; clickable link to the Family record when not editable)
  * Secondary Contact (uneditable; lookup from family record)
  * First Name (plain text; required)
  * Last Name (plain text; required)
  * Active (checkbox; defaults to checked)



  


Additional behaviors:

  * When Family is set, Last Name is set to the Last Name of the family.
