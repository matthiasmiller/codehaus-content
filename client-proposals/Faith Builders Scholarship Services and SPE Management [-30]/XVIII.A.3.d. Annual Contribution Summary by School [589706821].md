18.0.3.4. Annual Contribution Summary by School

This is a report of admitted SPE Member Applications.

  


Accessed from: Faith Builders SPE | Financial Reports | Annual Contribution Summary by School

  


Title: Annual Contribution Summary by School

  


The report will prompt for:

  * Tax Year (default to current tax year; required)
  * School (blank = all) (drop-list of active schools)



  


The columns should be:

  * Member
  * Address
  * City
  * State
  * Zip
  * County
  * Contribution Amount (total type = sum)



  


Grouped by: School

  


Sorted by: Member
