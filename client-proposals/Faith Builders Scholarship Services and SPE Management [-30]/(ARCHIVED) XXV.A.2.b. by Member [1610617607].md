25.1.2.2. by Member

  


Requirements

The SO needs a list of all admitted members and to which schools they've contributed for a specific tax year.

  


The report will prompt for:

  * Tax Year (default to current tax year; required)



  


The report will show:

  * Member
  * Address
  * City
  * State
  * Zip
  * County
  * School
  * School County
  * Contribution Amount



  


The report should be grouped by member.

  


The report should be sorted by school.
