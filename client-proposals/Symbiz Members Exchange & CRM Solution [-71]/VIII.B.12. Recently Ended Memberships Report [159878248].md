8.2.12. Recently Ended Memberships Report

  


Requirements

Overview: This is a report of Member-type Contacts that have had their memberships ended within a selected month.

  


Accessed from:

  * Admin | Membership Management | Recently Ended Memberships (bypasses the filter screen to open the report directly). 
  * The Recently Ended Memberships notification



  


Title: Recently Ended Memberships

  


Columns: 

  * Member Name (link to open record)
  * Paid Membership Start Date
  * Membership End Date



  


Grouped by: Month & Year ("<Month>, <Year>"; most recent at the top)

  


Sorted by: Membership End Date (most recent at the top)

  


Filters: 

  * Memberships Ending Since (date field; defaults to the 1st date of the month before the current month; blank = all; looks at the Membership End Date)



  


Buttons: 

  * N/A



  


Menu Visibility: Regional Reps and Super Admins (by virtue of the Admin Menu being restricted)

  


Other Notes:

  * N/A
