8.2.6. New Members Report

  


Requirements

Overview: This is a report of Members who have joined within the number of selected days. Specifically, this displays all Member-type Contacts with active memberships, who have their Paid Membership Start date on or after the selected "Memberships Starting Since" filter date. 

  


Accessed from: Admin | Membership Management | New Members (bypasses the filter screen to open the report directly)

  


Title: New Members Since <Membership Starting Since selection>

  


Columns: 

  * Member Name (link to record) 
  * Paid Membership Start 



  


Grouped by: Month & Year (<Month>, <Year>; most recent at the top)

  


Sorted by: Paid Membership Start (most recent at the top)

  


Filters: 

  * Memberships Starting Since (required; date field; defaults to the 1st date of the month before the current month; looks at the Paid Membership Start Date)



  


Buttons: 

  * N/A



  


Menu Visibility: Regional Reps and Super Admins (by virtue of the Admin Menu being restricted)

  


Other Notes:

  * N/A
