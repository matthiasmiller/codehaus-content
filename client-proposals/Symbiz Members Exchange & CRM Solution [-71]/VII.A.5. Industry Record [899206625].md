7.1.5. Industry Record

  


Requirements

Overview: Industries will be configured on the Industry records and viewed on a Industries report. These are used to categorize the industries that Members and Organizations fit into.

  


Accessed via: Configure Industries report

  


Sections and Fields: 

  * Industry section: 
    * Active (checkbox; defaults to filled)
    * Industry Name (required; plain text; validate against duplicates)



  


Data Access: See the Data Access Controls section of the proposal for details.

  


Special Considerations:

  * Copy Record: N/A
  * Delete Record: Prevent deletion if the record is referenced anywhere in the Solution: "This Industry cannot be deleted because it is referenced by another record in the database."
  * Merge Record: N/A



  


Other Notes:

  * These items are to be set up by Symbiz, with help from CCI/CH as needed.
  * Note that this record should have a name, date, and time stamp for Created and Last Modified, as well as a link to access Modification History (standard).
