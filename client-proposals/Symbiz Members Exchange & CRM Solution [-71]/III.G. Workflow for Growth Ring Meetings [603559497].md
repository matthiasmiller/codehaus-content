3.7. Workflow for Growth Ring Meetings

The following is a basic description of how to manage Growth Ring Meetings:

  


Creating the first Meeting for a Group:

  * On the Members Menu, click "My Growth Ring Meetings" in the My Growth Ring Group Details section.
  * On the resulting report, click "Schedule New Meeting" in the top right corner.
  * Fill in the information as needed.
    * Note that Growth Ring Group and Tentative Meeting Date are required fields to create a Tentative Meeting.
    * Note that you can see the Meeting Status in the right corner of the "Growth Ring Meeting Details" section header.
  * To schedule the Meeting, click "Schedule Meeting"
    * Note that Development Resource, Meeting Location, and Host are required fields.
    * If the Development Resource for the Meeting is a new Resource, click "Add New Resource" and follow the directions in the live system:Â [Workflow for Creating Development Resources](https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-11& "https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-11&").
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner.
    * Note that the "New Growth Ring Meeting Scheduled" notification will be automatically sent out to all Members of the Growth Ring Group as soon as the "Save" button is clicked.
    * Note that a Tentative Meeting for the next month will be automatically created on the first Save after the "Schedule Meeting" button is clicked.



  


Creating subsequent Meetings for a Group:

  * The next Tentative Meeting for a Group should be automatically created when its predecessor is Scheduled.
  * If this does not happen and the next Meeting needs to be created:
    * On the Members Menu, click "My Growth Ring Meetings" in the My Growth Ring Group Details section.
    * Find the desired Meeting and open the record by clicking the Meeting Date..
    * Follow the steps above to create the next Meeting.
    * Click "Save" in the top left corner.



  


Canceling a Meeting:

  * On the Members Menu, click "My Growth Ring Meetings" in the My Growth Ring Group Details section.
  * Find the desired Meeting and open the record by clicking the Meeting Date.
  * Click "Edit" in the top left corner.
  * Click "Cancel Meeting".
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner.



  


Completing a Meeting:

  * After a Meeting is over, it should be marked as "Complete" to avoid confusion.
  * On the Members Menu, click "My Growth Ring Meetings" in the My Growth Ring Group Details section.
  * Find the desired Meeting and open the record by clicking the Meeting Date.
  * Click "Edit" in the top left corner.
  * Click "Mark Complete".
    * Note that "Attended" in the Attendance section is a required field for all Members.
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner.
