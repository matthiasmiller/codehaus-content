8.11.1.1. Meet & Greet Registrations Report

Overview: This is a filtered version of the right pane of the Event Registrations Report that displays registrations for the Meet & Greet portion of an Annual Summit-type Event.

  


Accessed from:

  * "M & G Registrants (Internal)" link in the Pre-Summit Meet & Greet section of the Event record



  


The following filters are set to something other than their default:

  * "Event": set to the corresponding Annual Summit-type EventÂ 
  * "Meet & Greet Registration Only": checkbox checked



  


Other Notes:

  * See the spec for the Event Registrations Report for additional details (title format, visible columns & buttons, etc.)
