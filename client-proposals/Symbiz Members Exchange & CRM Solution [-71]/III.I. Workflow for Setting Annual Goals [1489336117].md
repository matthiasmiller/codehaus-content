3.9. Workflow for Setting Annual Goals

The following is a basic description of how to set Annual Goals:

  


To Access and Set Initial Annual Goals:

  * On the Members Menu, click on "My Symbiz Profile" in the My Details section.
  * Scroll down to the "Annual Goals" section (this is the last section of the page).
  * Click "New Year".
  * Fill in the information as needed.
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner of the page.



  


To Set Additional Annual Goals:

  * Access Annual Goals via the instructions above
  * Click "New Year".
  * Fill out information as needed.
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner of the page.
  * Note: Use the "<" and ">" buttons to see past or current Goals.
