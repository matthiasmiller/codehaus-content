3.5. Workflow for Members Without Internet Access

The following is a basic description of managing Members without internet access:

  


To create the Member's Contact record:

  * Have the Member send their info to the main office.
    * Note that they will need to send their Name and Address for their Membership to be created.
  * Complete all the steps as if creating a Contact record for an Online Member except for the Online Member droplist, following the instructions in the live system: [Workflow for Managing Memberships](https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-6&State=pa4IpoPlMj0& "https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-6&State=pa4IpoPlMj0&").
  * In the Membership Details section, set the "Online Member" drop list to "No".
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner of the page.
  * Continue following the various steps in the live system: [Workflow for Managing Memberships](https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-6&State=pa4IpoPlMj0& "https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-6&State=pa4IpoPlMj0&") to activate their Membership and add them to a Group.
  * Follow the steps in the live system: [Workflow for Editing Your Profile](https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-14&State=pa4IpoPlMj0& "https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-14&State=pa4IpoPlMj0&") to set their Personal Details, Industry, Core Values, Life Vision, and Annual Goals.



  


For setting Growth Ring Goals:

  * Have the Member send the info to the main office or to his Facilitator / designated upline user.
  * An Admin or assigned assistant should log in with their own profile and access the Member's page in the Members Exchange and apply the appropriate Goal information, following the steps in the live system: [Workflow for Managing Growth Ring Goals](https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-8&State=pa4IpoPlMj0& "https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-8&State=pa4IpoPlMj0&").



  


For entering Development Resource Reviews:

  * Have the Facilitator send the info to the main office or to his Facilitator / designated upline user.
  * An Admin or assigned assistant should log in with their own profile and access the Member's page in the Members Exchange and apply the appropriate Resource Review information, following the steps in the live system: [Workflow for Development Resource Reviews](https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-15&ShowTitleBar=true&State=pa4IpoPlMj0&# "https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-15&ShowTitleBar=true&State=pa4IpoPlMj0&#").



  


For scheduling Growth Ring Group Meetings (if the Facilitator is an offline member):

  * Have the Facilitator send the info to someone on the admin team / main office / data entry team.
  * The person doing the data entry should log in with their own profile and create / manage the Meeting record as needed. 



  


Changing an Offline Member to be an Online Member: 

  * See the corresponding section in the live system: [Workflow for Managing Memberships](https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-6&State=pa4IpoPlMj0& "https://symbiz.silverloom.io/Detail/View/2?RecordType=WikiPages&NumberID=-6&State=pa4IpoPlMj0&") for instructions.
