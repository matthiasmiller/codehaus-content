5.10.3.4. Notification: Print Symbuzz Extra (Monthly)

Name: Print Symbuzz Extra (Monthly)

Record Type: Growth Ring Meeting

Date: Monthly on the third Thursday

Trigger / Scheduled Task Details:

  * In-App Alert: Report alert that runs on the 3rd Thursday of every month at 7:00 am ET.
  * Email: Scheduled task that runs on the 3rd Thursday of every month at 7:00 am ET.



Scheduled Task: "Print Symbuzz Extra (Monthly)"

Format: Email

Send To: Any user with the "Receives Symbuzz Extra Monthly Reminder" checkbox checked

Subject / Message:

Subject: Time to Print Symbuzz Extra (Monthly)

Message: It's time to print and mail the monthly Symbuzz Extra. Please print it from the attached PDF.

  


You can use the attached Excel file to see who should be receiving a copy of Symbuzz Extra this month.

  


Attachments: 

  * Symbuzz Extra (Monthly) PDF Printout
  * Excel export of the Monthly Mailing List Report
