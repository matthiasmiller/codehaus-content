This is a report of General Meeting records and includes the option to schedule a new General Meeting.

  


This is accessed from Admin | General Meeting Management | View General Meetings (bypasses the filter screen to open the report directly). 

  


Title: General Meetings

  


Columns: 

  * Meeting ID (link to record) 
  * Industry-Specific (Yes / No)
  * Industry 
  * Meeting Date 
  * Virtual Meeting (Yes / No)
  * In-Person Meeting (Yes / No)
  * Location
  * Address 



  


Grouped by: the selection in the "Group By" filter

  * if Group By is "Month", display the section headings in the format of <Month>, <Year> and show the newest Month at the top



  


Sorted by: Meeting Date (greatest date at the top) 

  


Filters: 

  * Industry (drop list of all industries; default is blank = all)
  * Format (choice of Virtual, In-Person, All)
  * Meeting Date Start (date field; default is 6 months before the current date; blank = all) 
  * Meeting Date End (date field; default is blank = all)
  * Group By (drop list of Month, blank; default is Month)
  * Display Only Future Meetings (checkbox; default is cleared)



  


Buttons: 

  * Schedule New Meeting (opens a blank new General Meeting record)



  


Menu Visibility: 

  * Regional Reps and Super Admins (by virtue of the Admin Menu being restricted)



  


Other Notes: N/A
