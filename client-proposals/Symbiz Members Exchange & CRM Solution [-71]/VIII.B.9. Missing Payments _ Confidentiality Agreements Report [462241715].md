8.2.9. Missing Payments / Confidentiality Agreements Report

  


Requirements

Overview: This is a report of Member-type Contacts who are at least 45 days past their Application Approved date, have the "Active Membership" checkbox checked, and have one or more of the following checkboxes empty: Initial Payment Received, Confidentiality Agreement.

  


Accessed from: Admin | Membership Management | Missing Payments / Confidentiality Agreements (bypasses the filter screen to open the report directly). 

  


Title: Missing Payments / Confidentiality Agreements

  


Columns: 

  * Member Name (name of contact; link to open record)
  * Application Approved (displays the date)
  * Invoice Sent (displays the date)
  * Initial Payment Received (Yes / No; "No" displayed in red text)
  * Payment Received Date (displays the date) 
  * Confidentiality Agreement (Yes / No; "No" displayed in red text)
  * Agreement Received Date (displays the date)
  * Growth Ring Group(s) (if there are multiple, comma-separated with wrapping text)



  


Grouped by: N/A

  


Sorted by: Application Approved date (oldest at the top)

Menu Visibility: Regional Reps and Super Admins
