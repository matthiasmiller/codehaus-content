3.1. Workflow for Editing Your Profile

The following is a basic description of how to edit various items in your profile:

  


Accessing your profile:

  * On the Dashboard, click "View Profile"
  * On the Members menu, click "My Symbiz Profile"



  


Editing your Contact information:

  * If you wish to change anything in the "Contact" section of your profile, please contact a Super Admin.
  * For other contact changes, click "Edit" in the top left corner of the page.
  * Make the desired changes to the "Address", "Phone", and "Email" sections.
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner of the page.



  


Editing your Personal Details:

  * Click "Edit" in the top left corner of the page (if you have not done so already).
  * Make the desired changes.
  * Click "Save" in the top of left corner of the page.



  


Editing your Notification preferences:

  * Click "Edit" in the top left corner of the page (if you have not done so already).
    * Note that all Notifications are defaulted to give an alert both in the Members Exchange and via email/fax.
  * Check or uncheck the desired checkboxes for the desired notifications.
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner of the page.



  


Editing your Industry:

  * Click "Edit" in the top left corner of the page (if you have not done so already).
  * Select your Industry from the drop list.
    * Note that you can select multiple Industries at once
  * Note that you can click the "Industry" button to view a list of all your selected Industries and can add or remove industries from that child screen.
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner of the page.



  


Editing your Core Values:

  * Click "Edit" in the top left corner of the page (if you have not done so already).
  * Click "Insert" to add a row to the top of the list.
  * Enter the Core Values Word and the Core Value Description.
    * Note that the Core Value Description is required if there is a Core Values Word.
  * Click "Append" to add a row below the selected row.
  * Use the "^" and "v" buttons to sort your Core Values Words into the desired order.
  * Click "Delete" to delete the selected row.
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner of the page.



  


Editing your Life Vision:

  * Click "Edit" in the top left corner of the page (if you have not done so already).
  * Fill in the information as needed.
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner of the page.



  


Editing your Annual Goals:

  * Click "Edit" in the top left corner of the page (if you have not done so already).
  * Click "New Year".
  * Fill in the information as needed.
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner of the page.
