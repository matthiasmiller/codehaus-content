3.10. Workflow for Managing Development Resources

  


Requirements

The following is a basic overview of how to create and submit Development Resources:

  


Creating a new Development Resource: (all Members)

  * There are 2 locations where new Development Resources can be created:
    * On the Members Menu, click "New Resource" in the Development Resources section.
    * On the Development Resources report, click the "New Resource" button in the top right corner.
  * Fill in the information as needed and save the record.
    * Note that Title, Author / Creator, Media Type, Initial Category, and Overview are required fields.
    * Note that Super Admins can merge records if duplicates occur via misspellings, etc.
    * Note that Regional Reps and Super Admins will get a monthly alert to review all new Development Resources.
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner.
    * Note that there will be a validation message when saving the record if the Title + Author / Creator matches an existing record.



  


Editing an existing Development Resource: (all Members)

If you wish to edit a Development Resource that you created:

  * On the Members Menu, click "Development Resources" in the Development Resources section.
  * Find the Development Resource you wish to edit.
    * Note: Members can only edit Development Resources that they created.
    * Note: To quickly find the desired Resource, click on "Development Resources" in the top left corner of the report to open filtering options
  * Click on the Title of the desired Resource to open its record.
  * Click "Edit" in the top left corner to enter edit mode.
  * Make the desired changes.
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner.



  


Reviewing & Approving Development Resources: (Regional Reps & Super Admins only)

  * From the monthly alert, click the "View Resources" option. This opens a report of Resources needing review. 
  * Click on a Resource title in the first column. This opens the Resource record (use "Ctrl+Click" to open it in a new tab and leave the report tab open). 
  * Click the "Edit" button for the resource,.
  * Make any edits that are needed.
  * Select the appropriate status from the Status drop list at the top of the "Development Resource Overview" section.
  * Click "Save" in the top left corner.
