5.10.3.1. Notificaiton: Prepare Symbuzz Extra Posts

Name: Prepare Symbuzz Extra Posts

Record Type: Growth Ring Meeting, with Status = Scheduled or Complete

Date: The day after the most recent Growth Ring Meeting, at 7:00am ET

Trigger / Scheduled Task Details:

  * In-App Alert: Report alert that runs daily at 7:00 am ET.
  * Email: Scheduled task that runs daily at 7:00 am ET; email is skipped/not sent to Members who do not have any email address specified on their Contact record.



Scheduled Task: "Prepare Symbuzz Extra Posts"

Format: Email & In-app Notification (always do both, since the Secretary keeps changing)

Send To: Meeting Secretary for the Meeting

Subject / Message:

Subject:  Prepare Symbuzz Extra Posts

Message: Hello <Member Name>, 

  


If none of the post fields have an entry: 

Please prepare the Symbuzz Extra Posts from yesterday's Growth Ring Meeting.

  


If one or more of the post fields have an entry: Please review the Symbuzz Extra Posts from yesterday's Growth Ring Meeting. 

  


If the Secretary is an online member: 

You can do so here: <link to corresponding Growth Ring Meeting Record>

  


If the Secretary is an offline member and Format = Email/Fax: You can fill out the worksheet and submit it to be entered into the Members' Exchange. 

  


<includes the contents of all 3 post fields; display "No post" if empty> 

  


Attachments: 

  * (If the Secretary is an offline member and Format = Email/Fax) Symbuzz Extra Worksheet Printout PDF
