5.10.5.2. Notification: Review / Update Annual Goals (Facilitator)

Name: Review / Update Annual Goals (Facilitator)

Record Type: Contact

Date: Review Date for the Annual Goal on the Contact record

Trigger / Scheduled Task Details: Scheduled task that runs every day at 5:00 am ET; email is skipped/not sent if one of the following conditions are met: 

  * The Member is not part of a Growth Ring Group (and therefore does not have a Facilitator). 
  * The Facilitator does not have any email address specified on their Contact record. 



Scheduled Task: Review / Update Annual Goals (Facilitator) 

Format: Member's choice

Send To: Facilitator for the Member

Subject / Message:

Subject: Review / Update Annual Goals (Facilitator)

Message: It's time for the following Member to review and update their goals for the coming year: <Member Name> <link to the Contact record>.
