26.5.4. Industry Meetings

The Industry Meetings section will include the following fields:

  


  * Industry
  * Next Meeting Date
  * Meeting Link



  


Buttons: Schedule New (clicking this will open the Create New Industry Meeting page that includes the following fields):

  * Meeting Title (single-line plain text field)
  * Meeting Agenda (single-line plain text field)
  * Select Industry (droplist of all Industries)
  * Select Date (standard date field)
  * Select Time
  * Paste Link (single-line plain text field)
