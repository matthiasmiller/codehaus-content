5.10.2.3. Notification: Growth Ring Meeting Canceled

Name: Growth Ring Meeting Canceled

Record Type: Growth Ring Meeting

Date: Date that the Canceled checkbox is filled

Trigger / Scheduled Task Details: On save, if the Meeting Status is Canceled but was something else before the save.

Scheduled Task: N/A

Format: Member's choice

Send To: All Members of the Growth Ring Group who have the notification enabled

Subject / Message: 

Subject: Growth Ring Meeting Canceled

Message: Hello, <Member FirstName>, 

  


The Growth Ring Meeting that was scheduled for <m/d/yyyy> at <HH:MM AM/PM> <Time Zone> has been canceled. Please update your calendar, and work on your goals as needed. 

  


Link to canceled meeting record: <link to the corresponding Meeting record> 

  


Link to meeting goals: <link to Goals record for the corresponding Meeting>
