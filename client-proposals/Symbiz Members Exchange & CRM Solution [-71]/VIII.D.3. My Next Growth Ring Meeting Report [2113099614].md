8.4.3. My Next Growth Ring Meeting Report

  


Requirements

This is the All Growth Ring Meetings report, filtered to show only meetings for the Group(s) of the current user. The Group By filter is set to Month. The Display Only Future Meetings filter is also set.

  


Accessed from Members Menu | My Growth Ring Group Details | My Next Growth Ring Meeting (if the logged-in user is part of more than one Growth Ring Group)
