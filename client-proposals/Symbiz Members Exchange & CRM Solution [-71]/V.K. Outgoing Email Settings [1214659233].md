5.11. Outgoing Email Settings

  


Requirements

The Solution uses [notifications@symbiz.org](mailto:notifications@symbiz.org) as the default sending address for all outgoing emails from the Solution. 

  


Gmail SMTP settings

  * Outgoing Mail (SMTP) Server: smtp.gmail.com
  * Use Authentication: Yes
  * Use Secure Connection: Yes (TLS or SSL depending on your mail client/website SMTP plugin)
  * Username: [notifications@symbiz.org](mailto:notifications@symbiz.org)
  * Password: communicated separately
  * Gmail SMTP Port: 465 (SSL required) or 587 (TLS required)
