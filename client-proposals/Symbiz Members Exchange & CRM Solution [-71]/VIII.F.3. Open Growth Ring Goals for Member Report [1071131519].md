8.6.3. Open Growth Ring Goals for Member Report

  


Requirements

This is a simple report of Growth Ring Goals with a Status of In Progress or Overdue, for the selected Member. 

  


This is accessed from the My Current Goals menu option, if the logged-in user has more than one Goal record with a Status of In Progress or Overdue. 

  


Title: Open Growth Ring Goals for Member

  


Column(s): 

  * Growth Ring Group (displays the Description)
  * Meeting (link to open record) 
  * Status



  


Grouped by: N/A 

  


Sorted by: Growth Ring Group Description, then by Meeting (newest Meeting Date at the top)

  


Filter(s): 

  * Member (required; drop list of all Member-type Contacts with an active membership) 



  


Buttons: 

  * New Goal (always visible; opens a new Growth Ring Goal record; if the current user is an upline of the Contact selected in the report, the goal is defaulted to that filtered member; otherwise, it defaults to the current user)



  


Menu Visibility: Each user can see their own

  


Other Notes:

  * N/A
