5.10.2.1. Notification: New Growth Ring Meeting Scheduled

Name: New Growth Ring Meeting Scheduled

Record Type: Growth Ring Meeting

Date: Date that the Meeting Scheduled checkbox is filled

Trigger / Scheduled Task Details: On save, if the Meeting Status was not Scheduled (or the Meeting record did not exist) but now is Scheduled.

Format: Member's choice

Send To: All Members of the Growth Ring Group who have the notification enabled

Subject: New Growth Ring Meeting Scheduled

Message: Hello, <Member FirstName>, 

  


A new Growth Ring Meeting has been scheduled. Here is your info:

Date: <mm/dd/yyyy>

Time: <HH:MM AM/PM>

Time Zone: <Time Zone> 

Host: <Host>

Location: <Location>

Resource: <Development Resource>
