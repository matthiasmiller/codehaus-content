10.3.4. Prepay/Credits Report

This is a report on all customers, showing their Available Credit amounts.

  


Columns:

  * Customer (link to the Customer/Ads Page)
  * Available Credit
  * Collect Credit (link to the Collect Credit prompt page for that customer)
  * Publication (Full Admin only)



  


Prompts:

  * Customer (default to blank = all)
  * Publication (only for Full Admins; list of publications; default to blank = all)



  


Grouping/Sorting:

  * No grouping
  * Sort by Customer
