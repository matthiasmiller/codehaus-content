4.2. Glossary (3b)

Overview: The purpose of this glossary is to provide a description of key terms, roles, and other things referred to in this proposal. These are listed in alphabetical order below:

  


  * Device: A printer, email account, or internet hotspot. These can be sold to or leased to a customer. 
  * Device ID: The identifying number assigned to a Managed Device when it is deployed to a customer.
  * Device Contract: Details specific to a Managed Device, such as pricing, warranty, etc. (example: Kyocera brand printers have 3 color print count categories.
  * Manged Device: A Device that is managed by Think Ink. Think Ink provides supplies (such as ink or toner) and maintenace. In most cases, the Device is owned by Think Ink and leased to a customer (previously called an "Asset"). In some cases, the Device is owned by the customer and simply managed by Think Ink. 
  * Managed Service Agreement: The agreement between Think Ink and a customer for managed services of a group of Managed Devices. This includes details such as length of the agreement, special terms, and billing details. 
  * Billing Group: A group of Managed Devices for a customer in a single location and grouped together on an Managed Service Agreement. Note that one customer may have multiple Billing Groups assigned to them (previously called an "Asset Group"). 
  * MPS: An industry-specific generic term: "managed printer service".
