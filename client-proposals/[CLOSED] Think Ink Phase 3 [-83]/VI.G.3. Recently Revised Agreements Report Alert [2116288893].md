6.7.3. Recently Revised Agreements Report Alert

  


Requirements

Overview/Purpose: This alert occurs after an Agreement has been revised, to help ensure that it is reviewed and confirmed by the Agreement Sales Rep.

  


Details: 

  * Title (can include an expression): Recently Revised Agreements 
  * Displays within 20 minutes of: The Agreement Revision background process
  * Action: Opens the Recently Revised Agreements Report, filtered to the corresponding Agreement Sales Rep 
  * User(s) to notify: The user of the Agreement Sales Rep linked to the Agreement needing renewal 
  * Dismiss type: Auto-dismiss when all items are resolved
