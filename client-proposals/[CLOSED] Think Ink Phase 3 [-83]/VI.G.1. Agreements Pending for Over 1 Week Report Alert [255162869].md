6.7.1. Agreements Pending for Over 1 Week Report Alert

  


Requirements

Overview/Purpose: This report alert occurs for a specified user if an Agreement has been in Pending for at least 1 week.

  


Details: 

  * Title (can include an expression): Agreements Pending for Over 1 Week
  * Displays within 20 minutes of: The Agreements Pending Over 1 Week background process
  * Action: Opens the Agreements Pending Over 1 Week report
  * User(s) to notify: Users with the "Receive Agreements Pending for Over 1 Week Alert" checkbox checked on their Contact record.
  * Dismiss type: Auto-dismiss when all items are resolved
