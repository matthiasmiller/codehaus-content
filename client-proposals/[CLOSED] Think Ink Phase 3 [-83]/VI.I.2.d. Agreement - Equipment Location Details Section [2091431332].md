6.9.2.4. Agreement - Equipment Location Details Section

  * Group Location Details section:
    * Group Contact Name (optional; drop list of Individual Contacts that are linked to the Customer; default to blank; the contact selected here is the one who receives the emails for Page Count Request and Page Count Reminder) 
    * View/Edit Contact (visible if a Group Contact is selected; opens the selected Contact's record) 
    * Location Address (required; drop list of Shipping-type addresses for the selected Customer)
    * Street (auto-filled and read-only; based on the selected Address)
    * City (auto-filled and read-only; based on the selected Address)
    * State (auto-filled and read-only; based on the selected Address)
    * Zip (auto-filled and read-only; based on the selected Address)
    * Phone (auto-filled and read-only; if a Group Contact is selected, defaults to that Contact's primary Phone number, if no Group Contact is selected, defaults to the selected Customer's primary Phone number)
    * Fax (auto-filled and read-only; if a Group Contact is selected, defaults to that Contact's Fax number, if no Group Contact is selected, defaults to the selected Customer's Fax number; if multiple Fax numbers are listed in either case, this looks at the highest one on the list)
    * Email (auto-filled and read-only; if a Group Contact is selected, defaults to that Contact's primary Email address, if no Group Contact is selected, defaults to the selected Customer's primary Email address)
