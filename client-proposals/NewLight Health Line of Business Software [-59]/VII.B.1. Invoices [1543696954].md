7.2.1. Invoices

  


Requirements

The software would use the built-in Invoices module, linked to SKUs. It would track things like:

  * Customer
  * Date
  * Payment Date/Method
  * Terms; list of
    * Debit / Credit
    * ACH ("Check by Phone")
  * Embedded spreadsheet of:
    * Qty
    * SKU
    * Description
    * Price
    * Amount
  * Tax Code
  * Subtotal
  * Tax $
  * Total $
