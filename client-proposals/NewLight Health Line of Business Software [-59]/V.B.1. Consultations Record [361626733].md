5.2.1. Consultations Record

Fields:

  * Customer (list of customers)
  * Date (default to today's date)
  * Practitioner (list of practitioners; default to current user)
  * Protocol (list of protocols; copies recommended products into consultation)
  * Recommended Products; embedded spreadsheet of:
    * SKU (list of active SKUs)
    * Description (read-only from SKU)
    * Serving Size (default to SKU; allow override)
    * Notes (text)
  * Customer Notes
  * Internal Notes
  * Print Customer Notes (link)



  


Printing this would include a 1-page cover sheet, along with one or more sections from the related protocol.
