7.3.1. Invoices

This is the standard AppHosting All Invoices report, and is a report of all invoices in the Solution. This is a multi-pane report, with the left-hand pane providing the following views: 

  * Open Invoices
  * Closed Invoices 
  * All Invoices
  * Recent Invoices (show invoices that have been edited within the last ~30-60 days (entire last month plus current partial month to date)



  


This would be accessed from an "Invoices" menu option on the ___ menu (TBD). 

  


Title: Invoices

  


Columns: 

  * Invoice Number
  * Invoice Date
  * Total Amount
  * Contact
  * Organization
  * Location
  * Payment Date
  * Status



  


Grouped by: Status

  


Sorted by: Invoice Number (most recent at top) 

  


Filters: N/A

  


Buttons: 

  * Search (opens the AppHosting Invoice Search page)
  * New Invoice (opens a new blank invoice)
