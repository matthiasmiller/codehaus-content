1\. Overview

  


Requirements

NewLight provides a variety of health-related products to the Anabaptist community. They have three primary goals for this line of business software solution:

  * Documenting the wealth of knowledge that they have
  * Moving their catalog creation process in-house
  * Adding teleconsultations



  


This will be scoped in the following phases:

  


  * Phase 1 - Protocol Entry (including amendments), Recipes/Articles, Training Portal link, and SKU Imports from QuickBooks
  * Phase 2 - Customer Sync from QuickBooks 
  * Phase 3 - Practitioner Portal (include consultation records), Possible Appointment Links
  * Phase 4 - InDesign Sync
  * Phase 5 - Order Fulfillment (Sales Orders, Payment Processing, AvaTax Integration)



  


QuickBooks will remain the source of truth (i.e. system of record) until Phase 5.
