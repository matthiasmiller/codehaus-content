8.2.1. Truss Page

Truss Page (visible if the "Truss Order" checkbox is checked on the corresponding record):

  * Quote (checkbox; checked if the corresponding record is a Quote record)
  * Order (checkbox; checked if the corresponding record is a Order record)
  * Spacing
  * Res / Comm (checkbox; checked if Residential / Commercial checkbox is checked)
  * Ag (checkbox; checked if Agricultural checkbox is checked)
  * Loading GSL (displays the Loading GSL field from the Order Summary section of the record)
  * TCLL (displays the TCLL field from the Order Summary section of the record) 
  * TCDL (displays the TCDL field from the Order Summary section of the record) 
  * BCDL (displays the BCDL field from the Order Summary section of the record) 
  * Attic Loading (displays the Attic Loading record from the Order Summary section of the record)
  * Unlabeled table of the following (show 6 rows by default, even if there are blank rows):
    * QTY (displays the Qty column from the embedded spreadsheet in the Truss section of the record)
    * Span (displays the Span Ft and Span In columns from the embedded spreadsheet in the Truss section of the record)
    * Pitch - Depth (displays the Pitch column from the embedded spreadsheet in the Truss section of the record)

    * Right O.H. (displays the Right O.H. column from the embedded spreadsheet in the Truss section of the record)

    * Description (displays the Description column from the embedded spreadsheet in the Truss section of the record)

    * Total Price (displays the Price column from the embedded spreadsheet in the Truss section of the record)

    * Comments (displays the Deposit Comments from the Order record; if only "Lumber Order" or "Metal Order" is checked, displays on the corresponding page)
    * Delivery / Pickup
