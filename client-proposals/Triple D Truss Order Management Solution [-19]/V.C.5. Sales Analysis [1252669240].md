5.3.5. Sales Analysis

This is a report of all sales for the selected period.

  


This is accessed from Triple D Truss | Sales Items | Sales Analysis. 

  


Title: Sales Analysis

  


Columns: 

  * Month
  * 2022 (will always display the current year, if "Also show prior years" is checked will also show nine prior years for a total of ten years of history; If Period is set to Year to Date, YTD will be displayed above the year)



  


Grouped by: Group Order by filter

  


Filters: 

  * Period (drop-list of Month to date, Quarter to date, Year to date, Annual, Last Month, Last Quarter, All History)
  * Also show prior years (checkbox)
  * Group Order by (drop-list of Month, Quarter)
  * Reporting (drop-list of Revenue, Quantity)
  * Sales Categories (Blank = All) (multi-select list of Truss, Lumber, Metal)
