The customized PDF printouts for Quotes, Orders, and Packing Lists are generated on a basic Triple D Truss letterhead based on the following:

  


  * Logo
  * Quote / Order # (displays the corresponding Quote / Order number)
  * Salesperson
  * Bid Date
  * Order Date
  * Terms
  * Phone (displays Salesperson's phone number)
  * Sold To (displays the following: 
    * Customer Name
    * Customer Address
    * Customer Phone 
    * Customer Email 
    * Customer Fax)
  * Ship To (displays the following: 
    * Delivery Address
    * Job Phone
    * Travel Time)
