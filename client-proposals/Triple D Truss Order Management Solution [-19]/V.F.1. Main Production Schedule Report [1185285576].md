5.6.1. Main Production Schedule Report

  


Requirements

This is a report of non-Starlight Delivery records that have the "Scheduled" or the "Will Call" checkbox checked.

  


This is accessed from Triple D Truss | Deliveries | Main Production Schedule. 

  


Title: Main Production Schedule

  


Columns: 

  * Customer
  * Item
  * Board Feet (total type = sum)
  * Job
  * Made (Yes/No)



  


Grouped by: Week of Schedule Date

Sub-grouped by: Schedule Date replaced by "Will Call" if blank

  


Sorted by: Schedule Sequence + Delivery ID

  


Filters: 

  * Status (multi-select drop-list of In Progress, Made, Delivered; defaults to blank = all)
  * # of Historical Weeks (number field; defaults to 0)



  


Buttons: 

  * New Delivery
