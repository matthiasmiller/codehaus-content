5.5.2. Update Inventory Amounts

TODO_AP: Ben Reitz 11/14/2025: Standardize

  


This is a report of SKU lots.

  


This is accessed from Triple D Truss | Inventory | Update Inventory Amounts. 

  


Title: Update Inventory for #Location# as of #As of Date#

  


Columns: 

  * SKU
  * Description (unlabeled column)
  * Calculated Amount
  * Adjustment Amount (editable column)
  * Absolute Amount (editable column)
  * Unit (drop-list of UOM, editable column)
  * Notes (optional)  (editable column)



  


Grouped by: Active/Inactive

  


Sorted by: SKU

  


Filters: 

  * Location (drop-list of SKU lot locations)
  * As of Date



  


Buttons: 

  * Save Changes



  


Save Type (for editable reports): Save Button
