One of five orders are for a new customer, so it is important to streamline the order entry process to accommodate new and existing customers.

  


This report is accessed from Triple D Truss | Contacts | Quick Search.

  


This report has one filter: Customer Name / Phone, which is a drop-down list of Customer Name - Phone Number. This allows for searching by phone or name.

Entering a contact name and continuing will open the corresponding contact record.

Leaving the filter blank and continuing will open a new, blank contact record.
