8.2.3. Metal Page

Metal Page (visible if the "Metal Order" checkbox is checked on the corresponding record):

  * Quote (checkbox; checked if the corresponding record is a Quote record)
  * Order (checkbox; checked if the corresponding record is a Order record)
  * Spacing
  * Res / Comm (checkbox; checked if Residential / Commercial checkbox is checked)
  * Ag (checkbox; checked if Agricultural checkbox is checked)
  * Loading GSL (displays the Loading GSL field from the Order Summary section of the record)
  * TCLL (displays the TCLL field from the Order Summary section of the record) 
  * TCDL (displays the TCDL field from the Order Summary section of the record) 
  * BCDL (displays the BCDL field from the Order Summary section of the record) 
  * Attic Loading (displays the Attic Loading record from the Order Summary section of the record)
  * Unlabeled table of the following (show 6 rows by default, even if there are blank rows):
    * Quantity (displays the Qty column from the embedded spreadsheet in the Metal section of the record)
    * Description (displays "<Feet>',<Inches>", - <Description>"from the embedded spreadsheet in the Metal section of the record; if only Inches are displayed, auto-populate a "0" for Feet)
    * Unit Price (displays the Unit Price column from the embedded spreadsheet in the Metal section of the record)
    * Total Price (displays the Price column from the embedded spreadsheet in the Metal section of the record)
  * Unlabeled table of the following:
    * Job Information (displays the contents of the Job Information memo)
    * Sub-Total (displays the Total Metal Price field from the Total section of the record)
    * Delivery / Pickup Information (displays the contents of the Delivery / Pickup Note memo)
    * Comments
    * <Order Discount %> Discount (displays the Discount field from the Total section of the record)
    * Estimated Tax (displays the product of Sub-Total - Discount * Tax %)
    * Total (displays the sum of Sub-Total - Discount + Estimated Tax)
    * Delivery / Pickup
    * Date (displays the Delivery / Pickup Date)
    * Time (displays the Delivery / Pickup Time)
    * Lumber Delivery Pickup
    * Date (displays the Lumber Delivery / Pickup Date)
    * Metal Order (checkbox; checked if the "Metal Order" checkbox on the record is checked)
    * Moffet (checkbox; checked if the "Moffet" checkbox below the Metal Delivery label on the record is checked)
    * Customer Unload (checkbox; checked if the "Customer Unload" checkbox below the Metal Delivery label on the record is checked)
    * Sent w/ Truss (checkbox; checked if the "Sent with Truss" checkbox below the Metal Delivery label on the record is checked)
    * Straight Ship (checkbox; checked if the "Straight Ship" checkbox below the Metal Delivery label on the record is checked)
    * Straight Ship w/ Moffet (checkbox; checked if the "Straight Ship with Moffet" checkbox below the Metal Delivery label on the record is checked)
    * Roll Off (checkbox; checked if the "Roll Off" checkbox below the Metal Delivery label on the record is checked)
