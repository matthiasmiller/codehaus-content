5.3.1. Sales Report by Item

TODO_AP: Ben Reitz 11/14/2025: Standardize

  


This is a totals only report of non-canceled orders within the specified date range.

  


This is accessed from Triple D Truss | Sales Items | Sales Report by Item. 

  


Title: Sales Items #Start Date# through #End Date#

  


Columns: 

  * "" (no label; always contains Truss, Lumber, Metal)
  * Quantity (sum total)
  * Total Price (sum total)



  


Filters: 

  * Start Date
  * End Date
