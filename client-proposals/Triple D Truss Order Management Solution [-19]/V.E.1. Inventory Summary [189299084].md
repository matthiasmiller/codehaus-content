5.5.1. Inventory Summary

TODO_AP: Ben Reitz 11/14/2025: Standardize (probably just the Filters spec)

  


This is a report of SKU lots for Lumber category SKUs.

  


This is accessed from Triple D Truss | Inventory | Inventory Summary. 

  


Title: Inventory Summary

  


Columns: 

  * Description (link to record)
  * 30-Day Order Qty
  * UOM



  


Grouped by: Active/Inactive 

  


Sorted by: Description

  


Filters: 

  * Summary Type (Blank  = 30-Day Qty)  (drop-list of Total Inventory, Available Inventory, 30-Day Qty, 60-Day Qty, 90-Day Qty
