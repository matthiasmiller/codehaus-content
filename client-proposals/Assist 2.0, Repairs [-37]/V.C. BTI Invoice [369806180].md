5.3. BTI Invoice

Prompt for:

  * Start Date (default to start of prior month)
  * End Date (default to end of prior month)
  * Monthly Expenses
  * Company



  


Output

  * Invoice Number (using an autonumber, formatted as IN-00046)
  * Company (required; list of Companies)
  * District (automatic from Company)
  * Job Hours (calculated based on Repair Time)
  * Cost Per Hour
