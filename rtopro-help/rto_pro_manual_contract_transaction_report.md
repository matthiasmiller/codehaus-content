Revenue Reports Option "A"

The Contract Transaction Report will display contract transactions between any given dates. This includes listing new rentals, retail sales and cash advance loans as well as a list of agreements closed during the period. Any closed and re-opened contracts will affect the BOR count. A sample report is displayed below and some of the term definitions are below the report image.

![image39](image39.gif)

Rental Section:

Income: This is the rental income on all inventory on the agreement, including money paid on this agreement.

Profit: Contract amount - Cost

Turns: Contract amount divided by cost.

New: If the item was new at the time of rental.

BOR: If the item is a BOR counted item in RTO Pro. Each inventory item is set to BOR, yes or no at the time of receiving.

Retail Section:

Income: This is the rental income on all inventory on this sale.

Profit: Sale amount - Cost

Gross Margin: Inventory cost divided by Sale Amount.
