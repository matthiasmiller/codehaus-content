Sample Cash Advance Agreement or "Consumer Credit Disclosure and Promissory Note".

Cash Advances Loans or Deferred Presentment Loans as it is sometimes called is a feature of RTO Pro and is included at no additional charge. The sample is a Missouri agreement; this can be customized easily for any state. This contract was created using the Word Processor that is built into RTO Pro. Cash Advance Contracts can also be created using Microsoft Word.
