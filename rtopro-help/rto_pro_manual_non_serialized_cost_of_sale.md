Inventory Menu Option "8"

This is to print or display a non-serialized cost of sale report. This report will show non-serialized items that were sold between any given dates. It will show the number of items sold, the total cost and the quantity remaining in inventory.

To begin this report enter the sales dates, you may choose whether to display or print this report, and then push F5.
