Below are the definitions for the highlighted sections on the sample report below.

(1). The inventory categories as assigned at receiving.

(2). Number of inventory items in each category in this report.

(3). Total cost of said inventory.

(4). Revenue brought in for each inventory category.

(5). Revenue divided by total cost.
