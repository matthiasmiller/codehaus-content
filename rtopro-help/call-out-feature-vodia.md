When you choose Vodia as your service to use with the Call Out feature the screen below will display. Fill in the information and save.

IP or Domain of PBX: This is the address of you PBX phone system with Vodia

Users Extension: This is the user extension for your phone/user

Users Web Password: This is your user password for Vodia

Contact Vodia or your phone system provider to get the information that goes here if you do not know what any of this info is.
