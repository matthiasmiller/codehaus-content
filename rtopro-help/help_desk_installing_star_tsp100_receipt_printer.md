1\. Unpack the printer.

2\. Connect the USB Cable to the printer

3\. Open the cover and insert paper

4\. Pull out a small amount of paper and close the cover

5\. Connect the USB cable to the computer

6\. With the computer on Insert the CD_ROM and follow the install instructions

7\. Connect the power cord to the printer and plug the cord into the wall. Then turn the printer on.

To setup the printer to open a cash drawer go into the properties of the printer and set it like it shows below.

Change Pulse Width to 1100 milliseconds
